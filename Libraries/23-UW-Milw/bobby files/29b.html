Curriculum Collection

Welcome to the Curriculum Collection! We are located in the Golda Meir Library on the second floor, east wing.

The collection supports faculty and students in the School of Education and the School of Information Studies. It includes professional materials, children’s and young adult fiction and non-fiction, K-12 textbooks, reference materials, and a collection of historical materials.
