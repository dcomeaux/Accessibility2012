Welcome to UWM Distance Education

The UWM Libraries provides library and information support for the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee distance learning students and off-campus faculty and staff.  Currently enrolled students pursuing an education through distance learning methods are entitled to access to library resources and services equivalent to those provided for students in traditional campus settings.

The Libraries offer a variety of services to assist students with their coursework and research.
