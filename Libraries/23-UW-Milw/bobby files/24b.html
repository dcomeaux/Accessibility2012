Description of Services

  • Circulation - Information on borrowing library materials and renewing materials online, recall requests, remote storage retrieval, retrieving and holding items, photocopy services, and tracing missing items
  • Directory of Library Services or Facilities for Library Users with Disabilities
  • Distance Education Services including books by mail
  • Facilities Management handles carrel assignments, the conference center, lockers, and lost and found
  • Graduate Students, Guide to Library Resources
  • Interlibrary Loan - See also Options for Obtaining Library Materials, UW System Borrowing, and the Cooperative Access Program [CAP] (the Libraries of Marquette University, Medical College of Wisconsin, and the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee provide access to their collections and services for each other's academic communities)
  • Research and Instructional Support Services includes library instruction, government information, information desk, library workshops and research consultation
  • Reserve Services includes traditional Reserve and E-Reserve. To look for books, articles, videos, and other items your instructor has placed on reserve, use the Course Reserve catalog search.
