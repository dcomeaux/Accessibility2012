Research & Instructional Support

The UWM Libraries Research and Instructional Support Department (RIS) provides reference assistance, instructional services, and advice in the use of library materials and collections. The reference collection contains periodical & newspaper indexes and abstracts, encyclopedias, dictionaries, statistical data, directories, handbooks, bibliographies, and more. Stop by and see us on the first floor of the library's West Wing.

Ask a Librarian

Ask a Librarian Desk Service

The Ask a Librarian Desk is staffed by librarians and graduate assistants 81 hours/week when classes are in session [schedule of hours]. Hours change when classes are not in session. We can:

  • Suggest strategies & methodologies for your question or topic - saving you time!
  • Teach you how to use the online catalog & electronic databases
  • Help you connect to sources on the Internet
  • Suggest appropriate resources & collections
  • Refer you to other libraries, individuals, or associations

Our goal is to help you find the information you need while teaching you how to become a self-sufficient user. At times the Desk can become busy! Please be patient and we will assist you as soon as possible.

Telephone Reference Service

For telephone inquiries you may phone us at (414) 229-4659 or 1-877-893-8583 (toll free) during regularly scheduled desk hours. Because of the limitations of staff time, the RIS Department staff cannot provide extensive telephone reference service. Telephone reference questions which can be answered fairly quickly using the resources of the Reference Collection will be accepted.
