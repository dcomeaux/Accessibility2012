Library News

The Library participates in Simmons College's It Gets Better Project!

By Library on March 2, 2012 3:35 PM | View Comments | No TrackBacks The Library is happy to announce its support of the Simmons It Gets Better project!

[GROUP.JPG]See more pictures on our Facebook page!

In preparation for the April 12th unveiling of Simmons'involvement with It Gets Better, read the book that came out of the project's early days.

For more information on It Gets Better, an international organization which provides moral support to struggling LGBT youth, please visit www.itgetsbetter.org.

State Statistics Trial (with graphs and maps!!)

By Library on February 28, 2012 4:22 PM | View Comments | No TrackBacks Beatley Library recently acquired a month long trial of CQ Press State Stats (ending 28 March 2012). This web-based resource provides comprehensive data coverage of each state including health care, crime, education and more.

Try it out and let us know if you have any questions or feedback!

Displays on Library Guides!

By Library on February 21, 2012 3:50 PM | View Comments | No TrackBacks You've seen our displays on the first floor of the Library.  But did you know you can now browse items from recent Library displays online, too? Just head over to the Library Displays guide.

The Library displays highlight resources on a specific theme or event. These items are displayed on Level 1 of the Library near Diversions and New Books. Recent displays include: Black History Month, Downton Abbey, Yoga & Wellness, and Graphic Novels.

If you have any suggestions for displays please contact a librarian at reference@simmons.edu

DynaMed Trial

By Library on February 17, 2012 9:54 AM | View Comments | No TrackBacks The Library has a 30-day trial of DynaMed; an evidence-based clinical decision support resource.  This web-based resource is designed for health care professionals at the point of care.  In addition to the web-version (which is also mobile-friendly), there is also a mobile app for smartphones. 

[dynamed.png]

Try this resource out and let us know if you have questions or feedback!

Simmons College celebrates Black History Month

By Library on February 8, 2012 11:46 AM | View Comments | No TrackBacks

This year, the Library's Black History Month display is a collaboration with the Simmons Black Student Organization (BSO).  The BSO's theme the year is: "Redefinition: reinstate the purpose, pride and inclusivity that began us."

Come to the Library to see the specially selected items and to learn more about the events the BSO has planned. For more information about events going on around campus, please visit the online calendar.  Read more on the 300 The Fenway blog.

Streaming Movies and Music!

By Library on February 8, 2012 11:40 AM | View Comments | No TrackBacks The Beatley Library has set up a trial period for the Simmons community to test two new streaming media products. VAST: Academic Video Online and the Classical Music Library, both hosted by Alexander Street Press, contain thousands of full-length documentary films across a wide array of subject areas as well as a strong selection of classical records. During this month-long trial (ending March 24), the databases can be accessed from on or off campus by clicking through the links. (If you are off campus, you will be prompted for your Simmons College log-in information.) Please take a couple of minutes to explore what they have to offer and then fill out a short survey to let us know what you think.

New Look for ProQuest

By Library on February 1, 2012 10:46 AM | View Comments | No TrackBacks Notice anything different about ABI/INFORM Or Library & Information Science Abstracts? Or Health & Medical Complete? On January 24, the library switched to a new ProQuest interface, which includes a comprehensive, easy-to-navigate search environment, so more than 40 e-resources now have a new look. If you need help using any of these resources or have general questions or comments about ProQuest's new features, please ask-a-librarian!

We're looking for YOUR thoughts!

By Library on January 27, 2012 3:44 PM | View Comments | No TrackBacks If you are interested in participating in interviews about aspects of our online catalog, please contact Amy Deschenes at amy.deschenes@simmons.edu.  
Open to students and faculty!  $5 coffee gift cards will be given to those who complete the interviews.  Each interview will take about 15 minutes and will be scheduled on your time!

Looking for E-Resource Drop-Down Menus??

By Library on January 25, 2012 11:15 AM | View Comments | No TrackBacks

The E-Resource Subject drop-down menus now live on the Library Guides homepage here: http://simmons.libguides.com/content.php?pid=261542&sid=2158957#8368606 


You can also find them by clicking the "E-Resources by Subjects" link on the blue right-hand navigation menu on the Library's website.
