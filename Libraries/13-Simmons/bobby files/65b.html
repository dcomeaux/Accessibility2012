Request a Purchase

Request a Purchase

Simmons College Library collections are developed to support current curriculum of the College. Faculty are encouraged to contact the Library regarding required and supplementary materials needed to support their courses.

Requests for purchase of books or media from other members of the Simmons Community are welcome and will be evaluated for their appropriateness to the Library's collections. 

Please note: to request that an item be purchased for a course, please use the reserve requests form in the Library's course reserves system.
