A/V Room Reservations Request Form

Applicable Rooms

  • Library: Alumni Reading Room (ARR), MMB
  • Higgins Hall: HHC 015, HHC 016, Auditorium 018
    •

    This form is for one-time reservations for video/film screenings, meetings and classes.

    Academic Event reservation requests should be submitted directly to Multi-Media Services at mmsevent@pratt.edu.

    All requests are vetted by Multi-Media Services to determine appropriate room assignment based on capacity, scheduling and technology needs.

    Requests are handled on a first-come, first-served basis. MMS confirms all emailed requests. It may take more than two full business days to reply during periods of high activity.

    Room requests for other campus spaces including Technology Enhanced Classrooms (TECs) should be submitted to the Registrar.

    Audiovisual Room requests may only be made by current faculty, staff and students.

    Outside groups wishing to use Pratt spaces must contact the Office of the Vice President of Finance and Administration.
