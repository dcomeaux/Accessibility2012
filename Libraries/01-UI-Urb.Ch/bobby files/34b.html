Services for Affiliated Organizations

The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Library recognizes a number of affiliated organizations, including those holding allied status. Staff members and others associated with these affiliates may receive access to Library resources and services based on this affiliate status.

  • Allied Federal Government - Consists of governmental bodies that are directly associated with the University, support specific aspects of the University's mission and are located in the Urbana-Champaign community. Check here to see a current list of allied agencies.
  • Research Park at the University of Illinois - Consists of private companies that are located in the University of Illinois Research Park. Staff of Research Park agencies will be issued a courtesy card by the University Library. For more information, please visit Library Services for Research Park Organizations.
  • Osher Lifelong Learning - The Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign is part of a national network that recognizes learning has no age limits. Through a rich array of lifelong learning opportunities, members are inspired to take a fresh look at themselves, their world, and the possibilities that await them. For more information, please visit Library Services for OLLI members.

If you have any questions, please contact circlib@library.illinois.edu .
