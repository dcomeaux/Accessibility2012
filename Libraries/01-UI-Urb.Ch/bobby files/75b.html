Description

 The Undergraduate Library houses the primary audio-video collection within the University Library. The collection serves the instructional and curricular needs of the campus community at all levels, as well as the individual study, scholarship, cultural enrichment and recreational needs of students, faculty, and staff.

The media collection, covering all subject disciplines, consists of non-print materials in a variety of formats such as DVD, VHS, Video Game, Audiocassette and CD.  In-house viewing is provided for most formats.

Please note that the library is not responsible for any damage that may occur on equipment as a result of media borrowed from the library.

"Undergraduate Media Collection" Location:

 Items with this location are shelved, by call number, on the lower level of the Undergraduate Library.  These titles can be checked out for a One Week loan and can usually be renewed.

"Temporarily Shelved at Undergraduate Reserves" location :

 Items with this location are shelved behind the Undergraduate Library Circulation Desk.  These titles are restricted to in-building viewing only, unless being used by an instructor to teach a class.

For complete information on circulation policies for video recordings and video games, please see Media Circulation Policies.

Media items appear in My Account enabling  patrons to view what items are checked out, make renewals (if allowed) and view or cancel pending booking requests.

VHS players removed from classrooms

During the 2009-2010 fiscal year, CITES removed VHS players from their "smart" classrooms. Information Processing & Management (IPM) is aggressively pursuing DVD replacements for VHS titles that are requested for reserves.  The Library is not equipped, however, to handle converting one media format to another if not available commercially. Please use the Reserve Purchase Request Webform to request DVD replacements for VHS tapes you are accustomed to use for classroom showings and/or reserves.

Some tips for using VHS copies after CITES removes VHS players from classrooms:

  •  Your department may be able to arrange for a VHS player that you can bring in for the showing, you would need to check with them directly.
  •  The Undergrad Library has several individual/small group viewing booths with VHS players so your students can watch materials there.  Please make sure to put items on reserve, so that all of your students will have access to them.
  •  The Undergrad Library has a media viewing room equipped with a VCR which instructors can arrange to use.  The following website has additional information and an online form for booking the room for classroom use:    http://www.library.uiuc.edu/ugl/about/295.html
