Make a Gift

 

[CMS_Friends_Image_1] Your gift means a great deal to the Library and those who utilize its resources. Please use the form below to designate your gift to the departmental library of your choice or to the Library as a whole. You will then be directed to the University of Illinois Foundation's secure Online Giving Site for your personal and credit card information. If you prefer to mail your gift, please send a check in the amount of your gift made payable to "U of I Foundation/Library Annual Fund (32260)" to the Office of Library Advancement at the address below.  If you are interested in donating books or other materials, please see the Office of Collections Gifts Policy for information. Thank you for your support!

It's as Easy as...

 

1. Please select the area you wish to support from those listed below.

2. Once you make your selection and enter the amount of your gift, please click the "Continue" button below.

3. You will be redirected to the secure online giving site for the University of Illinois Foundation (UIF), where you will complete your personal and credit card information. This page also provides an opportunity for you to designate your Annual Fund gift to a collection, memorial fund, or other area of your choice (see "Comments and Other Instructions").

NOTE: For "Library Is Looking For" contributions, please select Annual Fund in the drop-down box above and include the title/name of the item you wish to fund in the "Comments or other instructions" section of the UIF Online Giving form.
