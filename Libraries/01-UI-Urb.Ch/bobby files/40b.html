Library Workshops - Spring 2012

What you need to know:

  • See the workshop schedule in calendar format. Register by clicking on the date you want to attend.
  • All classes will be held in the Main Library, Room 314, unless otherwise noted.
  • Workshops are limited to one hour for your convenience, however, feel free to stay after the session for specialized help.
  • Classes are limited to 20 attendees, so sign up early! (A wait list of 5 will be notified the day before if space becomes available.)
  • You can bring your own laptop or use one of our desktop computers for use during hands-on activities.
  • Looking for more workshops? Check out Academic Technology Services Events.

Workshops are co-sponsored by the Graduate College. Stay in the loop, read GradLinks.
