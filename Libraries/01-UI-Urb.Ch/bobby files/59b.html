Special, Manuscript, and Named Collections

There are several hundred named collections located in The Rare Book & Manuscript Library, ranging in size from single object to several hundred cubic feet.

Broad and deep, various and wonderful, the collections document a wide array of figures, subjects, formats, and time periods; Ranging from Incunabula to the the H.G. Wells papers, from illuminated manuscripts to the Spanish Civil War there is something here for every interest.
