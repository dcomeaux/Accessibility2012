Technology

  • Public Access Computers

  •  

    Public Computers Available in all libraries, these computers provide basic internet access and the tools to view library resources.

  • Library Access for Users with Disabilities - Details covering all aspects of library services for users with disabilities.

  • Loanable Technology at Undergraduate Library - Laptops, calculators, digital cameras, and other equipment available for checkout at at the Information Services Desk of the Undergraduate Library, located just inside the entrance to the upper level.

  •  

    Remote Access to Library Resources

     

  • UIUC VPN Service (CITES) - Use this service provided by CITES to connect to certain library resources. Make sure to download and use the library specific profile.

  • Citrix Client Download - Several library resources require the use of this Citrix client.

  •  

    Printing at the Library

     

  • Printing Help - Frequently asked questions, solutions to common problems and billing information for the LibPrint system.

  • Your Printing History - (report a problem) Have a problem with a print job printed at a LibPrint location, report a problem by logging into your printing history.

  • Printing from Your Computer - Software and instructions for printing from personal computers to conviently located printers throughout the library system.

  • LibPrint Printing Locations - Locations of laser printers that use the LibPrint system, including color laser printers.

  • Wireless Computing

     

  • UIUCnet Wireless FAQ (CITES) - This page answers frequently-asked questions that are specific to wireless technology in general and to UIUCnet Wireless in specific.

  • UIUCnet Wireless Access Locations (CITES) - Information on where wireless internet access is available throughout campus.

  • UIUCnet QuickConnect (CITES) - No software to download! New connection provided by CITES allows for authentication without the VPN client.

  • UI Public Wireless (CITES) - Visitors who have no affiliation with the University can connect to the Internet through this service while on campus.

  • Public Copying and Scanning

    Scanning is available at various University Libraries

    • In the Main Library at:
      • Main Stacks on 3 East, 5 East and 7 East
      • Reference, Research and Government Information Services, 200 Library
      • Applied Health Sciences Library, 146 Library
      • Business & Economics Library, 101 Library
      • Education & Social Science Library, 100 Library
      • Scholarly Commons,  328 Library
    • Undergraduate Library, 1402 West Gregory
    • Grainger Engineering Library, 1301 W. Springfield
    • Communications Library, 122 Gregory Hall
    • Mathematics Library, 216 Altgeld Hall
    • Music Library, 2146 Music Building
    • Art & Architecture Library, 208 Architecture

     

  • Photocopy Information - Information on locations and on using your I-card or copy card.

  • Scanning Available for Students at CITES Labs - The CITES locations at English Building and the Illini Union have scanners.

  • Scanning Available for Students Living in the Residence Halls - Located in the computer facilities rooms.

  •  

    Troubleshooting

     

  • Ask a Librarian - Ask a question and get an immediate response from reference staff in the Undergraduate Library or Reference Library during scheduled hours of operation.

  • System and Services Alerts - Lists library computer system and access problems and issues that may affect patron and/or staff computer access.

     

  • CITES Help Desk - Offers free advice as well as fee based consulting on personal computer and network account issues.
