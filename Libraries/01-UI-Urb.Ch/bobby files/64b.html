Contact Us

Contacting the University Library

Regardless of how you choose to contact us, we'll always make sure that your comments, suggestions, feedback, or questions make their way to the best possible people to answer your question. However, if you would please take a moment to select the most appropriate contact option for your current needs, we'll be able to get you your answer much more quickly. Thanks!

You have a reference or research question

You might need to figure out if the library has a particular item, get help finding materials on a topic, or anything else related to finding and using print and electronic information provided by the library. Use the Library's Ask A Librarian service.

You need to renew an item you have checked out

Please see the Central Circulation/Central Access Services My Account/Renewing page for information on how to renew materials (aas well as who can help you with renewal). Please be aware that Interlibrary Loan materials (obtained through WorldCat, I-Share, etc.) cannot be renewed.

You have a question, concern, feedback, or comment about the library that isn't a reference/research question or related to renewing materials

We look forward to your feedback and suggestions. Please Take a look at the options for contacting us on the University of Illinois Library - Write Us page.
