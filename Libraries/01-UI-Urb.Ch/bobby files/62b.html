    Specialized U of I Catalogs

     

  • UIC CAT (Library of the Health Sciences) - Go here to find medical books and journals located in the Health Sciences Library on our campus. Limit your search to LHS Urban.

  • Newspaper - Determine whether the library has a newspaper you want.

  • Slavic Newspaper - Find Slavic language newspapers owned by the University Library.

  • University Archives - Explore University Archives and Manuscript Collections.

  • Microform Collections - Microform collections acquired through 2001, some of which may not be in Illinet Online.

  • Fire / Emergency Services - The Illinois Fire Service Institute Library catalog concentrates on fire/emergency services and safety training information.

  • Classic Catalog

    --  Find items by title, journal title, author, subject heading, call number, or keywords.   Limiting to U of I branch libraries is possible.

  • Off Campus Catalogs

     

  • I-Share - Find items in the joint catalog shared by U of I and 75 other academic libraries in the state of Illinois.
