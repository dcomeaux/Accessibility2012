Recommend a Purchase

All members of the University of Alberta community are encouraged to suggest additions to the Libraries' collection. Requests will be evaluated by the appropriate subject librarian based on the Libraries' collection policies and the availability of funds. Materials that support the research and teaching programs of the University of Alberta will be given first priority.
