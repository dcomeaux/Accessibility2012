Location

The library is located in the Law Centre, at 111 Street and 89 avenue. It occupies the second and third floors. On the University of Alberta campus map (see East-Central map for more detail), the Law Centre is marked as # 91 and parking lot N / U is located on the north side of the library (access off 111 street at 90 avenue, just south of Saskatchewan Drive).

Underground parking is available at the Timms Centre (# 92 on map) and there are two meters on 111 street in front of the building. The Law Centre is connected by pedway to the Fine Arts Building, Timms Centre, HUB International, and the University LRT Station.
