Book Study Space

The following libraries offer online bookings of group study space - available to University of Alberta students, faculty and staff only.

  • Bibliothèque Saint-Jean (BSJ)

    book now 3 rooms | capacity: between 6 and 12 people | whiteboard | wireless access | Map

  • Cameron Library

    • Main Floor Group Rooms

      book now 7 rooms | capacity: between 6 and 8 people | computer workstation with Microsoft Office | access to printers | wireless access | whiteboard | large table and chairs

    • Cameron Study Hall Group Rooms

      book now 9 rooms | capacity: between 6 and 10 people | computer workstation with Microsoft Office | access to printers | wireless access | whiteboard | large table and chairs | available 24 hours, 7 days a week.

    • Knowledge Common Group Room

      book now 2 rooms | capacity: 6 people | computer workstation with Microsoft Office | access to printers | wireless access | laptop connection port | whiteboard | large table and chairs | large LCD screen | Map

  • Coutts (Education)

    book now  3 rooms (capacity): L-104A (10), L-222 (15), LB-13 (20) | tables & chairs | whiteboard | wireless access | Map

  • Rutherford (Humanities and Social Sciences)

    book now 2 rooms (2nd floor, Rutherford South) | capacity: 6 people & 8 people | Map

  • Scott (Health Sciences)

    book now 2 group viewing rooms (DVD & VCR in 2K3.04; VCR only in 2K3.06) | 4 rooms | capacity: 6 people | wireless access | Map (Level 1 - Group Study Rooms) | Map (Level 2 - Media Viewing Rooms)

    Priority for Media Viewing Rooms (2K3.04 & 2K3.06) is given to those who need to view DVDs/videos. You will be asked to leave if someone needs to use the room to view a DVD/video, and your group is not making use of the DVD/video equipment.

  • Weir (Law)

    • Law Sevice Desk Area

      book now 7 rooms | capacity: between 4 and 6 people | wireless access | Map

    • Law 2nd & 3rd floor Rooms

      book now 5 rooms | capacity: between 4 and 8 people | wireless access | 1 has DVD, 2 have DVD & VHS | Map
    • 2 rooms are available in the McLean Reading Room for Law students only (online booking not available)

Rules

  • A group (not each member of a group) may book one room for one block per day. Cameron Library rooms may be booked for a two hour block maximum, other libraries permit 2.5 hr bookings. Minimum group size is two people.
  • Please delete your booking if your plans change and you decide that you will be unable to attend. Note: A 10 minute grace period will be given for all room bookings and occupancy, after which time groups forfeit their booking to other groups waiting for a space to open. This applies to the entire length of your booking.
  • Rooms can be booked up to seven days in advance.
  • Groups that book using inappropriate language will have their bookings deleted. Code of Student Behaviour
  • Please remove all personal items and garbage when vacating rooms.
  • Rooms are not secure. You are responsible for all personal items left in your group room.
  • To ensure equitable access to Cameron Library Group Study Rooms during exam periods, rules may be revised. Access to Group Study Rooms is to be shared by all students. Students are reminded that block booking of rooms is not acceptable.
  • Please take care of the equipment and facilities so that we can continue to offer you these services.
