Cost

A Graphic services debit card [Graphics Services debit card]  costs $1.00 cash (non-refundable). and can be purchased at the service desk of any library. Cards are blank when purchased; credit may be added to the card at most photocopiers. A card charger unit is available in The D. B. Weldon Library, Taylor and Law libraries.

If you need change please ask at the service desk of your library location. A change machine is available in The D. B. Weldon Library.

* Archives and Research Collections Centre (ARCC):

  • Cash only payment made to staff at the service desk.
  • Print/photocopying is 10¢ per copy if done by patron, or 50¢ per copy if staff assisted
  • Scan costs are 50¢ per staff mediated scan.

** Map and Data Centre:

  • Cash payment only (debit cards not accepted)
  • Black and white copies cost 15¢ per copy; colour copies cost 75¢ per copy. Both letter (8.5" x 11") and ledger (11" x 17") size copies are available.

All Other Library Locations:

Photocopying:

  • photocopying is 9¢ per copy using a debit card
  • 15¢ per copy using cash
  • microform (microfiche or microfilm) photocopying is 10¢ per copy using a debit card.
Printing:
  • 9¢ per print using a debit card
Scanning:
  • print scanner in The D. B. Weldon Library is 10¢ per finalized scan
  • microform scan to a USB is FREE.

Print Stations are used to print from Library Sun Rays, public PCs, your laptop (wireless enabled) or mobile devices (e.g.: smart Phone, Ipad, etc.).

Mobile printing

To use mobile printing please visit the PrinterOn Mobile Apps page and follow the instructions for setting up your preferred mobile device (i.e. Android, Blackberry, iPhone or iPad). Once installed the PrinterOn app will locate and make available on-campus printers including those at Western Libraries.

Wireless Printing

Wireless Options:

  • Print Drivers
  • PrinterON
  • Print from a mobile Mac

 Reporting Problems

If you have any problems with the machines, the quality of copying or you need additional change, contact staff at the service desk of your library location.

Refunds

If you need a refund please contact staff at the service desk of your library location.

Alternatively, you can receive a replacement card at:

  • The Ivey Copy Centre located in the Ivey School of Business
  • The Graphic Services Copy Centre located in the Graphic Services Building off of Western Road
