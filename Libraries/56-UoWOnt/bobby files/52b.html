About the Project

To ensure that Mustang Football History, a mainstay of Western's best student experience since 1929, lives on for future generations, The "W" Club has forged a partnership with Western Libraries.

With your support, the Vintage Football Film Digitization Project will ensure that all game films depicting Mustang Football History will be preserved digitally and made accessible for scholarly use, to enrich student curricula, and for graduate and faculty research projects.

Help us achieve our most memorable win of all—protecting Western's rich football history for future generations of athletes, students and scholars. Let's get in there and make it happen.
