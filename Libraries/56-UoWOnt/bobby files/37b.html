About Summon

Summon is a simple and fast search engine that will discover content from Western Libraries' vast collection of books, journal articles and other formats for virtually any topic using a single search box. Summon is a great place to start your research for both discipline specific and interdisciplinary subjects.

Advanced researchers may continue to require and search stand-alone subject specific products including the Shared Library Catalogue or databases such as those offered by Proquest and EBSCO. These systems have specialized search options not available through Summon.

Some content owned or available through Western Libraries is not searchable through Summon owing to licensing arrangements between the content provider and the Summon service. A list of publishers included in Summon is available on the Serials Solutions website.

In addition to Summon there are many other search options available to you through Western Libraries:

Visit our Program Guides for a list of recommended databases to find journal articles as well as other recommended resources and contact information for your subject librarian.

Visit our Database page for a full list of databases available to search through Western Libraries.

Visit the Shared Library Catalogue to search library holdings for books and more.

You may also Contact Us for further assistance.
