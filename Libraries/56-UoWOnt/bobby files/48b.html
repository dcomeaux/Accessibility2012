Self Serve

Book a Study Room:
You can book a study room for yourself or a group in one of our libraries. Currently three libraries offer this service:
Click here to book a study room in the C.B. "Bud" Johnston Library.
Click here to book a study room in The Allyn & Betty Taylor Library.
Click here to book a study room in The D. B. Weldon library.

Course Reserve Request form :
Faculty/Instructors click here to access an online request form to have library items placed on a reserve course.

Interlibrary Loan (Racer) requests :
Currently registered UWO faculty, staff, students and Faculty/Librarian Emeritus/a click here to go to RACER and place an interlibrary loan request for item(s) Western Libraries does not own.

Self Checkout :
No need to wait in line, you can check out books at any of our self checkout machines.
These machines are currently available in the Allyn & Betty Taylor and The D. B. Weldon Libraries.

SMS Text Alerts:
You have the option to receive library alerts by text message on your cell phone. Click here to opt in.
