 Our exciting Digital Bookplates will provide you with a unique opportunity to say Thanks! or Way to go! or You will be missed! to that special someone in your life – a mentor, child, employee, friend, or colleague. Anyone who is special enough to honour, commemorate, or remember merits a digital bookplate.

A digital bookplate is an attractive digital crest, permanently linked to print and electronic book titles in Western Libraries online catalogue. Digital bookplates bear the name of the individual being honoured or remembered, as well as the name of the thoughtful donor who has chosen to pay tribute, in this meaningful way, while supporting Western Libraries.

Western Libraries bookplates recognize the support of endowments and gifts, which help to refine and enhance libraries’ collections, resources, services and individual and group study spaces for future generations of students, scholars and members of the community.

For more information, contact Sherry Foster, Alumni and Development Officer at 519-661-2111 x88078 or by email.
