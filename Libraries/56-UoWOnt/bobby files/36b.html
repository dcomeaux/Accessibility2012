Research and Publishing Support

Get help with your research

  • Contact your department/faculty librarian for help with literature searching, managing citations, setting up email alerts, and more
  • Use our Research Tutorials to answer your questions
  • Use the Dissertation Planner to break down the process into manageable steps

Learn more about our collectionS

  • View our Collections Policies
  • View our New Books by Library or Search for books purchased in the last month
  • Recommend a purchase (form)
  • Contact your department/faculty librarian to discuss or recommend library resources

  • Learn about scholarly publishing issues such as author rights and access barriers
