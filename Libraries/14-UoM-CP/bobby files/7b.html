Announcing the Popular Reading Collection at
McKeldin Library!

In response to your requests, the UM Libraries is proud to announce our newest service, the Popular Reading Collection!

This collection features "just for fun" books and CD audiobooks in a variety of fiction and non-fiction genres. You can search for titles in our Catalog, or browse the collection in Room 2109 of the Terrapin Learning Commons, McKeldin Library.

Books and audiobooks can be borrowed for three weeks at a time, and can be renewed one time for an additional three weeks.

Currently we have about 300 titles, and new items will arrive monthly. If you'd like to request a new release, email us at lib-pop@umd.edu.
