Reserve a Study/Meeting Room

Group Study Rooms at King Library

There are 36 rooms for groups of 2-12 people located throughout the King Library. These are dedicated to small group use for studying or working on projects.

  • Rooms in the Lower Level and on the 2nd, 3rd, 6th, 7th or 8th Floors must be reserved online:
    • Reserve a Study Room
    • Rules for Reserving Rooms
  • Small Group Project Room Use Policy

Public Meeting Rooms at King Library

[reserve a meeting room] Meeting Room Suites labeled 225 and 229 (combined 225/229) and Lecture Rooms labeled 255 and 257 (combined 255/257) on the 2nd Floor of King Library are available for meetings and public forums sponsored by organizations and groups affiliated with the Library, San José State University, CSU system or City of San José.

Note: If you wish to have a tour of the room(s) you are requesting, please make an appointment with the Meeting Room Coordinator: Candice McGee (408)808-2011 (all University and/or Community Organizations) or Marti Zarate (408)808-2163 (all City of San Jose) reservations."  Due to the high demand of the meeting rooms, room tours are only shown by appointment.

  • These rooms may be scheduled on a one-time use basis by community groups which meet the set requirements.
