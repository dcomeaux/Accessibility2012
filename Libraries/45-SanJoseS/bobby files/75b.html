Help! Contact the Library

Reference help in your major: contact a subject specialist librarian.

General reference help: contact the reference desk.

Help with your Library Account: contact Neil Ordinario or call 408-808-2370.

Student Computing Services: contact SCS for troubleshooting your remote access to SJSU Library.

Reset your Library PIN

Interlibrary Services: contact ILS for help with your ILLiad account and your requests.

Link+: borrow books from partner libraries in California by registering as a borrower at your local Link+ library.  Use your SJSU registration paperwork and a recent photo ID to become a visiting borrower. More information. 

Course Reserves: access electronic course reserve materials for your classes.

Tina Peterson: contact Distance Services Librarian for anything else.

    Welcome!

    Welcome SJSU Distance Students & Faculty!

    In this guide, you will find links to services and resources to help you complete your distance coursework successfully.

    • Under the Getting Started tab, you'll find a checklist of steps for connecting to online services and resources from the SJSU Library and from other libraries.
    • Under the Distance Services tab there is information on all the resources and services for SJSU students and faculty.
    • Under the Research & Writing Aids tab are resources on how to research and write papers.
    • Under the Quick Tutorials tab you'll find a series of 3-6 minute tutorials on using and customizing various library resources.

     Are you a distance student?
    Yes: You are enrolled in an SJSU satellite campus or online course or program, no matter how close to or far from the main campus you reside. No: You are enrolled only in on-campus courses, no matter how close to or far from the main campus you reside.
