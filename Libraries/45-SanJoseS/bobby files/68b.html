Wireless Access for the SJSU Community

 

  • An SJSUOne account is completely separate from a MySJSU /CMS /PeopleSoft account although both use your Student ID or Employee number, as it appears on the back of your Tower Card.
  • An SJSUOne account must be reactivated after any period during which a student is not taking classes, such as during summer break.
  • An SJSUOne account has a password that will be changed every 180 days.

SJSU Laptops and iPads

If you have an SJSUOne account but no laptop computer or iPad, you may borrow one from the Library's SJSU Student Computer Service  for use while in the King Library.

Requirements and Connection Instructions for library WiFi Use

  • Computer Requirements
    To use the library (SJSU) wireless network, your computer device must have:
    • A working WiFi (wireless) network interface device (802.11a, b, g, or n) - usually built into your computer
    • DHCP capabilities installed, properly configured, and active
    • Wireless connection capability turned on
    • A browser (e.g. Firefox, Internet Explorer, Chrome, Safari)
  • Connection Instructions
    Many wireless computers will automatically detect, and connect to, the nearest wireless network. See the user's guide for your device for instructions on making or changing a wireless network connection. If your computer presents a choice of wireless networks, select sjsu_campus.

    You may find information about configuring wireless cards on SJSU's Configuring Wireless Cards page.

Problems

  • For problems with wireless access, call the library SCS (Student Computer Services) office (408-209-2470).
  • For problems with your SJSUOne account, contact SCS Student Computer Services office, or the University Helpdesk (Clark Hall) for help.
