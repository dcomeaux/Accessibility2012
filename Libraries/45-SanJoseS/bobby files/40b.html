How Do I ...?

[Reserve a Study/Meeting Room] Reserve a Study/Meeting Room Make an online reservation for a study or meeting room at the King Library [Borrow Materials] Borrow Materials Learn how to check out, request, and renew library materials [Check out a laptop] Check out a laptop SJSU Student Computing Service (SCS) provides eligible SJSU borrowers with laptop equipment and software for use inside the King Library. [Reserve a Computer] Reserve a Computer Make an online reservation for a computer at the King Library. * Need a SJLibrary Card to make a reservation [Access the Library From Off Campus] Access the Library From Off Campus How-to information on remote access to the library's resources. [Contact a Subject Specialist] Contact a Subject Specialist Need help with your research? Consult an SJSU librarian by phone, email, IM, or face to face. [Get a Library Card] Get a Library Card Information and application form for SJPL and SJSU library users. [Pay a Fine] Pay a Fine Information on paying fines for lost or overdue items. [Reset a Pin] Reset a Pin You can Reset Your PIN automatically. An email will be sent to the email address in your library record, including a link that will allow you to select a new PIN. [Link+/Interlibrary Loan & Other Resources] Link+/Interlibrary Loan & Other Resources Use Link+ or Interlibrary Services (ILLiad) to borrow books or request copies of journal articles that the library doesn't own or that are checked out.
  • Interlibrary Services Interlibrary Services lets library users borrow hard-copy items (books, microfilm, etc.) and articles (paper, electronic) from libraries all over the world. (Watch these short videos to find out more about creating an Interlibrary Services account and placing a request.)
  • Link+ Link+ is a borrowing program among many California academic and public libraries. Link+ lets you request items from another library that are not available at the King Library or San José Public Library (SJPL) Branches.
  • California Libraries Catalog The California Libraries Catalog provides the ability to look up books and other materials in California libraries cataloged in the Online Computer Library Center (OCLC). It also provides access to WorldCat, the catalog of all OCLC libraries worldwide. Free to all cardholders. * SJLibrary Card Required To Access Outside the Library
  • Find in a Library With WorldCat Search Search over 2 million WorldCat records to find libraries anywhere that have your title.
  • Other Local & California Library Systems Check out what other local libraries have to offer. Select a library's website to find a phone number to call for assistance or check its Online Catalog yourself.
