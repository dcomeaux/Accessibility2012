Services

[SJSU Affordable Learning $olutions] SJSU Affordable Learning $olutions Affordable Learning Solutions / Open Educational Resources is a program that enables faculty to choose and provide quality educational content that is more affordable for their students.The program is cited in references as either Affordable Learning Solutions or Open Educational Resources. [Reserve a Study/Meeting Room] Reserve a Study/Meeting Room Make an online reservation for a study or meeting room at the King Library [Wireless Access] Wireless Access Information for San Jose State University students, faculty and staff on accessing the campus's wireless network. [Faculty Services] Faculty Services Information about library services available to faculty such as Course Reserves or Library Instuction Sessions. [Course Reserves] Course Reserves Find material set aside by instructors for exclusive use by their students. [Instructional Services] Instructional Services The campus Library & Information Literacy Instruction Program collaborates with faculty, instructional staff, and campus administrators to pursue its primary mission: To make information literacy integral to the SJSU experience through curriculum-focused instruction, provision of instructional materials, and faculty-librarian cooperation. [Printers, Photcopiers, Microform Readers, Scanners] Printers, Photcopiers, Microform Readers, Scanners Find prices and locations of the library's printing, copying and scanning services. [Borrow Materials] Borrow Materials Learn how to check out, request, and renew library materials. [Student Computing Services] Student Computing Services Provide students with a laptop that can only be used within the library. This help desk also provides help with getting onto the databases and wireless. [Disability Services] Disability Services The Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Library welcomes disabled visitors and provides many services adapted to the needs of people with disabilities. [Distance Learning] Distance Learning SJSU Library services for students and faculty in distance courses. including reference and connection help and materials such as articles, books, reports and electronic reserves.
