[Online Tutorials] Online Tutorials Brief online guides to help you do research and to use library resources [RefWorks] RefWorks A tool that can help you manage your book and article citations. It also automates the creation of bibliographies and in-text citations in the style format of your choice for your research papers. To learn how to use Refworks, watch RefWorks Tutorials. (For the RefWorks group code, go to Get Library Passwords. For Q&A, go to RefWorks troubleshooting.) Watch this two-minute video to find out more about the Assignment Calculator.
