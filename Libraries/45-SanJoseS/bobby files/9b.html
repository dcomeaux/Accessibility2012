Ask A Librarian

We are here to help. Do not hesitate to ask any question about the library or your research needs. There are many ways to reach us:

[visit us in person]

Visit Us in Person

During our open hours, one of our friendly librarians always is available to answer your research questions at the 2nd floor Service Desk.

 

[Call Us]

Call Us

For immediate assistance with any question about the library call (408) 808-2100 during our open hours. A librarian will help you over the phone or refer you to an expert for additional assistance.

[Email Us]

Email Us

We guarantee to respond to your email questions within 48 hours. Use our Email form to send us a question at any time. Before submitting your question, please refer to our Reference FAQs to see if your question has been answered there.

[schedule an appointment]

Meet with a Subject Specialist

For expert assistance on a particular research project, contact one of the library's subject specialists or visit one of our subject guides. We have librarians who specialize in every department on campus, and they are happy to meet with you for personalized research assistance.

[live homework help]

Live Homework Help

Students in grades K-12 through the first 2 years of college and adult learners can connect for online tutoring in a variety of subjects. More details ...

See Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Library for library hours, holiday closures, and other schedule changes.
