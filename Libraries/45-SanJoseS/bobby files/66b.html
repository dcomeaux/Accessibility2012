Laptop Equipment Loans

Equipment & Software

SJSU Student Computing Services (SCS) provides eligible SJSU borrowers (i.e., SJSU students, faculty and staff) with laptop and iPad equipment, including pre-installed software, for use inside the King Library ONLY.

The following equipment is available for loan from SCS (depending upon availability - first come, first served, no reservatons):

  • HP 6735s laptop [2009 model] (62 each)
    • Comes with power adapter and mouse
  • HP Compaq 6515b laptop [2008 model] (32 each)
    • Comes with power adapter and mouse

The following software is pre-installed on each device:

  • HP Compaq 6735s laptops come with the following programs installed:
    • Microsoft Windows XP Professional with SP3
    • Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access
    • Firefox, Internet Explorer and Google Chrome
    • SPSS (for statistics classes)
    • VLC (for playing most video formats)
  • HP Compaq 6515b laptops come with the following programs installed:
    • Microsoft Windows XP Professional with SP3
    • Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access
    • Firefox, Internet Explorer and Google Chrome
    • SPSS (for statistics classes)
    • VLC (for playing most video formats)
    • Chinese IME's for typing and inputting text in various Chinese language formats.
  • Integrated and peripheral connections on these systems:
    • Laptops:
      • USB ports
      • DVD/CD drive
      • ‚Öú‚Äù Stereo Headphone jack
      • Built in microphone
      • Built-in wireless (WiFi) network adapter
      • Wired network adapter
      • VGA video out
      • HP 6735s laptops(only):
        • Built-in front-facing camera

Laptop accessories (available upon request):

  • USB Floppy disk drives (these are legacy devices and provide extremely limited storage - 1.4 Mb - which may not even hold a single Word Document).
  • Headphones with boom mic
    • These are NOT ear-buds. SCS does not loan ear-buds due to health risks. Users are encouraged to use their own ear-buds, which are interchangeable between personal listening devices (like smart phones, MP3 players, etc.). There are a limited number (5 each) of these headphones.
