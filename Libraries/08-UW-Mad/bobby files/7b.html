Visitor Information

Welcome to UW-Madison Libraries! The University’s Libraries rank as one of the leading research collections in North America. Campus libraries include Memorial Library, the principal research library for humanities and social sciences; the undergraduate research facility, College Library, and more than 40 special purpose libraries.

The campus library collections include:

  • 7.3 million printed volumes
  • 55,000 serial titles
  • 6.2 million microforms
  • 160 linear feet of manuscript collections
  • over 7 million items in other formats, including government documents, maps, musical scores, audiovisual materials, and more

For a complete listing of UW-Madison Libraries, their hours and location, holdings, and other pertinent information, visit Campus Libraries.
