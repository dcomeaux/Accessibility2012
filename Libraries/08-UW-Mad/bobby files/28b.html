Ask a Librarian

The following table provides a brief explanation of the listed Web pages and their content.

Link Use For
My MadCat Web-based library account management system that allows patrons to view items checked out, renew items, check library requests, and check for library fines and fees.
Borrow/Renew/Return ... See Circulation FAQs for more information.
Article & Book Delivery ...For more information see: Library Express (an interlibrary loan and document delivery service for UW-Madison faculty, staff, students and Annual Fee or Courtesy Card holders), and WorldCat Catalog. This page also provides access to your Library Express Login.
Course Reserves & Materials Information for faculty, instructors and students about library course reserve materials. See: student, or faculty information.
Citation Managers A Web-based tool developed to create and improve organization of in-text citations and bibliographies. See also: RefWorks Workshops, or the Quick Start guide.
Request a Purchase Recommend a purchase by UW-Madison campus libraries.
Services for Faculty Collected links to a variety of library services of interest to faculty.
Services for Distance Students Information about distance library services for UW-Madison students, faculty, and staff that reside outside of Dane County.
Debit Cards Information about Debit Cards, including links to various Debit Card forms.
Equipment/Labs Details about locations and equipment of campus computer lab facilities.
Fee-based Services Fee-based services for corporate and private libraries, businesses and individuals.
