Ask a Librarian

The following table provides a brief explanation of the listed Web pages and their content.

Link Use For
Research Tips and Tricks Materials to facilitate use of library resources for research or academic work.
Workshops, Classes, and Tours A comprehensive and current listing of all UW-Madison campus libraries workshops, classes, and tours designed to facilitate knowledge and use of campus libraries and their resources and tools.
Instructional Services Libraries offer a variety of instruction support services.
Subject Librarians Listing of librarians by UW-Madison departments.
Copyright & Fair Use Information about UW-Madison Libraries copyright policies.
Off-Campus Online Access Provides help using the UW-Madison campus libraries' Remote Access.
Library Instruction Overview Description of instruction resources for learning about libraries and their resources.
CLUE Tutorial An online tutorial to introduce UW-Madison campus libraries and college-level research tools. Requires Macromedia Flash player v.6 or higher.
Alerts and RSS Feeds Information about personal accounts for saved searches, free email alert services, and RSS feeds available through the UW libraries Web pages.
