Research Tools

Make the best use of your research time

Start and finish your academic research with a Dalhousie library. We have all the tools and support you need to plan, work on or tune up any project you have in mind.

Make a work plan

Assignment Calculator: Our calculator will create a timeline for researching and writing your paper to help you meet that important deadline.

Start your research

Subject Guides:  Librarians select key resources in each academic field and assemble them into guides to help you with your research.

  • LibX:The Dalhousie LibX toolbar is a browser extension that allows you to search Worldcat Local, Dalhousie subscribed electronic journals and many citation and full text databases directly from your browser's toolbar. The toolbar is a convenient way to take the library and its resources with you, wherever you happen to be online.

  • Citation linker: Access an article when you already have a citation.
Article search
  • Databases: Find a database by name, discipline or keyword
  • Prowler Not sure where to search? Prowler searches multiple databases at once.

Manage your information

RefWorks: RefWorks is a web-based citation manager that allows you to compile, edit and format bibliographies.

  • Login to RefWorks

Quick answers

Online reference sources for quick answers to basic questions.
  • Oxford Digital Reference Shelf: A range of reference materials in the humanities, social sciences, sciences, languages and law.
  • Oxford English Dictionary: The digital reference shelf does not include the OED. Dalhousie users only 
  • Credo Reference: A large collection of key reference texts.
