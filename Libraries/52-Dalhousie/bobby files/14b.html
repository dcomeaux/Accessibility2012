W. K. Kellogg Health Sciences Library

The W. K. Kellogg Health Sciences Library holds Dalhousie’s biomedical collections. The Kellogg provides services to the Faculties of Dentistry, Health Professions, Medicine and serves health professionals throughout the Maritime provinces.
