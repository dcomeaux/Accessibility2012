Locations & Services

What's at each library and the services we offer

Dalhousie University’s libraries provide state of the art environments to explore the ever-expanding fields of human knowledge. Librarians offer a wide range of services to help you use the largest university library system in the Maritimes.

Locations

 

W.K. Kellogg Health Sciences Library

  • Focus: Dentistry, Medicine and Health Professions
  • Health databases: Pub Med, CINAHL, evidence-based resources & more.
  • Mediated fee-based searches

Killam Memorial Library

  • Focus: Humanities, Sciences, Social Sciences and Management
  • University Archives & Special Collections
  • Writing Centre
  • GIS Centre and Maps Collection
  • Floorplans

Sexton Design & Technology Library

  • Focus: Engineering, Architecture, Planning and related disciplines
  • Tour the Sexton Library
  • Floorplans 

Sir James Dunn Law Library

  • Focus: Law, International Law, Marine and Environment Law and Criminology
  • Legal Databases: Lexis, Westlaw and more
  • Floorplans
