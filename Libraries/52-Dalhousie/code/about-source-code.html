About

About

World-class education. Legendary Maritime charm.

Located in the heart of Halifax, Nova Scotia, Dalhousie is a leading, research-intensive Canadian university offering more than 180 degree programs in 11 diverse faculties. Explore all that Dalhousie has to offer!

Dalhousie has three campuses in Halifax—all within walking distance to the downtown core. Often referred to as Canada's "best-kept secret," Halifax boasts a youthful spirit, rich history and scenic waterfront.

Dalhousie is Atlantic Canada’s leading research university, attracting over $132 million in research grants and awards each year. Our researchers are nationally and internationally recognized for their work, whether it’s groundbreaking, thought-provoking or just plain cool.

From the classroom to the court to the quad, Dalhousie students are discovering the unexpected every day. Hear what students are saying about Dalhousie.

We welcome you to visit us and take a campus tour. Check out our beautiful campus, meet with professors and discover what life at Dalhousie is all about. Bring along your friends, your parents or your guardian.
