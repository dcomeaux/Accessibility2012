   Introducing RefWorks & Online Help

           RefWorks is a web-based bibliography and database manager that allows you to create your own

     personal database by importing references from text files or online databases and other various

     sources. You can use these references in writing papers and automatically format the paper and the

     bibliography in seconds.

            Refworks is available to all current NCCU students, faculty, and staff.

            To get started using RefWorks, there are a few things you'll want to focus on first:

  • Creating Your Account (or Database)
  • Getting References Into Your Database
  • Organizing Your References
  • Managing Your References
  • Finding References In Your Database
  • Creating A Bibliography And Formatting A Paper

    

    Accessing RefWorks

             If you are a new patron to NCCU RefWorks, please Sign up for an Individual Account. When

     creating your Login Name, please do not use the following characters:

                     * Asterisk

                     < >[ ] Brackets

                      = Equal sign

                     + Plus sign

                      " Quotes

                      \ / Slashes

                      , . : ; Comma, Period, Colon, Semicolon

             NCCU students, faculty, and staff may log into RefWorks from any campus location. For remote

     (off-campus) access, you need to either via Web VPN or Enter Your Group Code to access NCCU's

     subscription and your personal database.
