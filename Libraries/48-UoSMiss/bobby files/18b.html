How do I login to my account in the library's online catalog?

A SOAR username and password are needed to access to your personal library account in the library catalog. Your personal account shows what materials you have checked out and allows you to renew your loans online. The login is not required to search the catalog, nor is it necessary to access electronic resources from off-campus.

Anyone allowed borrowing privileges (current students, faculty, staff, or members of the Alumni Association) is also able to login to a library catalog account.

To use login, go to the library catalog and click the My Account link located along the top of the screen.
