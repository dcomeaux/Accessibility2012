Services at University Libraries

Access Services

Circulation - information about service eligibility, loan periods, renewals, overdue fines, lost or damaged items, and related information

Course Reserves - faculty may place electronic or physical items on reserve to make them easily available to all students in a course

Interlibrary Loan - use the ILLiad system to request delivery of articles and loans of books and other materials from other libraries

Laptop Rental - rent a laptop/notebook computer overnight

Mississippi University Library Borrowing Program - request a MUL borrowing card to gain on-site borrowing privileges at any of seven other university library systems in Mississippi

Reciprocal Faculty Borrowing Program - request a Reciprocal Faculty borrowing card to gain on-site borrowing privileges at any of over 150 academic institutions

Remote or Off-Campus Access - access the Libraries' electronic resources from anywhere


Reference Services

Ask-a-Librarian - receive reference assistance via e-mail

Consultations - schedule a research consultation with a librarian

Library Instruction - faculty may schedule a library instruction session for a class

RefWorks - web-based research management system


Technology Services

iTech Help Desk - the iTech Help Desk is located in Cook Library Second Floor South

Bibliographic and Collection Development Services

Bibliographic Services Department - the acquisitions, cataloging, and serials units are where much of the "behind-the-scenes" work occurs

Purchase Recommendation - recommend purchase of a book, video, journal, or other item

Order Inquiry - ask about a book, journal, or other resource listed in the library catalog as on order or in process

Catalog Error Report - report a problem with the library catalog

eOrder System - faculty liaisons, login to the eOrder system to recommend purchases using departmental allocations


Other Services

Research Carrels - rent a research carrel for a semester

Room Reservations - meeting rooms are available in Cook Library, Gulf Coast Library, or McCain Library

Library Forms - link to all University Libraries forms from one convenient location


Speaking and Writing Centers

As part of the University's Quality Enhancement Plan, the Southern Miss Speaking Center and Writing Center were created to to improve oral and written communication skills across the university. The centers are located within the libraries on both the Hattiesburg and Gulf Coast campuses.

  • Speaking Center, Hattiesburg campus
  • Writing Center, Hattiesburg campus
  • Gulf Coast Speaking and Writing Center
