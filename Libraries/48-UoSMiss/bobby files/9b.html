Books24x7 Now Available through University Libraries

University Libraries is pleased to announce the addition of a new electronic books collection--Books24x7. The Libraries' subscription to Books 24x7 includes four e-book collections: BusinessPro, FinancePro, ITPro, and Well-BeingEssentials. Faculty and students in the fields of accounting, business, computer science, finance, human resources management, information technology, international business, management, marketing, and tourism management will find relevant e-books in Books24x7. Areas of broader professional interest include Internet and Web development and project management. All students, faculty, and staff may find books of personal interest in the Well-BeingEssentials collection, which includes topics such as fitness, health, nutrition, parenting, personal finance, stress management, and weight loss. Books24x7 has something for everyone!

With the addition of Books24x7 to our collections the libraries now offer over 200,000 electronic books to our students across our campuses. To find e-books, visit the Libraries' e-resources page or search the library catalog. E-book collections available from the Libraries include Books24x7, eBooks on EBSCOhost, ebrary Academic Complete, and ScienceDirect Book Collection. For more information, contact Cook Library Reference Services at 601.266.4249 or Ask-A-Librarian.
