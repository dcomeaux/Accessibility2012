Support the University Libraries

The University Libraries welcomes private financial support. Such support for the Libraries is tax deductable and benefits all Southern Miss students and researchers in their academic pursuits.

There are many ways to support the Libraries such as:

Named Endowments

One of the most popular gifts that academic libraries enjoy is an endowment. Since endowments provide a flow of income each year, librarians can count on the money being available for the purchase of new materials, new technology or upgrades to study space. Endowments can be given in one lump sum, completed in installments, or made as part of a planned gift.

Planned Gifts

Planned Giving is the process of making a deferred charitable gift. Common types of planned gifts include wills and bequests, life insurance policy, or charitable gift annuities.

Gifts in Kind

The Libraries welcome gifts of original materials, books, personal libraries, reports, maps, and other items that fall within the scope of our collecting activities.

If you would like to support the University Libraries please contact the Office of the Dean of University Libraries at 601.266.4242.
