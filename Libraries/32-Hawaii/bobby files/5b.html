Library Use Calculator: What's your library worth to you?

How much would you pay out-of-pocket for your library services if you had to purchase them directly? To find out:

  • Enter the number of times you used each service in the last month (use the TAB key to get to the next row).
  • The estimated cost of each service will be automatically calculated.
  • The total value of your monthly use of the library will show at the bottom of the table.
