  • TROVE has replaced the Register of Australian Archives & Manuscripts which was a successor to the Guide to Collections of Manuscripts Relating to Australia published by the National Library of Australia from 1965 to 1995. Trove provides integrated access to approximately 90 million items from a range of the Australian National Library's collaborative services including over 1000 libraries around Australia, other cultural and educational organisations and international digital collections of relevance.
