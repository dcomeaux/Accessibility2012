Scholarly Communication @ UH Manoa

Currently there is an explosion of information about the changing model for the exchange of scholarly information. Because of the widespread adoption of digital technologies and an ever-increasing academic network, scholars are exploring new ways to exchange information for research, teaching, and learning.

The University of Hawaii at Manoa (UHM) Library aims to facilitate discussions about current trends in Scholarly Communication with our faculty and about how best to approach the sharing of scholarly work. The UHM Library would like to encourage the University community's active participation in creating a system that best serves the needs-and maximizes the impact-of scholarship and research at UHM.
