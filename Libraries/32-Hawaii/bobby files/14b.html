Hamilton Library Study Rooms & Presentation Practice Rooms


Group study rooms are available for use by University of Hawaii at Manoa students, faculty, and staff on a first-come, first-served basis. Presentation Practice Rooms are available for UHM students only. See Presentation Practice Rooms for information about reserving a presentation room. The rooms are available whenever the building is open. Presentation practice rooms include a computer with Microsoft Office software, internet, and a projector.

These rooms are checked out to individuals with a valid UH Manoa ID card in good standing.


Group study rooms 203-208 are located behind the elevators on the second floor of the main building.

Group study rooms A251-A254 are located on the second floor of the Hamilton Addition.

Presentation practice rooms A152a and A152b are located on the first floor of the Hamilon Addition, adjacent to the Science & Technology reference desk.

Use of group study and presentation practice rooms is subject to the Guidelines below.


Guidelines

Check out a study room key at the Circulation Desk in the main lobby with your valid UH Manoa ID card. Presentation practice rooms will be opened for you.

Return the key as soon as you finish using the room. Failure to return the key can result in a lost key fee of $100.

  • Use of the rooms is on a first come, first served basis. Reservations are not accepted. The rooms are intended for group use.
  • Keep the room door closed to avoid disturbing others.
  • Erase the chalkboard before leaving the room.
  • As a courtesy to others, do not occupy a room longer than needed to complete your work.

  • Do not leave your personal belongings unattended in the room.
  • Do not smoke, eat, or drink in the room. Water in covered bottles is permitted.
  • Do not move furniture into or out of the room.
  • Do not cover the windows of the room.
  • Library security personnel monitor rooms regularly.
  • Abuse of the rooms may result in loss of the room privilege.
