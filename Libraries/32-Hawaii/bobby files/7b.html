Computers in the Library

Hamilton and Sinclair Libraries have public PC workstations with internet access on the most heavily used floors. In addition the campus Information Technology Service (ITS) operates computer labs physically located within both libraries. Some library computers are equipped with Microsoft Office software and all but a few workstations require login with a UH username and password or Visitor Internet Account.

Accessibility Resources

Hamilton and Sinclair Libraries have at least one public PC workstations equipped with ADA-compliant software for screen magnification (Zoomtext) and screen reading (JAWS). The ITS labs also have ADA workstations.  Patrons are advised to bring their own headphones. More Information

Computer Labs

Information Technology Services (ITS) maintains a computer lab on the first floor of Hamilton Library and first floor of Sinclair Library. The computers have Microsoft Office software and Internet access. Printing is available with the Library's Copy/Print Card. Login with a UH username and password is required.  More Information

Campus Network Connections

University of Hawaii students, faculty, and staff who bring their own laptop equipped with a wireless card may access the campus network from many areas within the libraries. For more Information see laptop docking and wireless.

Public Computer Use Policy

Users must comply with the campus University of Hawaii Policy on Acceptable Use of University Computer and Information Resources.  Library computers and related equipment are to be used only to access information resources for educational, instructional, and research needs related to the mission and activities of the University of Hawaii. The use of Library computer equipment for other purposes is prohibited.  More Information

Scanners

Flatbed scanners are available in the CLIC Computer Labs on the first floor of Hamilton Library and the Science and Technology area on the first floor of the Hamilton Library Addition. A scanner is available in the Hawaiian and Pacific Collection on the 5th floor of Hamilton Library, which may ONLY be used for scanning materials from the Hawaiian/Pacific and Charlot collections.

Printing

Printing is available from all library computers. Printouts must be paid for by using the Library's Copy/Print Card. Color printing is available in the Science and Technology area on the first floor of the Hamilton Library Addition. The Copy/Print Card may be purchased at self-service machines in the lobbies of Hamilton and Sinclair Libraries.

Wireless Printing

Wireless printing using the PrinterOn campus printing software is available at selected printers in Hamilton Library and Sinclair Library. Patrons with UH user accounts or VIA access may print from any web browser on their laptop computer for retrieval from Goprint paystations. The address of the Hamilton Library wireless printing system is: http://hl-printeron.library.manoa.hawaii.edu/. The address of the Sinclair wireless printing system is: http://sl-printeron-avc.sinclair.hawaii.edu/

After logging in, you can choose any one of three printer locations in Hamilton:

  • BHSD (Business, Humanities, & Social Sciences area, first floor),
  • HAWNPAC (Hawaiian & Pacific Collections Reading Room, fifth floor), or
  • SCITECH (Science & Technology reference area (first floor, Hamilton Addition).

The SCITECH location is a color printer, which is $0.75 per page; other locations are $0.11 per page. When you select a printer location the print request (JOBNAME) on the GOPRINT paystation window is your UH login name. Print requests disappear after two hours if not executed or deleted.

Sinclair Library has two wireless printing locations.

  • RBR (Study Lounge [former Reserve Book Room], first floor)
  • AVC (outside of the Wong Audiovisual Center, third floor)
