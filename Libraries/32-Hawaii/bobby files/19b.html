Ask Service Questions by Telephone & Email

Include specific and pertinent key words, date limits and/or complete author names, as appropriate, in your email message. Be sure that your browser is set up with YOUR email address before you start your message. If other people use the same machine, the return email address may be set for someone else and you will not receive a reply. To provide the most complete assistance, include your email address, full name, department/affiliation, and a phone number in the BODY of the message.
