Loretto Memorial Library

300 Howard Avenue
Staten Island, NY 10301

The Loretto Memorial Library on the Staten Island Campus has more than 140,000 volumes of books and more than 1,000 magazine and journal subscriptions. It hosts 45 computers, all Internet ready, for public use. It commits to academic teaching and learning by providing information resources, library instruction, reference service, and other supporting services to a community of 2,700 students, faculty, staff, and administrators.
