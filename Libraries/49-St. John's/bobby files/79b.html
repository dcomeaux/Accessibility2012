Reserve Services

The St. John’s University Libraries provide Course Reserves in order to support the instructional requirements for specific courses

eReserve >
Search here [How do I?] for materials on electronic reserve. You will need to get the password for your course from your professor.

Books on Reserve >
Search here for books on reserve in the library.

To check for videos on reserve, contact the Queens Library Media Center at 718-990-6396.

Contact Us:

Queens Library Reserve Department: 718-990-6069
Staten Island Library Reserves: Circulation Desk (718-390-4359)
Manhattan Library Reserves: Davis Library Online Catalog (212-227-5136)
