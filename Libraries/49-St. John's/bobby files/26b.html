St John's Prep Records

St. John's Prep (St. John's Preparatory School), 1870-1972, offered preparatory schooling for boys who wished to enroll in St. John's College. Its teachers and administrators consisted of Vincentians who also taught in the College and/or the Seminary.

The original St. John's Prep ceased operations in 1972, but a merger was effected in 1973 whereby Materchristi High School for Girls in Long Island City (Queens) commenced coeducational operations at the latter's facility with the former's name, St. John's Prep.

The St. John's University Archives holds selected originals and copies of Prep materials, including some yearbooks, and will include Prep photographs in a digital preservation project. However, much of the original materials are scheduled to be transferred into the custody of the new St. John's Prep: 21-21 Crescent, Long Island City, NY 11105, (718) 721-7200.
