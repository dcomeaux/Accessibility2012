Records Management

Overview
Records Management for the University was implemented in 1997 as a component of an all-University archives and records program. Records management is under the supervision of the University Archivist and the Dean of the Libraries and is thus a component of the academic structure of the University. The position of Records Manager, an administrative position, was created in 1997 and implemented by former University Archivist Dr. Marilyn Pettit, Dean Emeritus of the Libraries Sr. Marie Melton, R.S.M., former Acting Dean of the Libraries (1997-98) Julie Cunningham and former University Provost Dr. Tony H. Bonaparte.
