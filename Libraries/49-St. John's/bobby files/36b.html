Media Center

The Media Center, located on the Queens campus, is a service area of the University Libraries and provides instructional materials University-wide; equipment, in-Center.
 
The Collection
Materials
The collection includes more than 3,000 DVD and video titles, supportive of curriculum and research. All patrons with current St. John’s ID may borrow materials. All holdings are available for use on both the Queens and Staten Island campuses.

In addition to the titles on video and DVD, over 100 educational videos are available to the St. John's community online from FMG Film on Demand.

Any faculty may make suggestions for purchase by sending requests to the Media Center. Materials are generally first obtained for preview to determine content, technical quality and worth. Faculty are alerted by e-mail, when their requested titles arrive.

Facilities
Located within the Media Center are individualized stations equipped with   DVD and VCR players.

Searching
All Media Center materials are included in the Libraries' Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC). Search in Advanced Search as you would any of the Libraries’ holdings and use "limit type to" Video. DVDs will also show up under this limit.

Reserves
Faculty may put their own or the Center’s DVDs/videos on reserve in the Media Center for their students to view. (Please note this location is not the Reserve area used for books, journals, etc.)
