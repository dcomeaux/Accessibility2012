Electronic Reserves

Electronic reserves can be accessed online. Click on the link below and search by the name of your professor or course. Note: you will need to enter a password, which is provided by your professor.

Print Reserves

Print Reserves can be located through Reserves Search in the catalog. Simply enter the name of the Professor or course name. 

When you have the call number for the item you want, access print reserves by visiting the service desk at your campus library.
