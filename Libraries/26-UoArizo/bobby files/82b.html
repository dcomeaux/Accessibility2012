Services

Document Delivery

We offer free electronic delivery of articles & book chapters to all UA-affiliated customers.

 

We also offer delivery of streaming video and audio to instructors for course use.

All current students, faculty, and staff automatically have a Document Delivery Account. Login to your account (NetID) to submit and view requests.
