Trial Database: Anthropology Online


Dates: January 26, 2012 - March 17, 2012

Contact: Mary Feeney

Description:

The University Libraries are pleased to offer trial access to Anthropology Online, a database which brings together a wide range of written ethnographies, field notes, seminal texts, memoirs, and contemporary studies, covering human behavior. At completion, the database will include more than 100,000 pages of material, including previously unpublished material from major archives.

The trial of Anthropology Online ends March 17, 2012. For more information, please visit the trial site here.
