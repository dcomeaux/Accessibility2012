Feedback Form

Send us feedback

Use the form below to give us suggestions, and to provide constructive feedback on library products, services or staff.

Note: We reserve the right to not publish and/or respond to messages that are outside of this scope, or which are obscene, derogatory, or otherwise inappropriate.

For time-sensitive issues or immediate assistance with any problems encountered while in our libraries, please call or talk to a staff member at a service desk in the Libraries so we can help resolve the issue or problem as soon as possible.

For research questions, use Ask a Librarian services (chat, e-mail, phone, or in-person). Please use plain text only in your message. If you need to send us a URL, just enter it as http://example.com/
