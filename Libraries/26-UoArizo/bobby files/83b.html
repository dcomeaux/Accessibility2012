Services

Equipment Lending

Need a calculator for your big exam? Is your laptop in the repair shop? Need to print from your laptop and you forgot your USB drive? We can help.

Laptops and netbooks

The UA Libraries have laptops, netbooks, tablet PCs, and MacBooks available for circulation to University of Arizona students. Netbooks circulate for 72 hours, while the rest circulate for 4 hours.

[laptop][tablet pc]       [black samsung netbook]  [MacBook]

iPads

The UA Main Library has iPads available for 72 hour circulation to University of Arizona students.

[iPad]

Wireless Keyboards

The UA Main Library has iPad wireless computer keyboards available for 72 hour circulation to University of Arizona students.

[wireless computer keyboard]

 

Projectors

The UA Libraries have projectors available for check out to University of Arizona students. The pocket projectors circulate for 72 hours, while the full-size projector/laptop combos circulate for 4 hours.

[projector]   [samsung pico projector]         [3m pico projector]

Calculators

The UA Libraries have scientific/graphing and basic calculators available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students.

[scientific calculator]     [basic calculator]

USB flash drives

The UA Libraries have USB flash drives available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff, along with community users.

[usb flash drive]

 

Headphones

The UA Libraries have headphones available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff.

[headphones]

Card Readers

The UA Libraries have micro card readers available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty and staff, along with community users.

[microcard reader]

35 mm Negative / Slide Adapters

The UA Main Library has 35 mm Negative / Slide Adapters, to use with our scanners, available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty and staff, along with community users.

[35mm negative or slide adapters]

 

Extension Cords and Power Strips

The UA Libraries have extension cords and power strips available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff.

[extension cord]     [surge protector]

 

Metronomes

The UA Fine Arts Library has metronomes available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff.

[metronome]

Magnifying Glass

The UA Main Library has magnifying glasses available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff, along with community users.

Cassette Player/Recorder

The UA Main Library has cassette player/recorders available for 4 hour circulation to University of Arizona students, faculty, and staff.
