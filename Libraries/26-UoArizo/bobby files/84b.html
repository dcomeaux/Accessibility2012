Print, Copy, Scan at Express Document Center

 

[EDC Logo]

The Express Document Center (EDC) offers a wide variety of fee-based printing and copying services in the Library. Most services provided by the Express Document Center are available to anyone using the Library.

Library Late Fees and Billing Charges

Library late fees and billing charges can be paid online through your Library Account or at the Express Document Center at the Main Library. Fees paid will be reflected immediately on your account.  For more information regarding late fees and billing charges, please visit the Lost, Damaged, and Late Library Materials page.
