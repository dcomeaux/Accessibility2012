NANOnetBASE [Info on NANOnetBASE]
Provides access to nanoscience and nanotechnology references. Includes information on topics such as biomedical engineering, communications, electronics, lasers and optical engineering, mechanical engineering, and synthesis and self-assembly.

National Bureau of Economic Research [Info on National Bureau of Economic Research]
Provides empirical, economic research in four areas: developing new statistical measurements, estimating quantitative models of economic behavior, assessing the effects of public policies on the U.S. economy, and projecting the effects of alternative policy proposals. Support of National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) is provided by OU Department of Economics. Access all content except the technical working papers. Full text available.

National Center for Biotechnology Information [Info on National Center for Biotechnology Information]
Provides access to PubMed, human genome resources, entrez databases, and nucleotide databases as well as tools for data mining and sequence analysis.
Neurosciences Abstracts [Info on Neurosciences Abstracts]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to information on all aspects of vertebrate and invertebrate neuroscience.
New York Amsterdam News Historical Newspaper [Info on New York Amsterdam News Historical Newspaper]
( 01/01/1922 - 12/31/1993 )
Archives the African-American historical newspaper the New York Amsterdam News, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
New York Times Historical Newspaper [Info on New York Times Historical Newspaper]
( 01/01/1851 - 12/31/2007 )
Archives the historical New York Times, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
Newspaper Source Plus [Info on Newspaper Source Plus]
Indexes and abstracts over 170 newspapers and related sources, including 140 regional U.S. newspapers, eighteen international newspapers, six newswires, nine newspaper columns, The Christian Science Monitor, and The Los Angeles Times. Some full text available.
Newspaper Source Plus (Mobile) [Info on Newspaper Source Plus (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Newspaper Source Plus.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, Dell, Axiom, iPhone, and Palm 750.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]

Nineteenth Century Masterfile (NCM) [Info on Nineteenth Century Masterfile (NCM)]
( 01/01/1786 - 12/31/1922 )
19th Century Masterfile provides access to Index to Legal Periodical Literature, 1786-1922; Index to Periodicals, 1890-1906; Mechanics, Physics, Mathematics Cumulative Index to a Selected List of Periodicals (1896-1899); Poole's Index to Periodical Literature, 1802-1906; The Psychological Index (1894-1905) and Richardson’s Index to Periodical Articles in Religion (1890-1899).
Nineteenth Century U.K. Periodicals [Info on Nineteenth Century U.K. Periodicals]
Provides access to periodicals published in the United Kingdom between 1800 to 1900. Full text available.
Nineteenth Century U.S. Newspapers [Info on Nineteenth Century U.S. Newspapers ]
Access hundreds of 19th century U.S. newspapers. Full text available.
Norfolk Journal and Guide [Info on Norfolk Journal and Guide]
Archives the African-American historical newspaper Norfolk Journal and Guide (1921-2003), including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues
North American Indian Thought and Culture [Info on North American Indian Thought and Culture]
Provides access to biographical information on indigenous peoples from all areas of North America. Presents the life stories of American Indians and Canadian First Peoples in their own words and through the words of others.

North American Women's Letters and Diaries [Info on North American Women's Letters and Diaries]
( 01/01/1675 - 1/1/1950 )
Contains diaries, letters and biographies of women from the Colonial Period through 1950. Full text available.

Nucleic Acids Abstracts [Info on Nucleic Acids Abstracts]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to information on cloning and sequencing strategies as well as gene regulatory sequences and proteins, RNA processing and transport, RNA editing, DNA biosynthesis, transcription factors, and oncoproteins.
Nanotechnologies for the Life Sciences [Info on Nanotechnologies for the Life Sciences]
Native American Literatures: An Encyclopedia of Works, Characters, Authors, and Themes [Info on Native American Literatures: An Encyclopedia of Works, Characters, Authors, and Themes]
Features four kinds of articles: on individual authors, individual works, important characters in works, and on terms of historical importance.
Native Americans: An Encyclopedia of History, Culture, and Peoples [Info on Native Americans: An Encyclopedia of History, Culture, and Peoples]
New Dictionary of the History of Ideas [Info on New Dictionary of the History of Ideas]
A six-volume survey of the history of Western thought and culture, presented through 700 alphabetically arranged entries. Each entry explores the origin, cultural interpretations, and historical themes of such subjects as beauty, love, feminism, diversity, and social capital, among many others.
New Encyclopedia of Africa [Info on New Encyclopedia of Africa]
Addresses the entire history of African cultures from the pharaohs and the ancient civilizations of the south through the colonial era to the emergence of 53 independent countries, some of them newly emergent in world commerce and others deep in conflict. Covers issues facing the continent such as global development, the AIDS crisis, and international terrorism.
New Pauly [Info on New Pauly]
Nineteenth-Century Literature Criticism [Info on Nineteenth-Century Literature Criticism]
North American Theatre Online [Info on North American Theatre Online]
North American Theatre Online is a comprehensive reference work covering all aspects of the Canadian and American theatre, including plays, people, theatres, productions, and production companies. The work includes images, playbills, postcards, scrapbooks and other resources. Provides access to reference works only.
