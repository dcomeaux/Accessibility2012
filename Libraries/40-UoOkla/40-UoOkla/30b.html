SEARCH TIPS

For additional resources and detailed search instructions, see the HES subject guide.

Begin searching by selecting a database. MEDLINE and SportDiscus are good places to start for most HES research. Depending on your topic, some of the other databases may also be useful. Click "More" to see a complete list of databases, and hover over "info" to see what each one contains. Most of the databases concentrate on journal articles; however, some also contain other resources, such as book chapters, dissertations, or conference proceedings.

Many articles will be available in full text. If not, you will see a link for OU Link to Article. Use it to check if an article is available elsewhere.
