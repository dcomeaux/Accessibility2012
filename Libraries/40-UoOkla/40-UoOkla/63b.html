Making of Modern Law [Info on Making of Modern Law]
( 01/01/1800 - 1926 )
From one of the most important periods of legal development, this archive is a comprehensive full text collection of Anglo-American legal treatises. It allows for full text searching of more than 21,000 works from casebooks, local practice manuals, form books, works for lay readers, pamphlets, letters, speeches and more - all separated into 99 subject areas.

Making of the Modern World [Info on Making of the Modern World]
( 01/01/1450 - 12/31/1850 )
The Making of the Modern World: Goldsmiths'-Kress Library of Economic Literature provides full text images of economic and business literature published from 1450 through 1850. Includes material on commerce, finance, social conditions, politics, trade and transport.

Marketline [Info on Marketline]
MarketLine is a leading provider of quality information on companies, industries and countries. Industry profiles offer standardized market data (including size & segmentation) and competitive analysis. Company profiles for 50,000 public and private companies giving detailed insight into how companies are organized by function. Country profiles present easy-to-digest summaries and standardized data. Base Drivers Database feature key macro-economic, social, demographic and industry statistics for 215 countries.
MAS Ultra - School Edition [Info on MAS Ultra - School Edition]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Indexes and abstracts over 500 magazines and provides full text from nearly 460 periodicals, 1990-present, covering general reference, health, science, and other areas. Also includes over 3,000 charts, tables and graphs; 5,000 full text Magill Book Reviews; full text of 144 books; over 540 health and science-related pamphlets in full text; coverage of the CIA World Factbook; and essential documents in American History.
MasterFILE Premier [Info on MasterFILE Premier]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Indexes and abstracts journals in reference, business, consumer health, general science, and multi-cultural. Some full text available.

MathSciNet [Info on MathSciNet]
( 01/01/1940 - Present )
Indexes and abstracts over a thousand mathematical related publications and is the online version of Mathematical Reviews, produced by the American Mathematical Society.
MEDLINE (EBSCO) [Info on MEDLINE (EBSCO)]

MEDLINE (FirstSearch) [Info on MEDLINE (FirstSearch)]

Index and abstracts to journals published internationally, covering medicine, dentistry, nursing, and other health fields.

MEDLINE (Mobile) [Info on MEDLINE (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to MEDLINE.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, Dell, Axiom, iPhone, and Palm 750.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]

MEDLINE (Ovid) [Info on MEDLINE (Ovid)]
( 01/01/1950 - Present )
Index and abstracts to journals published internationally, covering medicine, dentistry, nursing, and other health fields.
Mental Measurements Yearbook with Tests in Print [Info on Mental Measurements Yearbook with Tests in Print]
Mental Measurements Yearbook provides information and reviews about standardized tests covering educational skills, personality, vocational aptitude, psychology, and related areas. Tests in Print serves as a comprehensive bibliography to all known commercially available tests that are currently in print in the English language. It includes test purpose, test publisher, in-print status, price, test acronym, intended test population, administration times, publication date, and test author.
Mergent Online [Info on Mergent Online ]
Provides detail on items including business description, history, property, subsidiaries, officers and directors, long-term debt and capital stock. Includes U.S. company and international company data such as archive data, earning estimates, equity pricing, and industry reports. Financial statements are presented in "as-reported" form and in native currencies.  Full text available.

Mergent WebReports [Info on Mergent WebReports]
Provides access to an archive of corporate and industry related documents. Contains more than 180,000 documents covering over 100 countries and industries using an easy to navigate and reliable system.
MetaPress [Info on MetaPress ]
MetaPress provides access to hundreds of thousands of articles from hundreds of journals, newsletters and other professional and academic publications. Includes journals from Springer, Kluwer, and Taylor and Francis. Full text available.
