Taxation & Economic Reform in America (HeinOnline) [Info on Taxation & Economic Reform in America (HeinOnline)]
Includes tax regulations, laws, and legislative histories dating back to the late 1700s.  Full text available.
Teatro Espanol del Siglo de Oro [Info on Teatro Espanol del Siglo de Oro]
Access significant literary plays of the sixteenth and seventeenth century in Spain. Full text available.
Tests in Print [Info on Tests in Print]
See Mental Measurements Yearbook.
Theatre in Video [Info on Theatre in Video]
Provides access to streaming video of full-length BBC plays and documentaries.

Thesaurus Linguae Graecae (TLG) [Info on Thesaurus Linguae Graecae (TLG)]
Provides access to most literary texts written in Greek from Homer to the fall of the Byzantium in AD 1453. Funding for this resource is provided by the OU College of Arts & Sciences. Full text available.
Theses Canada Portal [Info on Theses Canada Portal]
( 01/01/1965 - Present )
Indexes multidisciplinary theses in the National Library of Canada Theses Collection and provides full text access to Canadian theses and dissertations that were published from the beginning of 1998 to August 31, 2002. Full text available.
THOMAS [Info on THOMAS]
Provides access to bills, resolutions, activity in Congress, the Congressional Record, committee information, Presidential nominations, treaties, and government resources.

Times Digital Archive [Info on Times Digital Archive]
( 01/01/1785 - 12/31/1985 )
Complete digital archive of The Times (London), except for the Sunday edition. Can be searched using keywords. Materials indexed include all articles, advertisements, illustrations, and photos. Full text available.

Toxicology Abstracts [Info on Toxicology Abstracts]
( 01/01/1981 - Present )
Toxicology Abstracts covers issues from social poisons and substance abuse to natural toxins, from legislation and recommended standards to environmental issues. Surveying the literature for toxicology studies of industrial and agricultural chemicals, household products, pharmaceuticals, and myriad other substances, each issue publishes information concerning the in vivo effects of toxic substances. Toxicity testing methodology and analytical procedures for toxic substances are also covered.

TOXLINE [Info on TOXLINE]
( 01/01/2000 - Present )
Provides access to information on chemicals and pharmaceuticals, pesticides, environmental pollutants, and mutagens and teratogens.
Transportation Research Board (TRB) [Info on Transportation Research Board (TRB)]
Provides access to research and resources on transportation information. Materials indexed include the TRB INDEX, TRIS, SHRP, NCHRP, TCRP and links to sources worldwide. Full text available.

Treaties and Agreements Library (HeinOnline) [Info on Treaties and Agreements Library (HeinOnline)]
Provides access to all U.S. treaties, whether currently in –force, expired, or not yet officially published.  Full text available.
Treaties and International Agreements Online [Info on Treaties and International Agreements Online]
Contains current, comprehensive and accurate versions of over 13,000 treaties and international agreements. Includes all treaties and international agreements ratified by the United States, major treaties in the process of ratification, and all tax treaties, even those to which the United States is not a party.
Thermochemistry of the Chemical Substances [Info on Thermochemistry of the Chemical Substances]
This reference tool is a revision of the table of values for the heats of formation in the thermochemistry section in International Critical Tables. It includes 5,840 values of heats of formation and 350 values of heats of transition, fusion, vaporization, and reaction.

Treatise on Geochemistry [Info on Treatise on Geochemistry]
Provides a comprehensive, integrated summary of geochemistry. The ten-volume set covers Meteorites, Comets, and Planets; The Mantle and Core; The Crust; The Atmosphere; Surface and Ground Water, Weathering, and Soils; The Oceans and Marine Geochemistry; Sediments, Diagenesis, and Sedimentary Rocks; Biogeochemistry; Environmental Geochemistry; and Appendices.
Treatise on Geophysics [Info on Treatise on Geophysics]
This eleven-volume set deals with all major parts of Solid-Earth Geophysics. Topics include Seismology and Structure of the Earth, Mineral Physics, Geodesy, Earthquake Seismology, Geomagnetism, Crustal and Lithosphere Dynamics, Mantle Dynamics, Core Dynamics, Evolution of the Earth, and Planets and Moons.
