Ask Us

Need research help? Ask Us reference service and research assistance is available in person, by telephone, by chat, and through Email a Librarian.

Technical Assistance

Technology problem? Contact the Library Systems Office if you are experiencing technical difficulties using the Library's website or electronic resources.

General Concern

Use the General Contact Form for general questions or comments about OU Libraries or OU Libraries Website.

Circulation, Interlibrary Loan & Fines

Contact Access Services for questions or comments concerning Circulation, Interlibrary Loans, or Fines/Billing.

Department Directory

See department and branch web pages to contact someone for help with a specific area or service, or view contact information for other departments.

Public Relations

Contact Public Relations if you are from the media and desire information about University Libraries, if would like to take photos of University Libraries, or if you have a question about using our facilities.
