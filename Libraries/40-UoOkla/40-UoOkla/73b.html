Wall Street Journal Historical Newspaper [Info on Wall Street Journal Historical Newspaper]
( 01/01/1889 - 12/31/1992 )
Archives the historical Wall Street Journal, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
Washington Post Historical Newspaper [Info on Washington Post Historical Newspaper]
( 01/01/1877 - 1990 )
Archives the historical Washington Post, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
Water Resources Abstracts [Info on Water Resources Abstracts]
( 01/01/1967 - Present )
Water Resources Abstracts provides summaries of the world's technical and scientific literature on water-related topics covering the characteristics, conservation, control, pollution, treatment, use and management of water resources.

Web of Knowledge [Info on Web of Knowledge]
( 01/01/1900 - Present )
Multidisciplinary index covering topics in the sciences, social sciences and humanities. It enables simultaneous searching of Web of Science, Current Contents Connect, BIOSIS Previews, Zoological Record, and Journal Citation Reports. Some full text available.
Web of Science [Info on Web of Science]
( 01/01/1900 - Present )
Multidisciplinary index covering topics in the sciences, social sciences and humanities. It is the electronic equivalent of the Arts & Humanities Citation Index, Social Sciences Citation Index, and Science Citation Index. Some full text available.
Wellesley Index to Victorian Periodicals [Info on Wellesley Index to Victorian Periodicals]
( 01/01/1824 - 12/31/1900 )
Provides an index to the authorship of articles in Victorian periodicals, with pseudonyms and bibliographies of each author listed.
WilsonWeb [Info on WilsonWeb]
A collection of databases available from H.W. Wilson on various subjects. Includes: Art Index, Art Retrospective, Biography Reference Bank, Education Index Retro, General Science Abstract, Humanities & Social Science Retro, Library Literature, and Readers’ Guide Retrospective. Some full text available

WilsonWeb (Mobile) [Info on WilsonWeb (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to select WilsonWeb databases.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
Women & Social Movements in the United States [Info on Women & Social Movements in the United States ]
( 01/01/1600 - 12/31/2000 )
Provides access to historical analysis and documents related to women and social movements in the United States. It also includes a dictionary of social movements and organizations and a chronology of U.S. Women's history.
World Bank Data [Info on World Bank Data]
This database provides access to over 2,000 World Development Indicators (WDI) from the World Bank data sets covering over 200 countries.
World Constitutions Illustrated [Info on World Constitutions Illustrated ]
Contains current constitutions in their original language for over 190 countries.  It also features historical constitutions and secondary resources, including more than 800 books about constitutional law, links to scholarly articles about constitutional development, and links to online sources.  Full text available.
World Shakespeare Bibliography Online [Info on World Shakespeare Bibliography Online]
Access annotated entries for international books, articles, reviews, dissertations, theatrical productions, audiovisual materials, and electronic media related to Shakespeare that have been published since 1964.
World Trials Collection (Hein Online) [Info on World Trials Collection (Hein Online)]
Provides access to trial transcripts and monographs from the 18th century to the 20th century. Full text available.
WorldCat [Info on WorldCat]
An international, multi-disciplinary catalog of books, Internet resources, audio visual materials, manuscripts, maps, sound recordings and other materials held by over 20,000 OCLC libraries. It contains materials representing 400 languages.

WorldCat Dissertations and Theses [Info on WorldCat Dissertations and Theses]
Provides access to records for all dissertations and theses currently in WorldCat.
WorldCat Local [Info on WorldCat Local]
Provides faceted searching of WorldCat, the OU catalog, and select OU article databases.
Worldwide Political Science Abstracts [Info on Worldwide Political Science Abstracts]
( 01/01/1975 - Present )
Covers international literature in political science and its complementary fields, including international relations, law, and public administration/policy. This database combines Political Science Abstracts and ABC POL SCI and includes over 800 journals.
WRDS [Info on WRDS]
Wharton Research Data Service (WRDS) indexes financial, accounting, economic, management, marketing, and banking information and includes insurance data sets. Full text available.
