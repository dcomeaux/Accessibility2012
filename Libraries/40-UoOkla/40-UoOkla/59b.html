IBIS World [Info on IBIS World]
One of the most comprehensive collection of industry market research reports, IBIS World provides instant access to over 700 United States industries as well as “Global” Industry Reports covering world industry analysis. In addition to their core Industry Report product, they also publish over 700 US Industry Risk Rating Reports, over 700 Industry Profiles, over 8000 Publicly Traded US Companies as well as hundreds of Economic & Demographic Profiles.
ICPSR [Info on ICPSR]
( 01/01/1962 - Present )
Provides access to an archive of raw social science data for research and instruction. Electronic access to ICPSR is funded by the College of Arts and Sciences. All questions about ICPSR should be directed to the campus Official Representative for ICPSR, Dr. Keith Gaddie, Department of Political Science.

IEEE Xplore [Info on IEEE Xplore]
( 01/01/1913 - Present )
Full text of IEEE/IEE journals, proceedings, and standards.

ILI Standards [Info on ILI Standards]
Provides access to over 400,000 standards, national and international, of value to engineers. Found the standard you want? Contact the Engineering Librarian about ordering options.
Illustrata: Natural Sciences [Info on Illustrata: Natural Sciences]
( 01/01/1997 - Present )
Provides access to charts, illustrations, tables, and figures from a wide range of biological and environmental disciplines. Some full text available.
Immunology Abstracts [Info on Immunology Abstracts]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to studies related to all aspects of immune systems in man and animals, in normal functions and disorders.
Index Islamicus [Info on Index Islamicus]
Provides access to literature on Islam, the Middle East and the Muslim world, including the main Muslim areas of Asia and Africa and Muslim minorities elsewhere.
Industrial and Applied Microbiology Abstracts (Microbiology A) [Info on Industrial and Applied Microbiology Abstracts (Microbiology A)]
Abstracts significant findings and practical applications in agricultural, food and beverage, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries.
Ingenta Connect [Info on Ingenta Connect]
Multidisciplinary database of more than 11 million citations from over 20,000 journals. Some full text available.
INSPEC [Info on INSPEC]
( 01/01/1898 - Present )
Provides access to the world's scientific and technical literature in physics, electrical engineering, electronics, communications, control engineering, computers, computing, information technology, manufacturing and production engineering.
Institute of Physics [Info on Institute of Physics ]
Multidisciplinary database of more than 11 million citations from over 20,000 journals. Some full text available.
Integrum [Info on Integrum]
Provides access to a large collection of Russian and Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) databases covering a wide range of topics. Includes archives of mass media - such as newspapers, magazines, radio, and television - with reference information. Full text available.
International Bibliography of Art [Info on International Bibliography of Art]
( 01/01/2008 - Present )
The International Bibliography of Art (IBA) covers European and American visual arts from late antiquity to the present. This database provides access to scholarship from over 500 core journals, in addition to detailed coverage of monographs, essay collections, conference proceedings and exhibition catalogs. Covers subjects such as fine arts from all media, museum studies, folk art, classical studies, material culture, antiques, and architectural history.
International Bibliography of Book Reviews (IBR) [Info on International Bibliography of Book Reviews (IBR)]
( 01/01/1985 - Present )
Contains entries about book reviews from an international and interdisciplinary perspective. The reviews have been published in over 6,000 academic journals, mainly in the humanities and social sciences.

International Bibliography of Periodical Literature (IBZ) [Info on International Bibliography of Periodical Literature (IBZ)]
( 01/01/1983 - Present )
IBZ, also known as "The Dietrich", has been published since 1896 and is an international and interdisciplinary bibliography of academic periodical literature mainly from the humanities, social sciences and arts.

International Bibliography of Theatre & Dance [Info on International Bibliography of Theatre & Dance ]
Access content in International Bibliography of Theatre & Dance as well as full text for titles including Canadian Theatre Review, Dance Chronicle, Dance Teacher, Modern Drama, PAJ: A Journal of Performance and Art, Research in Dance Education, Research in Drama Education, Studies in Theatre and Performance, TDR: The Drama Review, Theater, and many more. Full text available.

International Law in Domestic Courts (ILDC) [Info on International Law in Domestic Courts (ILDC)]
Provides access to a repository of domestic cases in international law from over 60 jurisdictions. The cases are selected by local reporters in conjunction with an editorial board and feature commentary, judgments in their original language and English translations of key passages. Full text available.

International Medieval Bibliography [Info on International Medieval Bibliography]
Contains articles from journals and miscellanies such as collected essays, conference proceedings and Festschriften.

ITER: Gateway to the Middle Ages and Renaissance [Info on ITER: Gateway to the Middle Ages and Renaissance]
Provides access to three databases regarding the middle ages and renaissance. The Iter Bibliography indexes journals and essays pertaining to the Middle Ages and Renaissance (400-1700).
Indo-European Etymological Dictionaries Online [Info on Indo-European Etymological Dictionaries Online]

Offers data on physical, thermodynamic, mechanical, and other key properties and is a major reference source used by those involved in chemistry, physics, and engineering.
