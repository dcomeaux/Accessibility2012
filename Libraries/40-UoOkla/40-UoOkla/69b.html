Sanborn Maps (Oklahoma) [Info on Sanborn Maps (Oklahoma)]
Provides access to detailed maps of Oklahoma cities from late 1800s to the mid 1900s. View street names, street and sidewalk widths, property boundaries, building use and physical features, house and block numbers, pipelines, railroads, wells and dumps. Special features include the ability to manipulate the maps, magnify and zoom in on specific sections, and layer maps from different years. Dates vary by city.
ScienceDirect (Elsevier Science) [Info on ScienceDirect (Elsevier Science)]
( 01/01/1995 - Present )
Provides full text access to select Elsevier Science journals.

SciFinder [Info on SciFinder]
Chemical Abstracts Service databases of the chemical literature; indexes journals, conference proceedings, patents, dissertations, reports and books. Some full text available.
Scitation [Info on Scitation]
Indexes journals published by the American Institute of Physics, covering astronomy and astrophysics, biomedical physics, chemical physics, civil engineering, computational physics, environmental physics, fluids and plasmas, and geophysics. Materials indexed include table of contents, abstracts, and over journal articles. Some full text available.

Session Laws Library (HeinOnline) [Info on Session Laws Library (HeinOnline)]
Contains the session laws of all 50 U.S. states, Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands, as well as governmental session laws from the United States, Australia, Canada, and the Bahamas.  Full text available.
Shakespeare in Video [Info on Shakespeare in Video]
Provides access to BBC videos on the Ambrose Digital website.
Shock & Vibration Digest [Info on Shock & Vibration Digest]
Covers areas related to shock and vibration, including noise, vibration technologies, and other areas of relevance to the discipline.

Smithsonian Global Sound for Libraries [Info on Smithsonian Global Sound for Libraries]
Includes the published recordings owned by the non-profit Smithsonian Folkways Recordings label and the archival audio collections of Folkways Records, Cook, Dyer-Bennet, Fast Folk, Monitor, Paredon and other labels. It also includes music recorded around the African continent by Dr. Hugh Tracey for the International Library of African Music (ILAM) at Rhodes University as well as material collected by recordists on the South Asian subcontinent from the Archive Research Centre for Ethnomusicology (ARCE), sponsored by the American Institute for Indian Studies.
Snapshots North America [Info on Snapshots North America]
Access business information including market size, market segmentation, market shares, market distribution, market forecasts, and socio-economic data provided by the Economist Intelligence Unit. Full text available.
Social Science Electronic Data Library (SSEDL) [Info on Social Science Electronic Data Library (SSEDL)]
Health and social science resource containing raw data from over 200 studies in nine topical collections. Full text available.
Social Theory [Info on Social Theory]
Provides access to a selection of documents that focus on the nature of social behavior and organization. Includes full text materials by theorists such as Herbert Spencer, Émile Durkheim, Auguste Comte, Harriet Martineau, and Karl Marx.

Social Work Abstracts [Info on Social Work Abstracts]
( 01/01/1966 - Present )
Indexes and abstracts journals in all areas of the profession, including theory and practice, areas of service, social issues, and social problems. Also includes Register of Clinical Social Workers, a directory of clinical social workers in the United States.

SocINDEX [Info on SocINDEX]
Indexes literature in sociology and related disciplines in the social and behavioral sciences. Provides abstracts and citations to journal articles, books, and conference papers. Includes author profiles.
SocINDEX (Mobile) [Info on SocINDEX (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to SocINDEX.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, Dell, Axiom, iPhone, and Palm 750.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]

South Asia Newspapers [Info on South Asia Newspapers]

Provides access to newspapers from India, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka published between 1864 and 1922.
SPIE Digital Library [Info on SPIE Digital Library]

Provides access to technical papers from SPIE journals and conference proceedings. Full text available.

SportDiscus [Info on SportDiscus]
( 01/01/1949 - Present )
International index that covers all aspects of sport, fitness, recreation, and related fields. Materials indexed include journal articles, book chapters, conference proceedings, audiovisual materials, and websites related to all aspects of sports and fitness. Some full text available.

Springer Protocols [Info on Springer Protocols]
Provides access to full text reproducible laboratory protocols in the life and biomedical sciences.

SpringerLink [Info on SpringerLink]
Provides access to resources in science, technology, and medicine. Full text available.
SRDS [Info on SRDS]
The SRDS database of media rates and information catalogs more than 100,000 U.S. and international media properties. The database contains detailed ad rates, dates and contact data.
State Papers Online: The Government of Britain, 1509-1714 [Info on State Papers Online: The Government of Britain, 1509-1714]
Includes documents from the 16th-18th century British government. Full text available.
Statistical Insight [Info on Statistical Insight]
Access statistics produced by the federal and state government, international intergovernmental organizations, professional and trade organizations, commercial publishers, independent research organizations, and universities. Includes the Congressional Information Service (CIS), the American Statistics Index (ASI), Statistical Reference Index (SRI), and Index to International Statistics (IIS). Some full text available.

References thousands of articles, books, government documents, loose-leaf services, court opinions and Internet sites that compare state laws on hundreds of subjects.
