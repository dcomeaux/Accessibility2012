General Science Abstracts [Info on General Science Abstracts]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Covers topics such as anthropology, astronomy, biology, computers, earth sciences, medicine. Materials indexed include journal and magazine articles.
General Science Abstracts (Mobile) [Info on General Science Abstracts (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to General Science Abstract.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
Genetics Abstracts [Info on Genetics Abstracts]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to a wide variety of resources related to genetics as a whole. Includes information on all aspects of DNA, differentiation and development, RNA, protein synthesis, ribosomes, nuclear proteins and chromatin, enzymes, and gene regulation.
GeoBASE [Info on GeoBASE]
( 01/01/1980 - Present )
Covers geography, geology, ecology, international development, and related disciplines. Materials indexed include journals, books, conference proceedings, and reports.
Geological Guidebooks of North America [Info on Geological Guidebooks of North America]
( 01/01/1940 - 12/31/1988 )
The database consists of references to geologic field trip guidebooks of North America and contains the information in the print publication The Union List of Geologic Field Trip Guidebooks of North America, sixth edition.
GeoRef [Info on GeoRef]
( 01/01/1693 - Present )
International database of geoscience literature of the world. Materials indexed include journal articles, books, maps, conference papers, reports and theses. Covers the geology of North America from 1785 to the present and the geology of the rest of the world from 1933 to the present. The database includes references to all publications of the U.S. Geological Survey. It is the electronic equivalent to Bibliography and Index of Geology.
GeoScience World [Info on GeoScience World]
A comprehensive resource for research and communications in the geosciences, built on a core database aggregation of peer-reviewed journals indexed, linked, and inter-operable with GeoRef.

Global Access WorldScope [Info on Global Access WorldScope]
Includes SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission - formerly COMPACT D database--in depth financial information on public companies) and WorldScope (international companies).
Global Oil and Gas Analyzer [Info on Global Oil and Gas Analyzer]
Global Oil and Gas Analyzer provides detailed historical and forecast data on oil and gas production, consumption and activity worldwide.
Godey's Ladys Book [Info on Godey's Ladys Book ]
( 01/01/1830 - 12/31/1898 )
Provides access to Godey's Lady's Book, a publication intended for American women. Covers topics such as fashion, mineralogy, handcrafts, dance, health and hygiene, recipes, and remedies. Full text available.
Google Scholar [Info on Google Scholar]
Google Scholar enables you to search specifically for scholarly literature, including peer-reviewed papers, theses, books, preprints, abstracts and technical reports from all broad areas of research. Use Google Scholar to find articles from a wide variety of academic publishers, professional societies, preprint repositories and universities, as well as scholarly articles available across the web.
Government Periodicals Index [Info on Government Periodicals Index]
( 01/01/1968 - Present )
Index to 175 government periodicals covering a wide range of subjects. Some full text available.
GPO [Info on GPO]
( 01/01/1976 - Present )
Index of U.S. government documents and some technical reports.
GreenFILE [Info on GreenFILE]
Provides access to scholarly and general interest titles, as well as government documents and reports, related to the the positive and negative ways humans affect the environment. Full text available.

Guardian and Observer Historical Newspapers [Info on Guardian and Observer Historical Newspapers]

Archives the historical Guardian and Observer British newspapers, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
