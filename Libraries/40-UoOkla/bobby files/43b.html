ABA/BNA Lawyers' Manual on Professional Conduct [Info on ABA/BNA Lawyers' Manual on Professional Conduct ]
Provides access to news and analysis on developments in legal ethics, malpractice and other professional issues.
ABI/INFORM Complete [Info on ABI/INFORM Complete]
( 01/01/1971 - Present )
Database covering business and management topics with information on more than 60,000 companies as well as executive profiles, reports on market conditions, and in-depth case studies of global business trends. Includes Snapshots North America, Oxford Analytical Research (OxResearch), Hoover's Company Records. Full text available.
Abstracts in Anthropology [Info on Abstracts in Anthropology]
( 01/01/2001 - Present )
Includes abstracts for articles published in the areas of cultural anthropology, physical anthropology, archaeology, and linguistics. 
Academic Search Premier [Info on Academic Search Premier]
Multidisciplinary database covering social sciences, humanities, education, physical and life sciences, and ethnic studies.

Academic Search Premier (Mobile) [Info on Academic Search Premier (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Academic Search Premier.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, Dell, Axiom, iPhone, and Palm 750.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]

Access: The Supplementary Index to Periodicals [Info on Access: The Supplementary Index to Periodicals]
( 01/01/1987 - Present )
Indexes approximately 85 of the most important popular periodicals such as the New York Review of Books and Rolling Stone.

ACM Digital Library [Info on ACM Digital Library]
( 01/01/1985 - Present )
Includes journal and newsletter articles and conference proceedings published by the Association for Computing Machinery and affiliated organizations. Full text available.
Acta Sanctorum [Info on Acta Sanctorum]
( 01/01/1643 - 12/12/1940 )
Provides access to an electronic version of the complete printed text of Acta Sanctorum, including prefaces, original texts, critical apparatus, and indices. This collection of documents examines the lives of saints, arranged according to each saint's feast day.
African American Newspapers: The 19th Century [Info on African American Newspapers: The 19th Century]
Provides access to Freedom's Journal, The Colored American, The North Star, The National Era, Provincial Freeman, Frederick Douglass Paper, and The Christian Recorder. Full text available.

African Newspapers [Info on African Newspapers]
( 01/01/1800 - 12/31/1922 )
Provides access to more than 40 African newspapers published between 1800 and 1922.
AIAA Electronic Library [Info on AIAA Electronic Library]
( 01/01/1963 - Present )
Contains information on aerospace technology, engineering, and science. Includes journal articles and conference papers from the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Some full text available.
Algology Mycology and Protozoology Abstracts (Microbiology C) [Info on Algology Mycology and Protozoology Abstracts (Microbiology C)]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to literature on reproduction, growth, life cycles, biochemistry, genetics, and infection and immunity in man, other animals, and plants.
America's Historical Imprints [Info on America's Historical Imprints]
( 01/01/1639 - 12/31/1900 )
Covering every aspect of American life during the early decades of the United States, this rich primary source collection provides full text access to early American books, pamphlets and broadsides. Includes Early American Imprints, the Evans Series (1639-1800), the Shaw-Shoemaker Series (1801-1819), and American Broadsides and Ephemera.
America's Historical Newspapers [Info on America's Historical Newspapers]
( 01/01/1690 - 12/31/1922 )
Features early American newspapers with cover-to-cover reproductions of hundreds of historic newspapers, providing more than one million pages of full text, searchable facsimile images.

America: History and Life [Info on America: History and Life]
( 01/01/1954 - Present )
Covers the history and culture of the United States and Canada from prehistoric times to the present, including topics in the social sciences and humanities. Materials indexed include journals articles, book reviews, media reviews, and dissertations.

American Antiquarian Society Historical Periodicals Collection [Info on American Antiquarian Society Historical Periodicals Collection]
( 01/01/1691 - 12/31/1877 )
Presents over 7,600 titles dating from 1691 through 1877, ranging from early works imported by the colonists to the Civil War and Reconstruction.  Covers all aspects of American society during this time period. Full text available.
American Association of Petroleum Geologists Publications [Info on American Association of Petroleum Geologists Publications]
( 01/01/1917 - Present )
A compilation of articles and analogs about petroleum exploration. Provides access to the AAPG Bulletin and AAPG Special Publications, including the SEPM Journal of Sedimentary Research. Materials indexed include bulletin articles, book articles, and meeting paper abstracts. Full text available.

American Bibliography of Slavic and East European Studies (ABSEES) [Info on American Bibliography of Slavic and East European Studies (ABSEES)]
( 01/01/1939 - Present )
Interdisciplinary database covering Slavic and East European studies in regards to the social sciences and humanities. Materials indexed include journal articles, books, book chapters, book reviews, dissertations, online resources, and selected government publications on East-Central Europe and the former Soviet Union published in the United States and Canada. This database replaces the print edition of ABSEES and is now exclusively online.

American Broadsides and Ephemera [Info on American Broadsides and Ephemera]
See America's Historical Imprints for access to this resource.
American Founding Era Collection [Info on American Founding Era Collection]
Provides access to the papers of many of the major figures of the early republic. These full-text digital editions are fully searchable.
American History and Culture Online [Info on American History and Culture Online]
( 01/01/1500 - 1926 )
Sabin Americana contains works about the Americas published throughout the world from 1500 to the early 1900's. Includes books, pamphlets, serials and other documents that provide original accounts of exploration, trade, colonialism, slavery and abolition, the western movement, Native Americans, and military actions. Full text available.
American History in Video [Info on American History in Video]
Provides access to commercial and governmental newsreels, archival footage, public affairs footage, and important documentaries related to American history.
American Periodicals Series [Info on American Periodicals Series]
( 01/01/1740 - 12/31/1941 )
Provides access to over 1,100 periodicals that first began publishing between 1740 and 1900, including special interest and general magazines, journals, and other historically significant periodicals. Full text available.
American West [Info on American West]
Provides access to the Graff Collection. Includes a mixture of original manuscripts, maps, ephemeral material and rare printed sources related to frontier life, to Native Americans and to vigilantes and outlaws as well as the growth of urban centres, the environmental impact of westward expansion and of life in the borderlands. Full text available.

Animal Behavior Abstracts [Info on Animal Behavior Abstracts]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Abstracts materials on animal behavior and related studies. Subjects range from neurophysiology to behavioral ecology and from genetics to applied ethology. Both field and laboratory inquiries are included.
Annual Bibliography of English Language and Literature [Info on Annual Bibliography of English Language and Literature]
( 01/01/1892 - Present )
Provides access to an electronic literary index that covers literary output from 1892 - 1962, in addition to current records. The database lists monographs, periodical articles, critical editions of literary works, book reviews, collections of essays, and doctoral dissertations published around the world.
Année Philologique [Info on Année Philologique]
( 01/01/1924 - Present )
L'Annee Philologique is an international bibliography of books, periodical articles, and dissertations that covers works dealing with all aspects of classical antiquity, from prehistory to the Middle Ages.
Anthropology Plus [Info on Anthropology Plus]
Provides access to Anthropological Literature and Anthropological Index. Covers the fields of anthropology, archaeology, art history, demography, economics, psychology, and religious studies. Materials indexed include journal articles, book series, reports, commentaries, obituaries, and the complete contents of Anthropological Literature: An Index to Periodical Articles and Essays.

AnthroSource [Info on AnthroSource]
Indexes the published journals of the American Anthropological Association. Full text available.

AP Images [Info on AP Images]
Provides access to Associated Press images.

AP NewsMonitor [Info on AP NewsMonitor]
AP News provides near real-time access to top world-wide news from Associated Press on a continuous basis. Provides access to the last 30 days of articles in full-text.
Applied Science & Technology Index Retrospective [Info on Applied Science & Technology Index Retrospective]
( 01/01/1913 - 31-DEC-83 )
Indexes a wide range of interdisciplinary fields covered in a broad array of applied science journals, including the complete content of Industrial Arts Index (1913-1957) and Applied Science & Technology Index (1958-1983).
Applied Science & Technology Index Retrospective (Mobile) [Info on Applied Science & Technology Index Retrospective (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Applied Science & Technology Index Retrospective.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
Archive Finder [Info on Archive Finder]
Current directory of repositories and collections of primary source material across the United States and the United Kingdom. Includes the entire National Union Catalog of Manuscript Collections, as well as descriptions of holdings and information to contact the repository directly.

Archive of Celtic-Latin Literature [Info on Archive of Celtic-Latin Literature]
Contains over 1300 Celtic-Latin texts from the period 400-1200 A.D.
ArchiveGrid [Info on ArchiveGrid]
Allows searching through historical documents, personal papers, and family histories held in archives around the world.

Aristoteles Latinus [Info on Aristoteles Latinus]
Medieval Greek-Latin translations of Aristotle's works.
Art Index [Info on Art Index]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Includes materials on traditional art, art history, archaeology, architecture, fashion design, interior design, visual art, and non-western art. Materials indexed include journal articles, yearbooks, and museum bulletins. Includes multi-lingual material.
Art Index (Mobile) [Info on Art Index (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Art Index.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
Art Index Retrospective [Info on Art Index Retrospective]
( 01/01/1929 - 12/31/1984 )
Includes materials on traditional art, art history, archaeology, architecture, fashion design, interior design, visual art, and non-western art. Materials indexed include journal articles, yearbooks, and museum bulletins. Includes multi-lingual material.
Art Index Retrospective (Mobile) [Info on Art Index Retrospective (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Art Index Retrospective.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
ARTbibliographies Modern [Info on ARTbibliographies Modern]
( 01/01/1974 - Present )
Indexes modern and contemporary arts from the late 19th century onwards and includes traditional arts, technology-based arts, performance arts, photography and crafts. Materials indexed include journal articles, books essays, exhibition catalogs, dissertations, and exhibition reviews.
ARTFL [Info on ARTFL]
American and French Research on the Treasury of the French Language (ARTFL) covers classic works of French literature, non-fiction prose, and technical writing from the 13th through the 20th century. Some full text available.
Article1st [Info on Article1st]
( 01/01/1990 - Present )
General database covering all subject areas, including business, science, humanities, social science, medicine, technology, and popular culture. Provides citations to more than 12,600 academic journals and magazines.
ARTstor [Info on ARTstor]
Repository of images that include collections from a wide variety of civilizations, time-periods, and media. The images are drawn from different sources, such as museums, archaeological teams, photo archives, slide collections, and art reference publishers.
ASCE Library [Info on ASCE Library]
This database is an online tool for locating articles across all disciplines of civil engineering. It provides access to more than 80,000 full-text articles from ASCE Journals and Proceedings.
ASFA 3: Aquatic Pollution and Environmental Quality [Info on ASFA 3: Aquatic Pollution and Environmental Quality]
( 01/01/1990 - Present )
This database is devoted exclusively to research and policy on the contamination of oceans, seas, lakes, rivers, and estuaries. ASFA 3 contains information that will prove essential to specialists who deal in any capacity with aquatic environments and marine pollution problems, including biologists, oceanographers, limnologists, environmental engineers and scientists, industrial engineers, waste managers, corporate regulatory affairs managers, and government officials.

ASFA Aquaculture Abstracts [Info on ASFA Aquaculture Abstracts]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Provides access to studies related to cultivating marine, freshwater, and brackishwater species.
ASFA Marine Biotechnology Abstracts [Info on ASFA Marine Biotechnology Abstracts]
( 01/01/1989 - Present )
Provides access to information on the science, technology, and management of marine environments.
ASTM Standards and Engineering Digital Library [Info on ASTM Standards and Engineering Digital Library]
The ASTM Standards and Digital Library contains a large collection of industry-leading standards and technical engineering information.This database covers a variety of engineering disciplines, including aerospace, biomedical, chemical, environmental, geological, health and safety, industrial, materials science, mechanical, nuclear, petroleum, soil science, and solar engineering.
ATLA Historical Monographs Collection [Info on ATLA Historical Monographs Collection]
Presents more than 15,000 titles from 13th Century through the 1893 World Parliament of Religions. It includes many volumes documenting the recovery of languages used during the biblical era and provides an in-depth view of the way that interest in, and knowledge of, biblical languages emerged during the nineteenth century, an important foundation for today’s study and understanding of biblical languages. It also contains titles in Aramaic, Arabic, Greek, Hebrew and Syriac.
ATLA Religion Database [Info on ATLA Religion Database]
( 01/01/1949 - Present )
Covers topics in biblical studies, world religions, church history, and religion in social issues. Indexes citations to journal articles, book reviews, and collections of essays. Includes ALTASerials, an online collection of religion and theology journals, which provides some full text access.

Atlanta Daily World Historical Newspaper [Info on Atlanta Daily World Historical Newspaper]
( 01/01/1931 - 12/31/2003 )
Archives the African-American historical newspaper the Atlanta Daily World, including full page digital reproductions of every page from all available issues. Full text available.
Audio Drama: The L.A. Theatre Works Collection [Info on Audio Drama: The L.A. Theatre Works Collection]
Over 300 important dramatic works in streaming audio, featuring literature from the 19th through 21st centuries performed by leading actors.
Avery Index to Architectural Periodicals [Info on Avery Index to Architectural Periodicals]
( 01/01/1934 - Present )
Indexes periodicals published worldwide on architecture, archaeology, interior design, and historic preservation. Covers the international scholarly and popular periodical literature, including the publications of professional associations, US state and regional periodicals, major serial publications in the architecture and design of Europe, Asia, Latin America, and Australia.
AccessScience [Info on AccessScience]
A collection of science and technology reference materials from the latest edition of the McGraw-Hill Encyclopedia of Science & Technology, Research Updates from the McGraw-Hill Yearbooks, The McGraw-Hill Dictionary of Scientific & Technical Terms, and the latest Science News® headlines. Materials are fully illustrated and searchable. Full text available.

African-American Almanac [Info on African-American Almanac]
Provides historical and current information on African American history, society, and culture in 28 topical chapters. Includes a chronology, a chapter of important primary documents, directories of organizations and businesses, a bibliography of recently-published works, annotated lists of crucial court cases, a filmography, hundreds of brief biographies, and more than 650 photographs, illustrations, maps, and statistical charts within the text.
American Foreign Relations Since 1600: A Guide to the Literature [Info on American Foreign Relations Since 1600: A Guide to the Literature]
American History Through Literature [Info on American History Through Literature]
American Indian Art Series [Info on American Indian Art Series]
Includes Hopi-Tewa Pottery, Pueblo Indian Pottery, American Indian Textiles, Southern Pueblo Pottery, American Indian Jewelry, and American Indian Baskets by Gregory Schaaf.
American Indian Religious Traditions: An Encyclopedia [Info on American Indian Religious Traditions: An Encyclopedia]
Written from an American Indian perspective with input from religious scholars and community leaders, this pioneering reference work explores indigenous North American religions and religious practices and rituals.
American National Biography Online [Info on American National Biography Online]
Access biographies of individuals from all eras who have influenced and shaped American history and culture. The portraits reflect the rich diversity of American life from pre-colonial times onwards. Full text available.
American Women Writers [Info on American Women Writers]
Provides coverage on the best-known American women writers spanning from colonial days to the present, including biography, criticism, and bibliography for each; also includes lesser-known writers whose work is found in journals, letters, stories, and even hymns. Entries feature an assessment of the writer's works; basic biography and complete bibliography, as well as comprehensive data on writers' names, including pseudonyms, aliases, parents' names and husbands' names, etc.
Ancient Europe 8000 B.C. to A.D. 1000 Encyclopedia of the Barbarian World [Info on Ancient Europe 8000 B.C. to A.D. 1000 Encyclopedia of the Barbarian World]
Examines the diverse peoples of early European civilization through a series of 212 essays, presented in chronological order. Coverage includes prehistoric origins through the early Middle Ages (8000 B.C. to A.D. 1000). Includes maps, photos, and chronologies.
Annual Reviews [Info on Annual Reviews]
( 01/01/1932 - Present )
Access to 29 Annual Reviews publications: Anthropology, Biochemistry, Astronomy & Astrophysics, Biomedical Engineering, Biophysics & Biomolecular Structure, Energy & the Environment, Cell & Developmental Biology, Clinical Psychology, Immunology, Earth & Planetary Sciences, Medicine, Ecology & Systematics, Nutrition, Entomology, Fluid Mechanics, Genetics, Genomics & Human Genetics, Law and Social Science, Materials Research, Microbiology, Neuroscience, Nuclear & Particle Science, Pharmacology & Toxicology, Physical Chemistry, Physiology, Phytopathology, Pathology, Plant Physiology, Political Science, Psychology, Public Health, Sociology. Full text available.
