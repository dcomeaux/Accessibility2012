Bacteriology Abstracts (Microbiology B) [Info on Bacteriology Abstracts (Microbiology B)]
Medically oriented abstracts on topics ranging from bacterial immunology and vaccinations to diseases of man and animals. Materials indexed include journal articles and clinical findings on all aspects of pure bacteriology, biochemistry, and genetics.

Bibliography of Asian Studies (BAS) [Info on Bibliography of Asian Studies (BAS)]
Indexes western-language articles and book chapters on East, Southeast, and South Asia.

Bibliography of Native North Americans (BNNA) [Info on Bibliography of Native North Americans (BNNA)]
Covers Native North American history, life, and culture from the Human Relations Area Files (HRAF). Materials indexed include the cumulative eight volumes of the Ethnographic Bibliography of North America, books, journal articles, essays, conference papers, dissertations and U.S. government documents.

Bibliography of the History of Art [Info on Bibliography of the History of Art]
( 01/01/1975 - 12/31/2007 )
Also see International Bibliography of Art (IBA).
Biography and Genealogy Master Index (BGMI) [Info on Biography and Genealogy Master Index (BGMI)]
Covers biographical material on individuals from all time periods, geographical locations, and fields of endeavor. Indexes material from current and major retrospective publications and includes 13 million entries.

Biography Reference Bank [Info on Biography Reference Bank]
Biography Reference Bank contains biographical information on approximately half a million people, from antiquity to the present. It contains the full-text of the articles from more than 100 volumes of biographical reference books and provides citations to biographical magazine articles.
Biography Reference Bank (Mobile) [Info on Biography Reference Bank (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Biography Reference Bank.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, iPhone, and Android phones.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]
Biological Sciences [Info on Biological Sciences]
( 01/01/1982 - Present )
Provides access to abstracts and citations for research in the fields of biomedicine, biotechnology, zoology and ecology, and some agriculture and veterinary science.
Includes serials, conference proceedings, technical reports, monographs, books, and patents.
BioOne [Info on BioOne]
( 01/01/1998 - Present )
Indexes over 50 bioscience research journals, focusing on biological, ecological, and environmental sciences. Full text access.
BIOSIS Citation Index [Info on BIOSIS Citation Index]
( 01/01/2010 - Present )
Provides access to resources in the life sciences and biomedical research from journals, meetings, patents, and books.

BIOSIS Previews [Info on BIOSIS Previews]
( 01/01/1926 - present )
Provides access to the content of journals, meetings, patents, and books in the areas of life sciences and biomedical research covering pre-clinical and experimental research, methods and instrumentation, animal studies, and more.

Biotechnology Research Abstracts [Info on Biotechnology Research Abstracts]
( 01/01/1993 - Present )
Provides access to the world literature in which molecular biology and genetics are applied to the uses for biotechnological techniques in the food industry, agriculture, and the environment.

Black Drama [Info on Black Drama]
Provides access to approximately 1310 plays by 210 playwrights, together with detailed, fielded information on related productions, theaters, production companies, and more. It also includes selected playbills, production photographs and other ephemera related to the plays.

Black Freedom [Info on Black Freedom]
( 01/01/1890 - 12/31/1990 )
Provides access to texts that document the Black Freedom Struggle in the 20th century from the perspectives of men, women, and children. Contains more than 35 collections from the records of federal government agencies.
Black Studies Center [Info on Black Studies Center]
Includes the Schomburg Studies on the Black Experience, International Index to Black Periodicals (IIBP), the Black Literature Index, and historical black newspapers such as the Chicago Defender.

Black Thought and Culture [Info on Black Thought and Culture]
Contains the non-fiction published works of leading African Americans. Access interviews, journal articles, letters and other materials. Full text available.
Books In Print [Info on Books In Print]
Provides extensive bibliographic, format and cost information, as well as information on reviews, awards, and synopses for millions of titles.
British Literary Manuscripts [Info on British Literary Manuscripts]
( 01/01/1120 - 12/31/1900 )
Provides access to facsimile images of letters, poems, plays, novels, and other literary works.
British Newspapers (1600-1900) [Info on British Newspapers (1600-1900)]
( 01/01/1600 - 12/31/1900 )
Includes the Nineteenth Century British Library Newspapers and the Seventeenth-Eighteenth Century Burney Collection Newspapers.  Full text available.


British Periodicals [Info on British Periodicals]
( 01/01/1680 - 1/1/1930 )
Provides access to periodicals from the late seventeenth century to the early twentieth. Formats include magisterial quarterlies, scholarly and professional organs, coterie art periodicals, penny weeklies and illustrated family magazines. Full text available.
Bureau of National Affairs [Info on Bureau of National Affairs]
Provides online access to several BNA titles, including: ABA/BNA Lawyers' Manual on Professional Conduct, Antitrust & Trade Regulation Report, Corporate Counsel Weekly, Criminal Law Reporter, Family Law Reporter, International Trade Reporter, Labor and Employment Law Library, Mergers & Acquisitions, Securities Regulation & Law Report, and U.S. Law Week.
Business Source Premier [Info on Business Source Premier]
( 01/01/1984 - Present )
Business Source Elite covers business, management, economics, banking, finance, and accounting. Materials indexed include scholarly and peer-reviewed journals. Some full text available.

Business Source Premier (Mobile) [Info on Business Source Premier (Mobile)]
Provides mobile device access to Business Source Premier.  Supports the following devices: BlackBerry, Dell, Axiom, iPhone, and Palm 750.  [Note: You must be logged into the libraries' website to use this service from your mobile device. For easy, front-page access, add EBSCOhost Mobile to your My Library database list.]

Berkshire Encyclopedia of World History [Info on Berkshire Encyclopedia of World History]
A five- volume encyclopedia of world history, covering 250,000 years of human history. Compiles knowledge from paleoanthropology, archaeology, geology, and geography. Emphasis on cultural contact and social change over time and place. Overviews of core topics. Extensive coverage of arts, literature, religion, and science. Biographies of 100 leaders who shaped world history.

Birds of North America [Info on Birds of North America]
Covers the life histories of North American birds. Provides detailed scientific information for each of the species of birds nesting in North America.
