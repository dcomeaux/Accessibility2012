Welcome

On behalf of our faculty and staff, I cordially invite all students, faculty, staff, and the general public to visit the Bizzell Memorial Library, the branch libraries, and special collections, and to use our easily navigated website at http://libraries.ou.edu.

To University of Oklahoma students, I would like to extend a very special invitation. We believe it is important for students to learn how to use the University Libraries early in their academic careers and our library faculty and staff are committed to assisting them. Our goal is to provide everyone who uses the OU Libraries with the information resources they need. A summary of University Libraries resources and services is offered below. Our librarians and staff are ready to help with your information needs. I encourage you to contact them for assistance.

Sul H. Lee
Dean, University Libraries
Peggy V. Helmerich Chair
