Classes & Tours

The librarians in Reference and Instructional Services are available to meet with classes with a research component. Librarians teach instruction sessions that are tailored to the assignment and level of the students. Each department has a subject specialist who works with faculty on these sessions. If you have questions about library instruction, please contact Amy Harris
