Friends, Events & Giving

Giving to the University Libraries

Your contribution to the University Libraries helps serve the intellectual enterprises of UNCG's students, faculty, and the broader community. Thank you.

[Donate to Jackson Library]

There are several ways to make your donation to the University Libraries. Please visit our Giving To the University Libraries page for more information.

Would you like to make a donation to the University Libraries to honor or remember someone? Contact Linda Burr at 336-356-0184.

Friends of the Library

The Friends of the Libraries are advocates and supporters of the Libraries. Today, with costs rising and budgets tight, the Libraries need the friends' support more than ever. Our Friends make a real difference in our ability to serve the campus and the local community.

You can support the Libraries by joining the Friends of the Libraries.
You'll be in good company - thousands of alumni, faculty members and community residents have come together for the Libraries over the last 40 years.
