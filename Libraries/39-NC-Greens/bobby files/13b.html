Teaching online...

We may provide instructional related services to you, if you contact us in advance, such as:

  • Visiting a remote location for library instruction.
  • An embedded librarian in Blackboard available for threaded discussions or virtual office hours.
  • Teaching an online synchronous session in your course using our library's Blackboard Collaborate virtual room.
  • Creation of customized library resourcesfor your class into Blackboard - under the Libary Tab like this.
  • Creation of tutorials/screencasts of specific online tasks or resources.
  • An libguide of resources for classes.
