New Books and Media

We've changed the way we offer new book lists.

Previously, new material lists could be viewed in a range of categories, as webpages or as RSS feeds. The lists of materials were refreshed quarterly, and were frequently out of date.

In an effort to simplify the process, and to offer more current information, we've decided to limit new material lists to the top 16 subscribed feeds. These can be followed in any feed reader (click on the link, then copy the URL to your reader of choice).
