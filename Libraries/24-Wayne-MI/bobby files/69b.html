About

The Student Technology Studio (STS) supports the mission of Wayne State University by helping students master technology that will enable them to succeed in their courses and become better prepared to participate in a global society and working environment.

Policies

Use of Facility:

  • For currently registered students
  • Must show OneCard
  • Must sign-in
  • Drop-in use

The STS Lab is not to be used for the following:

  • Checking e-mail
  • Surfing the Internet
  • Personal uses not related to coursework
  • Using resources to complete assignments that require only standard hardware and software that is readily available elsewhere on campus or in the libraries
