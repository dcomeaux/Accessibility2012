Improve Your Libraries: Digital Suggestion Box

The WSU Libraries welcome your suggestions, ideas and comments about the ways we can improve our services.

Responses to comments made through the digital suggestion box may be seen in the Improve Your Libraries blog. To respect your confidentiality, we will not include your name or contact information when posting comments on the blog.

If you have a reference question, those may be submitted through Ask-A-Librarian. Also here are departmental and individual staff phone number listings.
