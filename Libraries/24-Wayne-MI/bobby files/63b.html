EndNote®

  • Software program available free to faculty, staff and students of Wayne State University.
  • Store and organize citation information when composing research papers.
  • Insert citations while at the same time creating bibliographies in the style of your choice.

Download EndNote - Download EndNote free from WSU Computing & Information Technology Software Clearinghouse

EndNote tutorials and online training - View online tutorials from EndNote or sign up for an online live training session

Short Videos on Using EndNote functions at Wayne State University – View online video demos of exporting to EndNote from the catalog, Google and more

EndNote Resources - Handout developed to assist Wayne State EndNote users

EndNote Technical Support - Call or email EndNote Technical Support if you are having a problem using EndNote

EndNote Export Providers - A list of library databases that support direct export into EndNote

Zotero

  • Free, easy-to-use extension of the Firefox browser.
  • Collect, manage, and cite your research sources.
  • Export your citations as formatted references into Microsoft Word documents.

Download Zotero – You will need to use a Firefox browser to install Zotero

Zotero Installation Instructions – Information that might help if you are having trouble installing Zotero.

Zotero tutorials – View online tutorials about how to use Zotero

Zotero support – Read online guides on how to use Zotero

Microsoft Office Word Citations & Bibliography

  • A citation manager built into Microsoft Office Word 2007
  • Collect, manage and cite your research sources

Create a Bibliography – An online guide to entering citations and creating a bibliography using Microsoft Office Word Citations & Bibliography function on the References tab

E-books for using Microsoft Word – A selection of e-books from the WSU library system collection on using Microsoft Word. Click on the book title, then click on Open Safari E-book to access the e-book contents.
