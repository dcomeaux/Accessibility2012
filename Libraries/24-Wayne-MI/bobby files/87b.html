Wayne State University has five major Libraries on campus and one extension center. Choose a library for maps and/or directions.

All campus libraries except the Shiffman Medical Library are located at Main Campus on Gullen Mall. Gullen Mall runs north-south, and sits between Anthony Wayne Drive on the west and Cass Avenue on the east, Palmer Avenue on the North and Warren Avenue on the south. Main Campus is just south of the Ford Freeway (I-94) and just east of the Lodge (M-10).
