Library Initiatives & Projects

The Gulf Oil Spill Information Center houses extensive information on the BP Deepwater Horizon oil spill and cleanup efforts in the Gulf.

The Karst Information Portal is a digital library linking scientists, managers, and explorers with quality information resources concerning karst environments.

The Holocaust & Genocide Studies Center is devoted to understanding and preventing genocide through global education and action.

The Florida Studies Center draws upon an extensive Floridiana collection to promote teaching and research of Florida's past, present and future.

The Oral History Program documents the surrounding community by creating and disseminating original interviews using state of the art technologies.

The Textbook Affordability Project (TAP) helps counter the rising cost of an education by connecting students and faculty with alternatives to full-price textbooks.
