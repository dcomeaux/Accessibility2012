Research Consultation Request

We’re here to help! Need to find articles or other resources for your paper or end of term project?

Research consultations must be scheduled at least 3 business days in advance to allow for adequate preparation time. Appointments are available 8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m., Monday – Friday. Inquire about exceptions for evening-only students. Faculty and staff working on grants and publications and students beginning a thesis, dissertation, or research paper are encouraged to use this service. Please sign up if you’d like to schedule a consultation with a librarian from your discipline to assist with your research. For immediate help, please Ask a Librarian »
