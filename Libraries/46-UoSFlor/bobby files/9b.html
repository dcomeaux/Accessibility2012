What is this?

The USF Libraries subscribe to many online research materials subject to license restrictions that limit off-campus access to USF students and employees only. This system verifies that you are a current USF affiliate and then enables access from your computer.

* Key

  • USF NetID: All students and employees of USF should obtain a NetID. If you do not have one, click here »
  • LibraryID: This is the 14 digit number beginning with 205 . . . on your USF ID card or library card.
  • Password: USF students, faculty, and staff: Until you change it in YOUR ACCOUNT, enter birthday (MMDD -- e.g. enter 0205 for February 5th).

If this doesn't work, please use 1234 or call the Tampa Library circulation desk at (813) 974-1611. For further assistance, Ask-a-Librarian »
