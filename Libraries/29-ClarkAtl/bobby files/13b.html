Wireless Access

Wireless access is available in most areas of Robert W. Woodruff Library. This service is available to any student, staff, or faculty. To access the Woodruff wireless network perform the following steps:

 

  • Power on your laptop, the wireless adapter should find the Woodruff public SSID rwwlpbwl01
  • If your laptop does not automatically detect the public wireless configure your adapter settings to use the SSID of rwwlpbwl01
  • Open your web browser
