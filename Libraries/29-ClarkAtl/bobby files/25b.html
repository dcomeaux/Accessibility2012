Interlibrary Loan

Help Desk Number:
(404) 978-2025

 

  • First Time Users
  • Returning Users
  • Distance Learning Users
  • General Information on Interlibrary Loan and Document Delivery
 

The interlibrary loan system previously used by the Robert W. Woodruff has been replaced. The new system, ILLiad Interlibrary Loan Management System, is a web-based interface that will allow users to access the Woodruff Interlibrary Loan Service from any internet browser.

 

Once users have set up their user profiles, they may then enjoy the convenience of keying in their names and passwords for access to their Woodruff Library Interlibrary Loan accounts. The ILLiad Interlibrary Loan Management System allows users to have immediate access to the status of their requested documents. Subsequent requests may be placed without having to re-enter personal information.

 

To establish a Woodruff Library ILLiad user account, library users will need to obtain a Woodruff Library barcode from our Circulation Department.

 

If you have questions or require additional information, please contact our Interlibrary Loan Librarian at ill@auctr.edu or 404-978-2025.
