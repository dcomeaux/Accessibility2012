FIND JOURNALS IN BULEY


When you want to know if Buley has a specific journal, you have two options:

1. Use the Journal Locator.
This will indicate what databases provide online access to the journal you're looking for. Click on the link to the database to search for articles within the journal. You can access online journals from off-campus if you set up a library PIN.

If we don't have online access, but do have copies of the journal on paper or microform, you'll see a link for SCSU Print and Microfilm Serials Collection, located on the lower level of the library.

2. Use CONSULS Journal Title search.
This will indicate whether we have the journal in print or microform. CONSULS serves all 4 CSU campuses, so please be sure that the journal is at SCSU, not CCSU, ECSU, or WCSU. Only some online titles are included in CONSULS; check the Journal Locator for online titles.
