G.E. Bentley Blake Collection
A collection of works by and about William Blake and his contemporaries, at Victoria University Library, Special Collections, it contains original Blake manuscripts and prints, Blake's contemporary commercial engravings, and modern reproductions and facsimiles. Manuscripts and drawings of George Cumberland, books by John Flaxman, illustrated books before 1835, and Blake scholarship and criticism also form part of this collection.
