The Maps Collection acquires cartographic resources for all world areas in a variety of formats: sheet maps, atlases, gazetteers, cd-roms, journals, and microfiche. Map Finder provides access to maps not cited in the online catalog. Please consult the Maps staff for further assistance in locating cartographic resources.

NOTE: The Map Finder does not display maps online, rather it assists in locating storage location information that must then be presented to Maps Collection personnel to view the maps.
