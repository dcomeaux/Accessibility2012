On the NSF requirement

[nsf logo]The (very general) guidelines for data management plans are presented in Section j. Special Information and Supplementary Documentation of the Grant Proposal Guide, Chapter II - Proposal Preparation Instructions. They are the second bulleted item in the linked section.

Data management requirements and plans specific to the Directorate, Office, Division, Program, or other NSF unit, relevant to a proposal are available at: http://www.nsf.gov/bfa/dias/policy/dmp.jsp. If guidance specific to the program is not available, then the general requirements listed at the first web site (i.e., in Section j, linked above) apply.

A break-down of the basics from the Research Remix blog, quoting the NSF documentation and with links to helpful references.

See also NSF Data Management & Sharing FAQs.
