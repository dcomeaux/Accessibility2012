What is .eDU?

Stay on top of the latest in the electronic world with this free series of lunchtime sessions on topics for your e-life.

Learn new things and practice skills that you can use in class, at a job, or just for fun.

When and Where?

  • Every other Wednesday, beginning February 1, 2012
  • 12:15-12:45pm
  • Undergraduate Library, Room 124

Who can attend?

.eDU classes are open to all members of the UNC community. Students, faculty, and staff are welcome to participate. No registration is required, and you are welcome to attend only the sessions that interest you.

All classes are introductory and assume no prior knowledge, other than general computer skills.
