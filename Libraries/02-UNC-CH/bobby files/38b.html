Disability Services

The University Libraries are committed to providing access to their collections, resources, and to all library users, including people with physical and learning disabilities.

Please inform us if you need any assistance in using the Library. If your needs are not met by the policies and procedures described here, please notify us. We will examine and, if necessary, extend our policies and procedures to accommodate your needs.
