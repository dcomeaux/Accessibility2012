Davis Library Research and Instructional Services actively collects spatial data for our collection. We have a large collection of data layer sets, each of which is made up of potentially many individual data layers. The GIS Data Finder allows our patrons to browse our collection in several ways.

Browse data by thematic category  |  Search the GIS Data Finder  |  View all data layers  |  View all data layer sets
NOTE: Davis Library Research and Instructional Services asks our patrons to honor our license agreements with the companies and agencies that publish spatial data. The licenses for our spatial data may limit who is allowed to use it and in what ways it may be used. The GIS Data Finder clearly states any restrictions imposed on our holdings. Redistribution of restricted data to unauthorized users is strictly prohibited.
