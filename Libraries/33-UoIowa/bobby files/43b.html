My Personal Library

Check Your Account

Your Library Account will show a list of items that you currently have checked out and their due dates. It will also show a list of your requests and their status. This includes recalls of items from other patrons as well as searches for missing material. 

Renew Your Books

Your Library Account allows renewals for library materials on short-term loans (non-Reserve loans that are four weeks or shorter). Renewals are unlimited. For library materials on long-term loan, renewals are unlimited but books must be brought in to one of the libraries to be renewed.

Interlibrary Loan/Article Delivery Request System

Request a book, photocopy of an article or book chapter, view/modify outstanding requests, renew checked out items, and view request history.

Recommend a library purchase

Use this form to suggest journals or books that you would like to see added to the Library's collection. We welcome your input and thank you for your interest in our collection.

RefWorks

RefWorks is a web-based citation management and bibliography tool. It can help you to efficiently store, organize, and share citations and easily format them for papers and bibliographies.
