Library Closures

May 14, 2010: The Physics and Geosciences Libraries are closed. Materials from the libraries have been moved to the Sciences Library (formerly the Biological Sciences Library) on Iowa Avenue. The lesser used and electronically available materials have been moved to the Annex. The map cases from the Geoscience Library will be moved to the Main Library lower level. Leo Clougherty, head of the Sciences Library, will have an office in the Sciences Library. Kari Kozak, Sciences Librarian will maintain an office in Van Allen  Hall as the liasion with the department of physics and astronomy. For questions about science information resources, please contact Leo (leo-clougherty@uiowa.edu) or Kari (kari-kozak@uiowa.edu).
