Add UIowaLibRef to your buddy list on these networks.

[AIM] AOL
[Yahoo IM] Yahoo
[MSN IM] MSN
[Google Talk IM] Google
[Meebo] Meebo

You might need to use uiowalibref@hotmail.com for your buddy list on MSN.

This service is available during the Main Library Information Desk hours. When we are offline, see Ask a Librarian.

Additional chat services are available from the Ask a Librarian page.

TXT-to-IM
Send us a question by TXT and receive a short answer on your cell phone. Send a TXT to the “short code” number 265010. Your message must begin with uiowalibref followed by a space. Example: uiowalibref how late is Main Library open today? We receive your TXT in our chat window, recognize it as coming from a cell phone, and send back a brief reply. Note: your phone carrier will charge your cell plan for both the outgoing and incoming TXT.

 

Messages are carried on non-university systems.
Please refer to the network provider's privacy policy before sending confidential information.
