Student Employment (FAQs)

The University Libraries continually accepts applications from qualified students wanting to work in the libraries. Most vacant positions are advertised on Jobnet. Come in to the Main Library Administrative office to complete an application. The UI’s Office of Student Financial Aid has general information about student employment.

The University of Iowa is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer. Women and minorities are encouraged to apply.
