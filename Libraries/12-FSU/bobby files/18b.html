Our desired contribution to your success extends beyond simply providing access to scholarly resources. We do understand that utilizing the Libraries' resources at a distance comes with unique challenges. We hope to establish a meaningful and active relationship that will support your goals. These pages will:

    1. Orient you to the Libraries' website so that you can find your way to our resources
    2. Teach you how to use our resources effectively
    3. Provide one-on-one reference/research assistance
    4. Keep you abreast of signifigant developments within the University Libraries
