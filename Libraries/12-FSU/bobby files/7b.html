Who Can Borrow?

FSU Community
Members of the FSU community are granted borrowing privileges with their current FSU Card. 
See the FSU Card Center if you do not have a current FSU Card. 

  • Undergraduate students currently registered for classes
  • Graduate students currently registered for classes
  • Faculty  with appointments
  • Visiting faculty and researchers with an appointment from an FSU department
  • Adjunct faculty only while actively teaching courses
  • Full time staff
  • OPS employees
  • Retired FSU faculty and staff

Florida Community Colleges and Public Universities
Current students, faculty, and staff of the Florida Community Colleges and Public Universities are granted borrowing privileges.  Current university I.D. must be presented and verified.

Special Borrowing Permits
Free Special Borrowing Permits are given only to the following patrons.  Supporting documentation must be submitted with the application: 

  • Visiting scholars sponsored by an FSU department
  • Florida government employees (state or county)
  • Federal government employees
  • Staff and students in special FSU affiliated programs

Alumni and Visitors
Alumni and visitors are invited to conduct research inside the university libraries.  Borrowing privileges for alumni and visitors can be obtained by purchasing a Friends of the FSU Libraries membership.
