In addition, you can continue to schedule your class for a library instruction session. Library faculty will customize the session to your individual course and even to a particular assignment. A librarian will teach your students how to use appropriate library resources including subject databases to locate the information needed and how to properly cite their sources. This instruction can include or be focused on:

  • Electronic and print materials
  • Online catalog (LIUCat) to find books in the collection
  • Online databases to find periodical articles
  • Scholarly and peer-reviewed journals
  • Internet resources

Individual sessions can also focus on:

  • Citing references
  • Evaluating web sites
  • Plagiarism
  • Specialized collections and/or departments such as the Center for Business Research, Library & Information Science, Periodicals, Instructional Media Center (IMC), Special Collections, and the Digital Initiatives & Art Slide Library

You can now request a library instruction session for your class through our online request form.

We look forward to working with you.
