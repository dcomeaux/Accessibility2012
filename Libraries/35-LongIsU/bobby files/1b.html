Databases Optimized for Mobile Access
Some of the database vendors have designed simplified versions of their websites specifically to work with smartphones and other mobile devices that have small screens. You can use the links below to get to them. To use the full-featured versions of these sites or to see the complete listings of databases that we subscribe to (along with descriptions of them), please go to our main database page.

You can also download the Ebscohost Mobile App to your iPhone, iPod Touch or iPad. Follow the instructions at: http://library.liu.edu/apps/ebsco/ebsco_main.html to authenticate and install the app.
