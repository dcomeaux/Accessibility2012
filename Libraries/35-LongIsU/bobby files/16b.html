Special Collections


The Center for Business Research (CBR), dedicated to furthering the information literacy of its users, serves the students and faculty of Long Island University. Its collections consist of reference sources shelved on the main floor of the Library in the Business, Law, and Information Science Sources area; accounting, business, and economics journals located in the Periodicals Department on the lower level of the Library; and online databases which are accessible on campus and from home. A librarian is always available to help with your information needs.
