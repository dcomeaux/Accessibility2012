The Library and Information Science collection consists of books, journals and databases to support the Palmer School of Library and Information Science. Use the menu on the left to link to electronic databases and journals. Print copies of library science reference books and library science journals are housed in the Business, Law and Information Science Sources area on the main floor of the library at C.W. Post. Circulating books supporting the Palmer School are located in the main stacks. Any of the books from the circulating collection at Post can be sent to any of the other campuses where Palmer courses are held. The Instructional Media Center houses additional material on school media and children's literature.

Reserve readings are available electronically, except for reserve books which are located at the Main Circulation desk. Books and articles from other libraries can be obtained through interlibrary loan. AskALibrarian! allows you chat or IM with a librarian about your reference questions or e-mail your questions and receive a response from a librarian, usually within 48 hours. Palmer School dissertations are on reserve.
