Library Competency Exam

Library Competency Exam Schedule:

In addition to the dates below, the exam is also given in some Freshmen English 1 classes. See your teacher.
DayDateTimeRoom DayDateTimeRoom
Tuesday Feb 21 12:30-1:00 LS 139 Friday Apr 06 11:00-11:30 HM 119*
WednesdayMar 07 12:30-1:00 LS 139 Friday Apr 20 12:30-1:00 HM 119*
Wednesday Mar 28 12:30-1:00 LS 139
Registration is not required for these examinations, but please note that seating is limited and given on a first-come, first-served basis. Students who arrive five minutes after the exam begins may not take the examinations. You must bring a picture ID and know your student ID number. Students without photo ID or their ID number will not be allowed to take the exam. If the exam has not been taken by the time you have earned 60 credits, you will be automatically enrolled to take the library workshop. Transfer students with an associate degree or 60 transfer credits need not take the exam or workshop. You may contact your counselor at the Academic & Career Planning office at (516) 299-2746 if you have any questions. ( * Please note that HM 119 is handicap accessible)

For information about the Oral Competency, contact (516) 299-2436
