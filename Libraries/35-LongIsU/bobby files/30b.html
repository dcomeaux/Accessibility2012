  [Welcome to the] [Periodicals Department]  

The B. Davis Schwartz Memorial Library receives more than 4,000 current print and microform periodicals, and over 49,300 electronic journals. Print and microform titles, both current and back years, are available in the Periodicals Department on the lower level of the Library.

[INLINE]

Electronic or online periodicals can be accessed from campus or from off-campus locations with a library barcode number.
 
[INLINE]

The "Find a Periodical" link also includes print and microform titles held in the campus libraries of LIU.
 
[INLINE]

Periodicals not available online or in the C.W. Post library periodical collection can be ordered by Interlibrary Loan.


You can find help using periodicals in our print, microform or online collections by the following methods:

[INLINE]

[INLINE]
