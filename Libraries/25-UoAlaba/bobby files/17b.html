      Reserve collections in the libraries consist of materials faculty members have designated for specific class assignments. Course reserves may be in print or electronic format.

      Search Course Reserves

      Instructions for Faculty and GTAs for Placing Items on Reserve

      PRINT RESERVES

      If an item is available in print, it will have a call number.
      Finding a Print Reserve Call Number

      Go to the appropriate library's circulation desk to request the course reserve item.
      Learn more about circulation periods and library fines

      In order to check out print reserve materials, you must show a University of Alabama Action card. Only three reserve items may be checked out at one time. All reserve items must be returned to the appropriate reserve desk. Reserve items may not be renewed.

      ELECTRONIC RESERVES

      All articles, tests, and class notes placed by faculty are offered electronically. Viewing E-Reserves may require the free Adobe Acrobat Reader.
