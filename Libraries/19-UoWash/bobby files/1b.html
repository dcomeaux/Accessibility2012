Events

[Suzzallo Reading Room]

Friends Annual Meeting
Thursday, Jan. 26; 6 p.m.
Petersen Room, Allen Library

Celebrate the achievements of the Friends of the Libraries, with the Dean of Libraries, Friends Board members and Student Scholarship Award Winners. A reception will follow the meeting. Pre-register by email at uwlibs@uw.edu or call 206-616-8397.

[Stephanie Coontz]

Blom Lecture with Stephanie Coontz
Thursday, Feb. 23; 7 p.m.
Odegaard Undergraduate Library 220

Stephanie Coontz teaches history and family studies at The Evergreen State College in Olympia. She also serves as Co-Chair and Director of Public Education at the Council on Contemporary Families. Her most recent book is A Strange Stirring: The Feminine Mystique and American Women at the Dawn of the 1960s. Pre-register with the UW Alumni Association at http://engage.washington.edu/site/Calendar?id=105442&view=Detail or by calling 206-543-0540.

[Rebecca Brown]

Literary Voices
Saturday, April 28; 6 p.m.
UW Club

Rebecca Brown was the first writer in residence at Richard Hugo House, co-founder of the Jack Straw Writers Program and now serves as the creative director of literature at Centrum in Port Townsend. Brown's best-known work is her novel The Gifts of the Body, winner of a Lambda Literary Award, which has been translated into seven languages. Tickets available January 30. Email uwlibs@uw.edu for more information.
