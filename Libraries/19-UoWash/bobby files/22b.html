A subject librarian is your personal guide to the services and collections of the University of Washington Libraries. Subject librarians are assigned to departments and programs and are responsible for purchasing library materials, providing reference help and consultations, and instructing UW students (both formally and informally) in the research tools and methods of the discipline.

If your subject does not appear here, please contact Linda Di Biase, Collection Development Librarian at ldibiase@u.washington.edu
