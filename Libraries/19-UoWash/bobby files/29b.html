Articles & Research Databases

Use databases to find citations (and sometimes full text) to articles and other materials. There are specialized databases for various subjects (see Resources by Subject and Electronic Journals).
Help! How Do I... Find Journal Articles?

Starting points for most subjects

  • Academic Search Complete (EBSCO) UW Restricted
    Scholarly and general interest sources in business, medicine, humanities, social sciences, and science and technology.
  • Web of Science UW Restricted
    Science, social sciences, and arts and humanities citation indexes.
