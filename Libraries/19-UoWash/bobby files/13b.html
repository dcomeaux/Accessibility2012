Connect from Off-Campus
to UW-Restricted Resources

Most of the databases, electronic journals and e-books available through the Libraries are restricted to UW faculty, students and staff. If you want to connect to a UW-restricted resource from outside of the UW campus network, you must log in through the UW Libraries off-campus proxy.

To connect to restricted resources using the proxy:

  1. Your browser should have cookies enabled.
     
  2. Click on the [off-campus access (log in)] button in the upper right corner of most Libraries webpages or go to http://offcampus.lib.washington.edu/.
     
  3. You will be prompted to enter your UW NetID.
     
  4. Once logged in, note that the "Off-Campus Access" button now says [off-campus access (logged in)] and your browser address bar includes "offcampus.lib" in the address.
     
  5. Browse to the restricted resource you would like to access.

Or watch the video to see how to log in:

Help

  • Use the forward and back buttons on your browser to move around
  • Do not use the home button, your favorites or bookmarks
  • If you fall out of the off-campus proxy space, and no longer see the special url or the "logged in" message, then click on the "Off-Campus Access" link again or the proxy bookmarklet to re-enter.
  • More off-campus access help and FAQ >

Proxy Bookmarklet

If you often do your research from off-campus, we recommend you install the proxy bookmarklet on your browser's bookmarks toolbar. The bookmarklet lets you log into the proxy without having to browse from the UW Libraries website.
