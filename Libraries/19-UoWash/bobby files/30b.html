Popular e-journal packages

  • JSTOR UW restricted
    Full-text back issues of hundreds of scholarly journals, some of which date back to the 1800's.
  • Project Muse UW restricted
    Current issues of more than 100 scholarly journals primarily in the humanities & social sciences.
  • Science Direct UW restricted
    Current issues of scholarly journals published primarily in the sciences.
