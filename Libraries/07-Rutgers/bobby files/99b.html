Hot new books in the Libraries' Recreational Reading collection

The Libraries are pleased to announce the following new titles now available for check out from the Recreational Reading collection, based in the Kilmer Library but available to students, staff, and faculty through out all Rutgers' campuses.

  • The Host / Stephenie Meyer
  • The Gunslinger (complete series) / Stephen King
  • Sing You Home / Jodi Picoult
  • Sepulchre / Kate Mosse
  • The Help / Kathryn Stockett
  • Midnight Sons / Debbie Macomber
  • The Girl Who Kicked the Hornet's Nest / Steig Larson
  • I Am America (And So Can You!) / Stephen Colbert
  • The confession / John Grisham
  • Perfect harmony / Nora Roberts
  • The Hunger Games / Suzanne Collins
  • The terrible Axe-Man of New Orleans / Rick Geary
  • Beautiful Jim Key : The lost history of the world's smartest horse / Mim Eichler Rivas
  • The postcard killers : a novel / James Patterson and Liza Marklund
  • The gathering storm / Robert Jordan and Brandon Sanderson
  • Dead or alive / Tom Clancy with Grant Blackwood
  • Lakeside cottage / Susan Wiggs
  • Earth (the book) : a visitor's guide to the human race / written and edited by Jon Stewart
  • House rules : a novel / Jodi Picoult
  • Wrecked / Carol Higgins Clark
  • Next to love : a novel / Ellen Feldman
  • Zero day : a novel / David Baldacci
  • The house / Danielle Steel
  • The book of fate / Brad Meltzer
  • The moonflower vine : a novel / Jetta Carleton

For a complete listing of all the books in the recreational reading collection, please see: www.tinyurl.com/kilmerrecread
