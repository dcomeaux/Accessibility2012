Media Materials - Booking Form

Use this form to book video and audio items for pickup and return at locations throughout the Rutgers University Libraries. The service is available only to Rutgers faculty/staff and graduate students, and also may be used to preview materials.

For more information about booking, please read the Media Materials Booking Request Service guidelines or Frequently Asked Questions. For help in choosing titles, search the Library Catalog or contact a Ask A Librarian.

Check out our service for Mediavision Cablecasts!

Have a question about an existing booking?
