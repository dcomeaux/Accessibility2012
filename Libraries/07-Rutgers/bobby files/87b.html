Welcome

The Sharon A. Fordham Multimedia Lab, based at the Douglass Library, is intended for use by members of the Rutgers University Community including students, faculty, administrators and staff to create multimedia projects and materials in support of the academic programs at Rutgers University.

Lab resources are available during the hours listed on the libraries website.
