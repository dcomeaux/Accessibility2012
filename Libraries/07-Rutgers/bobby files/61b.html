Scholarly Communication and Open Access: Research and Publication in Flux

This website is designed to inform you about how scholarly publishing is changing and how you can help direct that change. Scholarly Communication requires the collaboration of researchers, publishers, libraries, and archives. Economic and technological change is blurring the boundaries among the participants. Rising costs are creating barriers to access, but technology and our own ingenuity may enable us to transform the system to our benefit and the benefit of society. Rutgers is a land grant university committed to public service. The free flow of research and ideas is important to us.
