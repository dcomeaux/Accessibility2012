How Do I...
...find video or audio information?

To find video or audio material look in the Library Catalog.

  • In the Library Catalog choose Advanced Search.
    • Enter the word/phrase, author, title, and/or subject terms you wish to search.
    • Use the Search Limits on the lower section of the page to limit the format to visual or audio materials.
      • Select Visual Materials for the moving image collections.
      • Select Recorded Sound for audio collections.
  • Click on the Search button.
  • The results of your search will be displayed on a Search Results screen. Click on the title in any listing to view the complete details for that item, including locations, call numbers, and status (availability).

Book Video Titles for a Class

Rutgers faculty/staff and graduate students are also eligible to:

  • Request a Mediavision Cablecast for class viewing at RU-TV cabled locations.
  • Book video titles for a class by completing a Media Materials - Booking Form.

Booked videos (and other moving image materials) are available for pickup and return at the following Media Pickup/Return Locations:

  • Alexander Library (CAC)
  • Art Library, next to Murray Hall (CAC)
  • Camden - Robeson Library (Camden)
  • Chang Library (Cook)
  • Dana Library Media (Newark)
  • Douglass Library (Douglass)
  • Kilmer Library (Livingston)
  • LSM (Busch)
  • SMLR (Cook)

For more information please see Media Materials Booking Service or Media Materials Booking Frequently Asked Questions.
