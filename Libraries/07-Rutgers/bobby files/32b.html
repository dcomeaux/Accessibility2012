Alumni Library

Welcome Alumni!

Graduation certainly has its privileges, and one of them is continued access to the resources and services of the Rutgers University Libraries.

Through this site, you can search or browse the Libraries' catalog; find out how to get your Alumni borrowing card; get to current and historical news from and about Rutgers; search or browse a large collection of general and specialized databases; ask the libraries a question; or share your memories of the libraries or Rutgers.

We want this site to reflect your needs and interests. So if there's something else that you'd like to see here, please let us know!
