How Do I...
...contact a librarian?

You may want to contact a librarian to ask a reference question, make an appointment for in-depth research help, arrange a library instruction session for your class, or recommend a title be purchased for the collection.

The following links provide the information you need to get in touch with us:

  1. Reference Desk Phone Numbers
  1. Instructional Coordinators
  1. Collection Development / Subject Specialist Librarians (To recommend a purchase, or to make an appointment for research help, or to schedule an instruction or group session.)
  1. Directory of Libraries, Centers, and Departments

You may also contact us through our online Ask a Librarian service.
