Self-Services

Self Services are available during library building hours.

Copy Card Vending

Copy cards may be purchased for $1 (a $ .50 charge for a new copy card is included) from vending machines at the following libraries. More value can be added to a copy card (maximum $25) in denominations of $1, $5, $10, or $20 at the vending machines.

  • Alexander Library*
  • Art Library
  • Chang Science Library
  • Dana Library-Newark
  • Douglass Library*
  • Kilmer Library*
  • Library of Science & Medicine*
  • Mathematical Sciences Library
  • Robeson Library-Camden
  • Science & Engineering Resource Center
  • School of Management and Labor Relations

* Libraries with card vending machines that give receipts.

Copy cards are also available at the Library Annex.

Photocopying

Photocopiers are available at the Alexander Library, the Library of Science & Medicine, SERC, SMLR, Libraries Annex, Dana Library, and Robeson Library. Hard copies can be produced at other locations by using the scan to print functionality of our public scanning stations, described below. Black and white copies cost $ .10 per page.

All photocopying is paid for by copy cards, which can be purchased from copy card vending machines located throughout Rutgers University Libraries.

Self Service Scanning

Public scanning stations are available throughout the libraries. Some key features of these devices include: sending of scanned files to either email or USB drive, providing choice of black and white or color images, optical character recognition, cloud based scanning (to both Google Docs and the Rutgers based platform, Scarlet Docs), and scanning to print.

Rutgers affiliated patrons may scan for free by logging in with a NetID. Non-affiliated guests are charged $.10 per scan. Fee based scanning is paid for by copy cards, which can be purchased from copy card vending machines located throughout Rutgers University Libraries.

Networked Printing

Networked printing is available at the following libraries: Alexander Library, Art Library, Chang Science Library, Dana Library-Newark, Douglass Library, Kilmer Library, Library of Science & Medicine, Robeson Library-Camden, and School of Management and Labor Relations Library.

Black and white print jobs cost $ .10 per page. Color print jobs cost $ .25 per page. Prints may be paid for by copy cards, which can be purchased from copy card vending machines located throughout the Rutgers University Libraries. Students that have RU Express accounts can also use their cards to pay for printing.

Please note: Library computers are able to print to OIT labs within the building at the following libraries: Alexander, Douglass, Kilmer, LSM, and Dana.

Microform Printing

Microform equipment is available for use throughout Rutgers University Libraries except for the following libraries: Art Library, Mathematical Sciences Library, Science & Engineering Resource Center, and School of Management and Labor Relations Library.

Copies printed from microform cost $ .20 per page. All copies are paid for by copy cards, which can be purchased from copy card vending machines located throughout the Rutgers University Libraries.

Microform equipment is available for use throughout Rutgers University Libraries. See the "Services Offered by Library" table above for locations.

Document Printing and Binding/Theses and Dissertations

During hours of operation the Imaging Services office in the basement level of Alexander Library can bind paper booklets of up to 200 sheets in width by using a comb binder. Comb binding is a quick, easy way to give any document a perfect soft cover "book finish." The fee for this service is $5.00 per bound document. Imaging Services will print documents that require binding for a reduced fee of $.05 per page. All copyright restrictions apply.

Hard cover bindings are also available for between $30 and $50 per copy, (printing included), depending on the number of pages to be bound and type of paper used. For details on this service, please refer to the order form [PDF].

Scanning

Black and white, grayscale or color scanning is available at Alexander Library Imaging Services during hours of operation. Imaging Services staff will scan materials for you for $ .25 per page for sheet fed scans. Overhead book scanning or image editing adds a handling fee of $5.00 per 10 minutes of staff time (minimum of 10 minutes). PDF, JPEG, and TIF formats are available, with resolution up to 600 dpi. Imaging Services staff can run OCR, (optical character recognition), on a scanned file at the customer's request for an additional charge of $5.00 per 10 minutes. Scanned images can be saved to a floppy, CDROM, printed or emailed. Scanning services are available by appointment only.

Analog to DVD Reformatting

Imaging Services at Alexander Library offers an Analog to DVD reformatting service. A patron can bring a personal VHS tape or a 16mm film to the library, complete the request form [PDF] and return 5-7 working days later to retrieve the original analog format item and a new DVD copy. The patron will be notified when the reformatted copy is available. Payment for this service must be made at time the order is submitted in the form of cash or a personal check made out to Rutgers University.

By submitting an order the patron declares and warrants that all materials provided for conversion and/or duplication are not in violation of any copyright laws. The analog format materials submitted must be created by the patron, or the patron has the right to permit Rutgers University Libraries staff to perform duplication services, or the patron has the permission of the copyright owner to duplicate video materials and a written release form is provided.

Your Analog to DVD conversion will be handled with great care by a qualified technician with many years of experience. However, Rutgers University Libraries are not liable for any damages or losses of any kind to the original VHS tape or 16mm film provided by the patron.

The price for the Analog to DVD Reformatting Service is as follows:

  • VHS Tape: $15 fee for transfer and reformatting of material on a single VHS tape to a single DVD
  • 16mm Film: $20 fee for transfer and reformatting of material on a single 16mm film reel to a single DVD

If you require multiple copies of the DVD copy, please see the CD/DVD Duplication section for prices according to number of DVDs required. The Analog to DVD Reformatting Fee above will only pay for one DVD copy.

CD/DVD Duplication

Imaging Services at Alexander Library offers the use of a multi input CD/DVD duplicating computer and a specialized printer for printing directly onto discs. Because of copyright law, only material created by the requester is eligible for duplication. Imaging Services reserves the right to determine whether or not the duplication request is permissible under copyright law.

Examples of acceptable uses of this service include:

  • Students needing multiple copies of a CD for their presentations
  • Faculty members needing to distribute copies of presentations at conferences
  • Student organizations distributing copies of self made films of their events to members
  • Film students needing multiple copies of self made movies for their professional portfolios
  • Student musician needing to distribute copies of self made recordings for a class project

The prices are as follows for CD/DVD duplication:
