Important Notes about Library Hours

Circulation and reserve desks close 15 minutes before the Libraries' official closing time. Routine closing will begin 10 minutes before the building is closed and locked.

UNT Library Hours are subject to change due to weather or other unforeseen circumstances without notice. Always call ahead when traveling a long distance.
