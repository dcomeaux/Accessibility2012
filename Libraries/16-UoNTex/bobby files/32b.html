Audio Center changes

[Audio Center changes]

As of March 1st, the carrels in the Audio Center will no longer be accessible, in anticipation of a significant reorganization of Willis Library’s fourth floor.  In order to maintain our services:

Audio materials on course reserves will remain accessible via the digital system (https://audioreserves.library.unt.edu/) 
For those wishing to access the physical items (CDs and LPs) on reserve, we will change their status to a 24-hour circulation period (exceptions: non-circulating items and personal copy CDs) 
Patrons who want to access non-reserve audio materials that are not CDs (i.e. LPs, cassettes, etc.) will eventually be able to do so elsewhere on the fourth floor; until then, there will be no way to access those formats in the library (you can still check them out, though)
  Questions/concerns can be sent to andrew.justice@unt.edu  

Thanks in advance for your patience and flexibility as we go through these changes, and rest assured that when the reorganization is complete (probably sometime this summer), we anticipate a heightened service aesthetic for all of our patrons.
