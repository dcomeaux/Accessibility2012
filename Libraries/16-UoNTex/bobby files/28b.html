Library Research Workshop March 5th

Monday, March 05, 2012. 07:00 PM Monday, March 05, 2012. 08:00 PM Monday, March 05, 2012 from 07:00 pm to 08:00 pm Willis Library Learning Center (Room 136)

Library Research Workshop Undergraduates Workshops Workshop Graduate students

Contact: Douglas Campbell

Learn how to get started with your library research with this hands on workshop.

Need some help with starting your research? Need some help knowing how to find scholarly articles? Do you have some questions? Then this workshop is for you!

Come to this FREE workshop and get all your questions answered. Learn how to use databases, interlibrary loan, digital dissertations, and the library catalog to help you succeed.

To sign up for this workshop visit the Workshop Registration Form page.
