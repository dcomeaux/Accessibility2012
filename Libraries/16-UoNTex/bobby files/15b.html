Find Articles (Beta)

Try out our new search that lets you find articles in thousands of e-journals and databases.

[Summon Beta 1]

 

 Advanced Search

 

 

About This New Search Tool

 

  • Unified Index searches across 92% of Electronic Resources we subscribe to. See what's included.
  • Refine or expand your results by type, subject, date of publication, etc.
  • Expand your search beyond UNT's subscribed collections.
  • Integrate search results into your workflow using the Citation Generator and Bibliographic Export Tool.
  • Get citation counts for select search results
  • More fun inside... explore.
 

Notes for Off-Campus Users

Most off-campus access issues have already been resolved. Please use our "report a problem" form if you continually experience access issues using this new search feature from an off-campus location.

What's Next

 

The library website will get an updated look at the beginning of the Fall 2012 Semester.  At that time we will more fully integrate this search tool with the site's homepage, the catalog, and other services. Based on your feedback, we may make additional customizations to how this service functions.

 

Please Tell Us What You Think

 

We are really interested in what you have to say about this new tool, so tell us about your experience using it!
