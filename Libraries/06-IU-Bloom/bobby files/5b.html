Outages and Downtime

Information about IU Notices and Downtimes including IUCAT downtime.

This page lists the electronic journals and databases for which a report has been made that the title cannot be reached by users on the Bloomington campus.  Titles are listed in alphabetical order. 

 

Report a Problem


When a vendor of a collection of electronic journals reports problems in connecting to all or a large number of its journals (often several hundred titles), the problem is noted, but all of the titles in the collection cannot be listed individually.  Sample titles will be listed.  Search the name of the Collection to see all titles in the collection. 

Titles are removed as soon as the problem has been resolved.

 

 

ISSN   February 7, 2012

Due to a major overhaul of the ISSN database, the ISSN Portal will not be available from

Friday February 24 at 1:00 pm until Tuesday February 28 at 3:00 am EST.

 


OneSearch @ IU:

In addition to individual database titles for Bloomington, this page includes all reports of problems with links to databases searched in the OneSearch system on Subject pages in the library web site. 

 

The system is in the process of being upgraded.  Please report problems to comments@indiana.edu
