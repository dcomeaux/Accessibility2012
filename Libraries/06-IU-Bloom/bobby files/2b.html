Development and External Relations

The External Relations & Development Office seeks support for priority programs of IU Libraries by building positive relationships. We develop partnerships with individuals, corporations, foundations, and government agencies that share our goals for Indiana University, and we foster widespread support for IU Libraries among citizens of Indiana and the nation.
