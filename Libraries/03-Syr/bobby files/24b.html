Special Collections Research Center

[upper banner]

Welcome

The Special Collections Research Center (SCRC) maintains and provides access to distinguished collections of rare books, manuscripts, and other archival materials. Syracuse University students, faculty, staff, and other scholars have available to them more than 150,000 printed works and 2,000 archival collections, including important editions, manuscripts, documents, letters, diaries, drawings, photographs, and memorabilia.
