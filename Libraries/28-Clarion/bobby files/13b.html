Charles L. Suhr Library

Venango Campus
Clarion University of Pennsylvania
1801 West First Street
Oil City, PA  16301
Circulation Desk: 814-393-1242
or 814-676-6591, ext. 1242
[Suhr1]

 Suhr Library,  located on Venango Campus in Oil City, supports bachelor's and associate degree programs and General Education courses that are taught at Venango Campus and at other campuses through distance education. Two librarians, two full-time library technicians, and student assistants provide library service to students and faculty at this branch campus and at distance education locations.

[SuhrReadingCorner]

Suhr Library contains 29,000 volumes and has 175 periodical subscriptions as well as access to over 7,000 full-text journals via the World Wide Web. Suhr's print collections are supplemented by those of Carlson Library on Clarion Campus. Both collections are cataloged in one on-line catalog (PILOT). The libraries are able to share their resources through a courier service that runs between the campuses twice weekly, and through electronic document delivery services.

[Suhr in Use]

Print resources at Suhr Library have been greatly enhanced through subscriptions to indexing and full-text databases which are available through the World Wide Web. The library provides eight public access computer terminals so that students can use these and other electronic resources.
