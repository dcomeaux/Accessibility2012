Research Resources

[Clarion University Secondary Image 020]

◊ Databases A-Z - Comprehensive list of the periodical, e-book, and other informational databases subscribed to by the CU Libraries.

◊ E-Book Collections - Online, electronic (E-Book) collections and titles that the Libraries subscribe to, as well as freely available collections on the Internet. You will encounter links to many of the subscribed items when searching the PILOT Online Catalog.

◊ E-Media - Some Library materials are available in streaming video format.

The E-Media area is under construction.

◊ Eagle Gold Search - Search the Libraries' databases and/or catalog through one common search interface!

◊ Google Scholar - Search the web for scholarly research resources such as peer-reviewed articles/papers, theses, etc. When searching from on-campus, you'll be able to link directly to many full-text articles held by Clarion University (no log in required). Off-campus users must set their Google Scholar preferences to the Clarion University Libraries.

◊ Journals/Magazines A-Z - Determine whether or not the Libraries have immediate access to any given periodical title.

◊ Off-Campus Login Information - Don't know how to log in to Library resources? We can help!

◊ PILOT Online Catalog - Identify books, e-books, media, critically reviewed web sites, and online U.S. government publications in the Libraries' collections.

◊ WorldCat - Comprehensive, up-to-date bibliographic database including millions of records from more than 60,000 libraries. Fast, one-stop searching for citations to books, media, chapters, etc., in over 400 languages. Searches may be global or limited to resources owned by Clarion.
