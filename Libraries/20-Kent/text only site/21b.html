Tax Resources

Kent State librarians recommend:

Forms, Publications, and Tools

You need to connect to the Kent State VPN to access the research databases listed on this page. Click this button for instructions. OhioLINK Authentication users can ignore this alert. City of Kent Division of Income Tax This page includes City of Kent income tax forms and contact information for the Division of Income Tax. Free Tax Return Preparation For You by Volunteers The IRS Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) Program or the Tax Counseling for the Elderly (TCE) Program offer free tax assistance for those who qualify. IRS: Forms and Publications The Internal Revenue Service site is the official source for federal tax forms and publications. A variety of tax resources are available as well. IRS: Free File Free File is online tax preparation and electronic filing through a partnership agreement between the IRS and the Free File Alliance, LLC. Eligible tax payers can now file their taxes electronically for free. State of Ohio Tax Forms In addition to official forms, the Ohio Department of Taxation site provides a number of state tax resources.

Guides to Online Tax Resources

You need to connect to the Kent State VPN to access the research databases listed on this page. Click this button for instructions. OhioLINK Authentication users can ignore this alert. Essential Links to Taxes The Taxes page of Essential Links provides access to quite a few good tax sites. WorldWideWeb Tax This site offers access to state and federal forms, other online resources. WorldWideWeb Tax is also an authorized IRS e-file provider.

FAQ's, Tips, and General Information

You need to connect to the Kent State VPN to access the research databases listed on this page. Click this button for instructions. OhioLINK Authentication users can ignore this alert. IRS: Frequently Asked Questions and Answers The IRS offers this well-organized page of answers to common tax questions. The FAQ's can be browsed by category or searched by keyword. IRS: Tax Information for Business This page links businesses to information related to federal business taxes. IRS: Tax Information for Individuals This page offers information for individual taxpayers, including income tax, tax credits, and filing status. Ohio Department of Taxation Business Links This page links you to some of the government services and information you need as a business taxpayer of the State of Ohio. Ohio Department of Taxation Individual Links This page links you to some of the government services and information you need as a taxpayer of the State of Ohio. Yahoo! Tax Center This site offers a directory of online resources as well as tools and tips.
