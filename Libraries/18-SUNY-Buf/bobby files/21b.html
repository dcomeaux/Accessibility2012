Support Our Libraries

Gifts Enhance the UB Libraries Vision for the 21st Century

The University at Buffalo Libraries are central to the University's mission of teaching, research and service.Â  Gifts made to the UB Libraries have a significant impact on the innovative services the Libraries offer and help to ensure that their critical needs are addressed. Donations help the library acquire new resources, preserve existing materials, and provide cutting-edge technologies that offer multiple formats and improved access to information.

You can make an immediate impact by make a donation to the UB Libraries through the Giving to UB website. UB’s intellectual growth and the strength of each and every school, faculty member and student, all depend on the vitality of the Libraries.

For more information about giving to the University Libraries or to include the Libraries in your estate planning, please contact our development office at (716) 645-0850 or email at library@buffalo.edu.
