Interlibrary Loan, Document Express, Annex

Use your ILLiad account to request materials from other libraries, intercampus delivery and Annex requests. These services are available to faculty, staff and students of the University at Buffalo ONLY.

  • ILLiad Requests - Logon

Interlibrary Loan obtains books, articles and other materials not owned by the UB Libraries from other institutions for current members of the UB community.

Document Express retrieves books/media or articles from a library located on the campus opposite to your department.

Annex Requests: For current members of the UB community, materials in the Annex may be requested through your ILLiad account.Â If you are NOT a current UB student, faculty or staff member, you may request electronic delivery of journal articles and book chapters via an ILLiad Non-UB user account for a fee. To set up an account please contact the Information Delivery Office at 829-3900, ext. 116 or via email at hslids@buffalo.edu.
