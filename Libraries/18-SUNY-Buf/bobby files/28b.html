Forms A-Z


[form icon]   Annex Delivery for UB Community - Book
UB ONLY - Use this form if you are a current UB student, faculty or staff member and after consulting the BISON Catalog, you have determined that a book you want is held in the Libraries Annex.
[form icon]   Annex Delivery for UB Community - Journal Article or Book Chapter
UB ONLY - Use this form if you are a current UB student, faculty or staff member and after consulting the BISON Catalog, you have determined that an article or book chapter you want is held in the Libraries Annex.
[form icon]   Annex Delivery for non-UB patrons
Use this form if you are NOT a current UB student, faculty or staff member and after consulting the BISON Catalog, you have determined the material you want is held in the Libraries Annex.
[form icon]   Assignment Alert
This form is for all University at Buffalo faculty and teaching assistants to inform University librarians of current research assignments which require library use.
[form icon]   BISON Catalog Problem
Use this form to notify the Catalog Department of recommended corrections or modifications to the BISON Catalog.
[form icon]   Contact Us
We encourage you to send us your comments and concerns regarding the University at Buffalo Libraries web site or resources.
[form icon]   Course Reserve Submission
You are an instructor and want to put books, articles & other materials on reserve for your students.
[form icon]   Disability Services
You are registered with the UB Office of Disability Services and need article photocopying or book retrieval.
[form icon]   Electronic Journal Problem Report Form
Please complete the form below to report any technical difficulties that you experience when accessing electronic journals.
[form icon]   Electronic Journal Request Form
You want to recommend that the UB Libraries purchase an electronic journal.
[form icon]   General Catalog Services
To request items noted in the BISON Catalog as "In Process," "On Order," "Incomplete Issues," " At Bindery," or "In Preservation".
[form icon]   In Storage
To request items noted in the BISON Catalog as "In Storage," "Storage Item Ask at HSL Circulation Desk," "Arch/Plan Storage," or "In Storage Ask at Music Library".
[form icon]   Interlibrary Loan
UB ONLY - To request a book, journal article, or other item that is not listed in the BISON Catalog.
[form icon]   Library Instruction - General - Graduate Level
You are an instructor and want to request a library instruction session for a graduate classes in all relevant academic areas.
[form icon]   Library Instruction - General - Undergraduate Level
You are an instructor and want to request a library instruction session for an undergraduate classes in all relevant academic areas.
[form icon]   Library Instruction for High School Teachers & Librarians
You are a high school teacher or librarian and want to request a library instruction session.
[form icon]   Library Instruction - Health Sciences Library
To request a course-related instruction session.
[form icon]   Online Resources Technical Problems
To report any technical difficulties that you experience when accessing library online resources.
[form icon]   Purchase Recommendation
You want to recommend that the UB Libraries purchase a book or other material.
[form icon]   Reference Question
You are a member of the UB community (or have a special interest in our collections) and want to ask a question.
[form icon]   Research Consultation
You are a member of the UB community and want to ask a make a research consultation appointment.
[form icon]   Special Collections
To request access to items in Special Collections, (Poetry, Archives, or Rare Books).
[form icon]   Study Carrel Application for Lockwood Library
You are faculty, professional staff, Ph.D. candidates and graduate students from the College of Arts & Sciences, Graduate School of Education, and the Schools of Architecture and Planning, Management or Social Work and would like to apply for a study carrel in the Lockwood library
[form icon]   UB Document Express
UB ONLY - You are on the North or South campus and the item you need is listed in the BISON Catalog as being on the other campus.
[form icon]   UB Document Express for Roswell
You are at Roswell Park (RPCI) and you have determined that a journal article you want is held in a UB library.
[form icon]   UB Institutional Repository Project Request Form
Request projects to be included in the University at Buffalo Institutional Repository.
