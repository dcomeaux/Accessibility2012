Ask a Librarian

Walk-in reference service is available during most hours of library operation.    Alternatives include:

[chat] Chat
Get instant answers by using our Instant Message chat services.
 
[text] Text
Send your text reference question to 716-304-0002.
 
[email] Email
We provide reference assistance via e-mail for current students, faculty & staff of the University at Buffalo.
   
[phone] Phone
We provide reference assistance by phone for current students, faculty & staff of the University at Buffalo.
   
[in-person] In-Person
Get a complete list of all Subject Librarians and contact them regarding your question.
