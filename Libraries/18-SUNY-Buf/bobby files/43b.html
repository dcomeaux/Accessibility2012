About the Library

[baird hall]Lockwood Memorial Library, located on the University at Buffalo North Campus, features services, collections and librarians supporting students and faculty in Arts & Sciences, Education, Management and Social Work. The library is open to the UB and Western New York communities, and includes a government documents collection and a Cybrary site for computer access. Check our schedule of hours to see when the library is open. Download or view an audio tour of Lockwood Memorial Library.

Events & Workshops

The Libraries offer workshops throughout the year.  These workshops cover essentials about research using the Libraries Web site, library catalogs, article databases, government information, EndNote bibliographic management software, and more.

See the Libraries Events & Workshops website for a complete listing.
