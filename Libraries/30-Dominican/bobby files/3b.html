Rosary College of Arts & Sciences
Offering more than 50 majors and minors along with dual-degree or certificate programs Dominican's Rosary College of Arts & Sciences will help you achieve your professional or personal goals. In keeping with Dominican University’s mission of preparing students to pursue truth, give compassionate service and participate in the creation of a more just and humane world, the Rosary College of Arts and Sciences strives to embody a community of learners supportive of each learner’s development and committed to using their talents to make a positive contribution to the world.
