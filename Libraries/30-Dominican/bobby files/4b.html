Undergraduate Studies
  • More than 50 majors and several pre-professional programs
  • A strong academic base of core curriculum and liberal arts seminars
  • Service learning opportunities in and out of the classroom
  • The average class size is 17 students, which is small enough for focused, personalized attention
  • Research opportunities for independent projects or work in collaboration with a faculty member
  • Dual degree (undergraduate/graduate) possibilities



Graduate Studies
  • More than 30 amazing possibilities for graduate studies.
  • Professors who combine strong academic credentials with real-world experience and professional connections
  • Full time and part time schedules and many evening classes
  • Multiple locations
  • Endorsement and certification programs
  • Combined/Dual Graduate Degree Programs with McCormick Theological Seminary (MDiv); Public History (MA) with Loyola University Chicago; Music History (MM) in Music History with Northwestern University School of Music
