All libraries provide both coin- and card-operated black-and-white copiers for patron use. Hagerty Library also provides access to a color copier, and to a Microfilm Reader/Printer.

Costs and Payment Methods

Cost per page:

  • Black-and-white: $.10 - $.20
  • Color: $.50 - $1.00

Payment methods:

  • Cash -- at least one copier at each library accepts coins.
  • Dragon Card -- Value ($1, $5, $10, or $20) can be added to Dragon Cards at these locations:
    • Hagerty Library, 1st floor
    • Hahnemann Library, 24-hour room, 2nd floor
    • Queen Lane Library, Copy room
    • DragonCard offices in Creese Center, Suite 1608 in the New College Building, or Room 101 at Queen Lane.

Hagerty Library

Copiers:

First Floor
Black and White copiers: Coin-operated copiers and DragonCard-operated copiers -- in and outside of Room 138 (near the public printer).

Coin-operated Multi-function copier/scanner: near reference-area printer. Black and white and color copying, priced as follows:

  8-1/2 x 11 11 x 17
Black & White $.10 $.20
Color $.50 $1.00

Can also be used as scanner at no charge; scanned documents can be emailed to yourself or others; additional "full-service" scanners are available on the Lower Level and at the top of the stairs on the Second Floor.

Second Floor
Black and white copier, DragonCard-operated, at top of stairs.

Lower Level
Black and white copier, DragonCard-operated and coin-operated, in study area outside of room L33 (next to the public printer).

Microfilm Readers/Printers:

First Floor
Microfilm Reader/Scanners are available at the Circulation Desk. Scanners print to public printers located in First Floor computer area or outside of L33 on Lower Level.

Dragon Cards are used to release the print job at the printer; those without Dragon Cards may pay in cash at the Circulation Desk.
