Drexel Libraries Hours

Choose calendar to view:

Bookmark Café: Open 24/7
IM Reference (Hagerty): Monday – Thursday, 8:30am – 10pm; Friday, 8:30am – 5pm; Saturday, noon – 6pm; Sunday, noon – 10pm
Circulation Desk (Hagerty): Open when Hagerty Library is open.
Call Hagerty Library toll-free: 1-888-278-8825

Library Hours may vary around University-wide holidays, prior to and during exam periods, and during term breaks (see Academic Calendars). Please consult individual library calendars for actual hours. Please note that on University-wide holidays and other federal holidays when the Libraries are open, weekend access policies apply. University-wide holidays include:

  • New Year's Day
  • Martin Luther King, Jr.'s Birthday
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Columbus Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Thanksgiving Friday
  • Christmas Day

Hours may also vary on Easter Sunday and Christmas Eve.
