Factiva

Global news and business information service that combines the content sets of Dow Jones Interactive and Reuters Business Briefing. ncludes The Wall Street Journal, the Financial Times?, Dow Jones and Reuters newswires and the Associated Press, as well as Reuters Fundamentals, D&B?, and Bureau van Dijk company profiles. More than 9,000 sources from 152 countries in 22 languages, including more than 120 continuously updated newswires. And, more than 900 sources are available on or before the date of publication. Subjects include International Business, Taxation, Finance, Company/Industry Research.
Faculty of 1000 Biology current

an online research service that highlights and reviews the most interesting papers published in the biological sciences, based on the recommendations of a faculty of well over 2300 selected leading researchers ("Faculty Members")
Fashion Snoops

Forecasting and trend analysis for the fashion industry
Faulkner Advisory for IT Studies (FAITS) Dates vary

Provides IT company and process information, and offers several different report types.
Faulkner Security Management Practices Current

Reports data monthly covering facility security, IT and network security, financial and legal security, business continuity, risk and crisis management, personnel security, and more.
Federal Register 1995-current

Includes U.S. federal agency announcements and information, such as presidential documents, agency meetings, grant opportunities, and proposed federal regulations. A new edition of the Federal Register is published every business day.
Film Literature Index 1976-2001

Indexes 150 film and television periodicals from 30 countries cover-to-cover and 200 other periodicals selectively for articles on film and television.
Fire Research Information Services (FRIS) Dates vary

Free resource featuring two extensive document collections in fire research, through FIREDOC and Building & Fire Research Laboratory Publications.
Firearms Research 2003-2008

A database of social science, criminology, law reviews, medical and public health research concerning firearms.
First Search Dates vary

Provides searching platform for a number of periodical databases covering the Sciences, Technology, Engineering, Arts & Humanities, and Social Sciences.
FirstGov.gov Dates vary

Search engine for government information, with more than 186 million web pages from federal and state governments, the District of Columbia and U.S. territories.
Food Science & Technology Abstracts (FSTA) 1990-current

Abstracts to literature relating to food science and technology, prepared by subject specialists.
Foreign Doctoral Dissertations Database Dates vary

Searchable database of foreign doctoral dissertation abstracts.
Foreign Law Guide Current

A tool for finding sources of non-U.S. law. Provides citations to official versions of legislation for more than 190 jurisdictions, and to English language translations where available, as well as to secondary sources in English.
Fundamental Physical Constants - Searchable Bibliography on the Constants Dates vary

Contains citations for theoretical and experimental publications relevant to the fundamental constants and closely related precision measurements.
