Printing from Computers in the Libraries

 

All Library computers are connected to a printing network.

 

PC Printing by Location:

 

Hagerty Library
Hahnemann Library
Queen Lane Library
Laptop Drivers

Hagerty Library

    • Print your document selecting Library_BlackandWhite_Printer on LXU-UNIPRINT as your printer.
    • Name the Print job (and add a password if you want to secure it), then select "OK".
    • Go to a DragonPrint Station.
    • Swipe DragonCard in card reader.
    - Select your print job. Verify the number of pages and cost of the job at the bottom of the screen.
    - Select "PRINT".
    - Select "LOG OFF" so no one else accidentally charges print jobs using your money.

If you do not have a DragonCard you can pay to release your print jobs at the Circulation Desk.

Printing Costs:

With a DragonCard - B&W: 10¢ per page, 8¢ per side for double-sided printing.

Without a DragonCard - B&W: 25¢ per page.

Printer Locations:

- First Floor: Multiple Printers located along the right side of the reference computer hubs and next to the public stations.
- Basement: 1 B&W Printer located near L33.

DragonCard Loading Locations:

- DragonCard Charge Station: Located between the printers on the first floor.
- Creese Student Center Room 124 Dragon Card Main Office.

- Online at the DragonCard Website

Funds added at the DragonCard office or online are not instantly available. Fund Added using a charge station will be immediately available.

Hahnemann Library

    • Print your document selecting CCLab B&W, CCRef B&W and CCLRC B&W for Black and White or CC Color for Color print jobs.
    • Name the Print job (and add a password if you want to secure it), then select "OK".
    • Go to a DragonPrint Station(Note: Check which printer it corresponds to. You can only release color print jobs downstairs).
    • Swipe DragonCard in card reader.
    - Select your print job. Verify the number of pages and cost of the job at the bottom of the screen.
    - Select "PRINT".
    - Select "LOG OFF" so no one else accidentally charges print jobs using your money.

Printing Costs:

B&W: 10¢ per page, 8¢ per side for double-sided printing.

Color: 25¢ per page, 20¢ per side for double-sided printing

Printer Locations:

- First Floor: 2 B&W printers and the Color printer are located in the corner between the systems office and the admin
office.
- Second Floor: 3 B&W printers, one located across from the Circulation Desk, the other Against the glass wall adjoining
the 24 Hour Quiet Room, and the last in the computer area of the 24 HOUR Quiet Room.

DragonCard Loading Locations:

- DragonCard Charge Station: Located in the 24 HOUR Quiet Room on the 2nd floor.
- New College Building Suite 1608 Center City Dragon Card Office.

- Online at the DragonCard Website

Funds added at the DragonCard office or online are not instantly available. Fund Added using a charge station will be immediately available.

Printer Locations:

- Ground Floor: 1 B&W printer located in the Computer Lab.

DragonCard Loading Locations:
