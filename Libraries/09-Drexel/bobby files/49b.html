Borrowing from Other Libraries

Use E-Z Borrow or Interlibrary Loan (ILLiad) services to get books, articles, and other materials not owned by Drexel University Libraries, or not currently available. E-Z Borrow is only available to Drexel students, faculty and staff. The Interlibrary Loan service is free to University faculty, staff, students, residents and fellows, and available for a fee to Tenet Hahnemann Hospital staff.   Before placing a request in E-ZBorrow or ILLiad, please check online catalog. You may request a hold on the book. Books currently checked out will be held for pickup upon return. You will be notified by email when book is available for pickup.    

Borrow Books via E-Z Borrow

Document Delivery (Copy Service)

The Libraries will provide delivery of articles from journals in our collections.
  • Requests should be placed via ILLiad (above).
  • At all libraries, this service is available to faculty. At the Hahnemann and Queen Lane Libraries, it is also offered to students and University staff for a fee of $3 per article requested.

 

Request/Hold Books via Library Catalog

Use the Request choice in the library catalog to have a book owned by a Drexel University Library held for pickup at that library, or another Drexel Library.
  • Books currently checked out will be held for pickup upon return - use E-Z Borrow if needed quickly.
  • You will be notified by email when book is available for pickup.
