Name Description
Handbook of Chemistry & Physics 85th edition , 2004-2005

Online equivalent of the 84rd edition of this print classic.
HAPI (Health and Psychosocial Instruments) 1985-current

Provides information on measurement instruments (tests, questionnaires, etc.)
Health Information for International Travel 2008 Current

The Yellow Book is published every two years by CDC as a reference for those who advise international travelers of health risks.
Health Technology Assessment 2001 - Present

The HTA database brings together details of completed and ongoing health technology assessments (studies of the medical, social, ethical and economic implications of healthcare interventions) from around the world. The aim of the database is to improve the quality and cost-effectiveness of health care. The HTA database is produced by the Centre for Reviews and Dissemination (CRD) at the University of York, UK, using information obtained from members of INAHTA and other health technology assessment organizations.
HealthSTAR 1975-current

The HealthSTAR database contains citations to the published literature on health services, technology, administration and research. In addition to journal article citations and abstracts, HealthSTAR includes citations to meeting abstracts, technical reports and monographs
Hein Online 1789 - Current

Full text access to Law Reviews and Journals, including an extensive retrospective collection.
Historical Abstracts 1450-current

Provides coverage of over 1,700 journal titles published worldwide and serves as a complete reference guide to the history of the world from 1450 to the present (excluding the United States and Canada, which are covered in America: History and Life).
HIV Protease Database Current

Archive of experimentally determined 3-D structures of HIV and SIV Proteases from Protein Data Bank (PDB).
HLAS Online 1936-current

The Handbook of Latin American Studies is a selective annotated bibliography of scholarly works on Latin America.
Hoover's (via Lexis Nexis Academic) Current

Company, industry, and market intelligence covering small to large public and private companies worldwide. Subjects include Company/industry research. Hoover's is no longer available to Drexel patrons after November 30, 2010. Hoover's information is fully accessible through Lexis Nexis Academic: Once you are in the database, you can click on "Companies" from the list along the left side of the screen. In the Companies section, click on "Company Profiles" and on the next screen you can specifically choose Hoovers.
Hospitality and Tourism Complete

Scholarly research and industry news relating to all areas of hospitality and tourism.
Household Products Database Current

This database links over 5,000 consumer brands to health effects from Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) and allows scientists and consumers to research products based on chemical ingredients. With links to PubMED and TOXNET.
HSTAT (Health Services/Technology Assessment Text) Current

HSTAT (Health Services/Technology Assessment Text) is a searchable collection of full-text clinical practice guidelines, technology assessments and health information. Provided by the National Library Medicine and posted on its web site.
Human Mitochondrial Protein Database Current

Provides comprehensive data on mitochondrial and human nuclear encoded proteins involved in mitochondrial biogenesis and function.
Humanities & Social Sciences Index Retrospective 1907-1984

Provides full text plus abstracts and bibliographic indexing of major scholarly sources in the humanities and social sciences, as well as numerous lesser-known specialized magazines.
