LOUIS provides 61% of LSU Libraries' full-text access to electronic titles. LOUIS' 72 databases also provide indexing to help patrons find the information they need.

Percentages are approximate.

LSU Libraries pays for only 32% of its full-text electronic titles without the aid of LOUIS.

Percentages are approximate.

Open access publications can provide some relief for losses. However, Only 7% of LSU Libraries' full-text electronic titles are available as free open access resources.
