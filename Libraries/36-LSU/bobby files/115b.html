Welcome to LSU Libraries Special Collections

Special Collections has many resources available to researchers both at this site and in the reading room in Hill Memorial Library. The following information is here to help you get started using Special Collections at LSU.

Find out more about the materials housed in Hill by exploring the five major collecting areas:

  • The Louisiana and Lower Mississippi Valley Collections (LLMVC)
  • The Rare Book Collection
  • The E.A. McIlhenny Natural History Collection
  • Political Papers
  • The University Archives

View the policies and procedures in Use of the Collections

If you plan to visit Hill Memorial Library, please view the Location and Hours information.
