Ask a Librarian Reference Desks

University Library reference staff based on the Ann Arbor campus can answer your questions via phone or in person during specific service hours at each of our campus libraries. If you are unsure of which of these libraries can best meet your needs, contact the Hatcher Graduate Library Information and Reference Center at (734) 764-9373. Reference staff at this location can direct you to the most relevant reference desk and/or library.

For building locations, see the central and medical campus map and the north campus map.
