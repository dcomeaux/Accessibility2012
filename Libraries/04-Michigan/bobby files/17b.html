Giving to MLibrary

Staff Listing

The University Library at the University of Michigan is one of the outstanding research libraries in the country. Composed of 19 individual libraries on the Ann Arbor campus, the University Library has played a key role in shaping the academic excellence of the University of Michigan.

The quality of its collections and services, close collaboration between librarians and faculty, and the development of important and creative initiatives that have been emulated by many other institutions have positioned the Library as a true leader.

This is an exciting time for the University Library, and we look forward to your support in helping us keep the University of Michigan a Leader and Best!
