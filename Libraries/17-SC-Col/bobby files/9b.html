Irvin Department of Rare Books & Special Collections
 


[Earnest F. Hollings rear facade]
[Follow us on FaceBook!][Follow us on Twitter!]
  The Irvin Department of Rare Books & Special Collections is located in the Ernest F. Hollings Special Collections Library, at the south side of the main Thomas Cooper Library. Enter by the bridge at the opposite end of the main floor from the Thomas Cooper entrance.

Information on using the collections, department staff, detailed catalog descriptions of individual items, and broader collection descriptions, are linked at the left of this page.

Hours: Monday - Friday 8:30AM - 5:00PM
Phone: (803) 777-3847
Email: tclrare@mailbox.sc.edu
