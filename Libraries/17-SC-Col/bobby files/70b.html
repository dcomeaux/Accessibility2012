The Collection Development Department coordinates the work of librarians and teaching faculty in building library collections which strongly support the research and teaching programs of the University.

The University budget for 2009-2010 year includes money to cover inflation for library subscriptions. No cancelation of titles will be required for this fiscal year. If you have questions about subscriptions, please contact Gary Geer.
