Featured Item:


Fox Movietone News Story 51-15: The War in Italy: Eighth Army Front

Before the 99th became famous for its Red Tail P-51 Mustangs it flew the Curtiss P-40 against German ground targets. This edited film shows members of the unit preparing for a raid against enemy positions at Orsogna, Italy. Bishop J. A. Gregg of the A.M.E. Church brings good wishes from the Homefront.
