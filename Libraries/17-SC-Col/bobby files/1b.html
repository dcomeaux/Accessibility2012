Published Materials Division

at the South Caroliniana Library

Hours of Published Materials Division:
Monday through Friday:
8:30 AM to 5:00 PM

Saturday:
9:00 to 1:00 PM

[seach the library catalog]
[join our facebook group]
Published Materials (formerly Books Division) collects and preserves publications written by or about the people, places, and culture of the Palmetto State.

Holdings include books, maps, pamphlets, S.C. newspapers, sheet music, USC dissertations and theses, magazines and other periodicals, and vertical files of clippings and other ephemera. Consult the online subject guides for help locating these and other types of materials in the library catalog.

Some materials may be housed at the Library Annex. Check the library catalog or contact Published Materials Division before a visit. For more information, call (803) 777-3132 or contact Lorrey McClure.
