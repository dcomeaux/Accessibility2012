Technical Services Department
      

Welcome to the Technical Services Department of the University Libraries. The Technical Services Department is one of two academic departments in the  University Library college; the other is Public Services.

The Technical Services Department consists of faculty members in the Cataloging, Acquisitions, and Special Collections Units of the Libraries. In addition, the coordination of collection management functions resides in the Department.
