CML News

The fall semester kept us very busy with 600 patrons looking for curriculum materials and assistance at our Reference Desk.
We taught seventy classes, attended by approximately 1,700 students.  The Grand Challenge course, Education and Social Justice, was hosted and taught for the first time in the CML. We enjoyed having Dr. Terry Deeney with the EDC 566 graduate students and the children in the Reading Clinic on Tuesday afternoons in the CML.

More information about the CML is available in our newsletter.
