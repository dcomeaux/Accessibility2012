IM URILibrarian
Instant Messaging & Text Reference Assistance

The URI Libraries provide reference assistance using two instant messaging programs. Once you ask a question, the librarian can guide you in your research or help you find quickly-located factual information.

This service operates the same hours as the Reference Desk. You can reach us on AIM and Yahoo! using the screen name URILibrarian, or by using the box below during our regular Reference hours.

Click here to open the chat in a new window.

Want to send us a text message instead? Send a message to 265010 and start your message with URILibrarian: to reach us. For example, URILibrarian:[Your message here].

If you are unable to reach a librarian during the hours of service, or if you need assistance at another time, please stop by or call one of the URI Reference Desks.

Please note that your online reference interaction may be logged for quality assurance.
