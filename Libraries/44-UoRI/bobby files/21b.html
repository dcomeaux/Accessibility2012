Subject Selectors

The following is a list of subject selectors by departmental responsibility.

Subject selectors are responsible for selecting books and other materials for the University Library in Kingston. Subject selectors also act as liaisons to the various departments on the Kingston campus.

These responsibilities at URI’s College of Continuing Education Library in Providence are held by Joanna Burkhardt, Head Librarian. Joanna can be reached at (401) 277-5133 or by e-mail at jburkhardt@uri.edu.

Materials for the Pell Marine Science Library are selected by Coordinator of Library Services Joyce Downey. For more information, please call Joyce at (401) 874-6544 or e-mail her at jdowney@gso.uri.edu.

Instruction
Subject Selectors who are are available to provide course-related instruction sessions and in-depth, one-on-one research consultations are indicated by an * following their names below.
