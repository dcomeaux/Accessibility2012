User Guides

Library Guides are short guides to the University Libraries’ services and resources. The Guides are organized into the following categories:

General Guides
General Guides present the basics of finding your way around the library and getting started with your research.

Subject Guides
Subject Guides offer assistance in identifying information sources to answer frequently-asked questions and serve as a starting point for researching popular subjects.

Assignment Guides
Assignment Guides are online guides to library sources designed to assist students currently working on assignments in particular courses.
