Library Services

University of Texas at Austin library resources are accessed onsite and online annually by millions of users in Austin, around the state, and in over 100 foreign countries
Borrowing Borrowing, renewing, holding, recalling, returning library materials - Appealing fines - Confidentiality of Library Records - Summer library privileges - TexShare Card Program
Ask a Librarian Email or Chat with a Librarian - Call or visit a reference desk
Reserves Finding hard copy and electronic reserve materials - Placing materials on reserve
Interlibrary Services Interlibrary loan request - Location and hours of services - Research Libraries Cooperative Program - Big 12 Plus Library Consortium - Document Express
Copying/Printing Copyright restrictions - Copy cards - Copier/Printer locations - Price list
Study Spaces Places to study in groups - Places to study alone - Carrels - Lockers
Instruction Online tutorials - Library Classes & Citation Management workshops to introduce you research resources and services - Faculty services including requesting library instruction for your class
Media Services Collections of Audiovisual materials - Media services for faculty and for visitors
Request a Purchase Request that the library purchase a book, journal, musical score, CD, DVD or any other material
UT Digital Repository The UT Digital Repository accepts, archives, and provides access to the scholarly and research works of UT, as well as works that reflect the intellectual and service environment of the campus.
Digital Services Digitization Center - Metadata services - Protecting your intellectual property - Current and past digital projects
