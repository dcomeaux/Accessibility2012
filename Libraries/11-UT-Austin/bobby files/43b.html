What am I searching?
  • Articles searches Academic Search Complete, a multi-disciplinary database of articles from scholarly journals, magazines and newspapers. Try searching our other databases as well.
  • Books does a keyword search of the Library Catalog which lists all items owned in the University of Texas Libraries.
  • CDs/DVDs does a keyword search limited to CDs and DVDs in the Library Catalog, which lists all items owned in the University of Texas Libraries.
  • Background Information searches Gale Virtual Reference Library, a database of encyclopedias, including scholarly encyclopedias, from across disciplines.
