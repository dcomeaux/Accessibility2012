NoodleBib

NoodleBib for MLA, APA, and Chicago/Turabian

Generate, edit, and publish an MLA Works Cited list, an APA References list, or a Chicago/Turabian Bibliography that complies with the rules of the current handbooks and manuals for each style. NoodleBib takes care of punctuation, alphabetization and formatting, producing a polished source list to download as a Word document. 

NoodleBib Knowledge Base

Answers to questions about how to cite sources and use NoodleBib. 

NoodleBib Tutorial

This tutorial will walk you through the steps of creating a bibliography in NoodleBib.

 

FAQs

How do I get started in NoodleBib?

Go to NoodleBib and click on "Create a Personal ID." You can then set up your own username and password for NoodleBib.

How do I create a bibliography?

There are five tabs at the top of the screen: Projects, Dashboard, Bibliography, Notecards and Paper.

On the Bibliography screen:

You can now begin adding citations. At the top of the screen, use the pulldown menu to select the type of sources you’re citing. NoodleBib will prompt you for information about the source and will format the citation according to MLA, APA or Chicago/Turabian rules.

After you create a citation, you can click on "Parenthetical References" and get a popup window which shows you how to create a parenthetical reference for this item. Be sure that you do not have a popup-blocker like the Google Toolbar preventing the help screen from coming up.

  • Step 1: Choose MLA Advanced, APA Advanced, or Chicago/Turabian Advanced.
  • Step 2: Give the list a name. This description is just for your reference and will not print out on the final product. Ex: Sources for Paper on Children and Television Violence

How do I print my bibliography?

When you’ve added all your citations, use the "Print/Export" menu for options.

I am having trouble figuring out my source type or what to enter into the fields in NoodleBib.

Need help? You can:

  • Search the NoodleBib Knowledge Base.
  • Ask a Librarian via chat, email, telephone or in person.
  • Take a look at our NoodleBib tutorial.

I want to access NoodleBib from off-campus. What do I do?

Access NoodleBib from the UT Libraries page. If you go directly to www.noodletools.com, you won't be recognized as a UT-Austin student and won't be prompted for your EID.

  • Go to NoodleBib
  • Enter your EID and password. This lets us know you are affiliated with UT.
  • You will be directed to the NoodleBib login page. Log in with the NoodleBib username and password you created for yourself.

I forgot my username and password. What do I do?

Email lib-instruction@utlists.utexas.edu and include the last four digits of the telephone number you had when you first created a NoodleBib account. A librarian will look up your username/password and email you back.


For help using NoodleBib, email, chat, call or visit a librarian at www.lib.utexas.edu/ask
