Student basics chart

This information applies to: McGill students

To borrow items:

  • Use one of our auto-loan machines using your Library PIN (for details, see your library account), or
  • Present your McGill University student ID at one of the Loans Desks.

  1. Students from the McGill School of Continuing Studies and McGill Community of Lifelong Learning enjoy the same borrowing privileges as McGill undergraduate students.
  2. PhD students whose McGill ID expires December 31 may have their borrowing privileges extended until the date their thesis is due. Either an email or a signed letter from the student's faculty administrator or thesis advisor must be sent to a Loans Supervisor at the appropriate library. (N.B. the email must be sent from a valid McGill address.)
  3. Students with a valid McGill ID card may renew books an unlimited number of times unless the book is recalled.
  4. Course reserve items on 3 hour loan can be borrowed overnight if they are borrowed within 3 hours before the closing of the Loans Desk. For example, when the Loans Desk closes at 10:00 p.m., any reserve item borrowed after 7:00 p.m. can be borrowed overnight. Course reserve items borrowed overnight will be due back one hour after the Loans Desk opens the next day.
  5. For laptops, if the 48 hours are up at a time when the Library is closed, the laptop will be due one hour after the Loans Desk opens the next day.
