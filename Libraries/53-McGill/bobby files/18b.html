How to cite

Legend

  • McGill users only
  • In-library-use only
  • Catalogue record

Please note: It is a good idea to consult your instructor before using the guides suggested below.

Citation Management Software

  • Create bibliographies and reference your sources automatically and in the correct citation style as you write your paper, using citation management software.
  • Choose between EndNote and Reference Manager.
  • Free to McGill students, faculty, and staff.

Citation guides by subject

Architecture and art

  • A Short Guide to Writing About Art. Barnet, Sylvan.
  • The Craft of Research, Booth, Wayne, et al.
  • A Manual for Writers of Term Papers, Theses, and Dissertations. Turabian, Kate L.

Citing images

  • How to Cite an Image. University of Cincinatti.
  • How to Cite Electronic Sources. Library of Congress.
  • Documenting Sources. Diana Hacker Online.
  • How do I cite an image, photograph or work of art? Noodletools.
    A brief guide to citing works of art.
  • Image Caption Styles. University of Washington Citation Guide.
    Guide to formatting captions of images within a publication using both Art Bulletin and MLA styles.
  • The Art Bulletin Submission and Style Guidelines. College Art Association (CAA).
  • ARTstor: ARTstorHelp
    Click on “How Do I Cite an ARTstor Image”.

Business research

  • Citing Business Sources in APA Style. McGill University Library.
  • see: APA Style

Chemistry

  • ACS style Guide: a manual for authors and editors. American Chemical Society.
  • A Quick Guide to Reference Citations Using the ACS Style Guide
    compiled by N.J. Butkovich at Penn State University

Economics

  • see: Chicago Manual of Style

Engineering

  • IEEE Editorial Style Manual

Geographic information

  • Guide to citing maps and atlases. McMaster University.

Government information

  • Canadian Government Publications: A Citation Guide
  • The Oxford Standard for the Citation of Legal Authorities

History

  • Guide to Writing History Essays. McGill University Department of History.
    The link is in the right-hand column of this page.
  • Citing Electronic Information in History Papers. Maurice Crouse, Department of History, University of Memphis.
    Based on Turabian, with his own adaptations.

Humanities

  • see: MLA Citation Style
  • see: Chicago Manual of Style
  • see: Turabian

Langue et littérature françaises

  • Protocole pour la présentation matérielle des travaux
    Université McGill Département de langue et littérature françaises

Law

  • Canadian guide to uniform legal citation = Manuel canadien de la référence juridique. McGill Law Journal.
  • Bluebook : A uniform system of citation. Harvard Law Review Association.
  • RedBook legal citation style (6th edition) for use with EndNote: RedBook Basic (English) , RedBook Basic (French)

Life sciences, nursing, and allied health

  • Life sciences citation style guide. McGill University Library.
  • for biology see: Council of Science Editors (CSE), Council of Biology Editors (CBE)
    Preferred citation style in the field of biology.

Music

  • Chicago Manual of Style Online.
    The preferred style of musicology
  • Citing Music Sources in Your Essay and Bibliography. UWO.
    Turabian/Chicago style.
  • GDRM: Présentation des notes et des bibliographies. Dr. Marc-André Roberge of Université Laval.
    (Chicago style en français)
  • Harvard Style Bibliographies and References. Leeds University.
    Guidance in citing blogs, emails, wikis, etc.
  • Publication Manual of the American Psychological Association.
    The preferred style of music education.

Physics

  • AIP Style Manual. American Institute of Physics.
  • Reference Guide: AIP Style. University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign College of Engineering.
  • Physical Review Style and Notation Guide. American Physical Society.

Science

  • see: American Chemical Society (ACS)
  • see: Council of Science Editors (CSE), Council of Biology Editors (CBE)

Social sciences

  • see: Turabian
  • see: Chicago Manual of Style
  • see: APA Style
  • Hints on Writing Papers in Anthropology, Department of Anthropology, McGill University

Social Work

  • see: APA Style

Sociology

  • American Sociological Association (ASA) Format. California State University, Los Angeles.
