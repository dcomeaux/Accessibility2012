Renewing items

This information applies to: McGill students

You may extend the loan period of the item you have borrowed through your library account. For further assistance, please contact your branch library.

Note:

  • You cannot renew an item that has been requested by someone else. See Recall notices.
  • You cannot renew items on Course Reserves.
  • Laptops cannot be renewed. To ensure equitable access for all, you will be required to wait one hour before borrowing the same laptop if no other laptop of the same model is available at the Loans Desk you are borrowing from.
