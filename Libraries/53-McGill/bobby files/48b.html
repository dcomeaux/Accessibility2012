Printing & copying

uPrint: McGill's cross-campus copying and printing service

Copying and printing at all branches of the McGill Library is now managed by uPrint, a campus-wide copy and print management service.

uPrint Refund Request Form
  • Printer jammed?
  • Printed out blank sheets of paper?
  • Charged for jobs you didn't get?

If so, you were likely still charged for what you sent to the printer. You can use the uPrint Refund Request form to ask for a refund.

For detailed information on uPrint, please see the relevant article in the IT knowledgebase:

  • uPrint for Students
  • uPrint for Guests
  • uPrint machine locations on campus

Technical support: For technical support with the uPrint service, please contact the ICS Help Desk.
