Special collections

Curriculum Resources Centre

The Curriculum Resources Centre includes elementary and secondary school textbooks and accompanying teachers’ manuals, resource materials, videos and DVDs, CDs, audio cassettes, learning kits, games, and puppets.

Videos and DVDs on teaching and counselling are included.

Equipment for viewing and listening is also available.

Children's Literature Collection

The Children’s Literature Collection focuses on children’s and young adult books of fiction, non-fiction, poetry, folklore, and picture books, and features award-winning titles and Canadian materials.

ERIC Documents

The ERIC Document Reproduction Service reproduced most ED documents on microfiche and sold collections to many libraries. The collections are often referred to as the ERIC Fiche. The ERIC microfiche collection was published from 1966 to July 2004; it begins with ED000001 and ceased with ED483046. No more ERIC fiche is being produced. The ERIC microfiche are filed by their six-digit ERIC number (for example, ED331465) in several cabinets located in the main room of the Education Library. Patrons can print out paper copies from the mircrofiche using the library's microfiche printer.

While most publications indexed in ERIC are available on microfiche, there are a few for which copyright permission could not be obtained. Journal articles indexed in ERIC (documents with EJ numbers) are not also not included in the ERIC Fiche. Many items indexed in ERIC are available online. For more information please consult our online resources page.
