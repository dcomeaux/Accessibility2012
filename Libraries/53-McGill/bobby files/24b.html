Selected current news sources (online)


[A newspaper in Press Display]

  • PressDisplay
    • Browse online newspapers as if you were browsing their print counterparts.
    • National and international newspapers.
    • Browse by country.
  • Canadian Newsstand
    • Canadian newspapers.
    • Search by date, newspaper, author, keyword.
    • Publications available through Canadian Newsstand
  • Factiva
    • International and Canadian publications.
    • Search by date, newspaper, author, keyword, subject.
  • Lexis-Nexis
    • Local, international news,legal sources.
    • Publications available through Lexis-Nexis.
  • Newscan.com
    Formerly Virtual News Library / Biblio-Branchée. Includes Canadian English and French language as well as European French language news.
  • Selected titles include:
    • The Canadian NewsWire
    • The Canadian Press
    • La Presse (PDF search available)
    • Le Devoir (PDF search available)
    • Le Soleil (PDF search available)
    • Le Droit (PDF search available)
    • Le Monde (France)
  • ISI Emerging Market Information Service (EMIS)
    "delivers news, company and financial data direct from more than 70 emerging markets in Asia, Latin America, Central and Eastern Europe, the Middle East and Africa."
    EMIS Quick User Guide
  • World News Connection
    full-text summaries of non-US newspaper articles, both translated and English language sources. View partial list of sources covered. Note: limited to 1 McGill user at a time.
    Continues FBIS: Foreign Broadcast Information Service Electronic Index

[historical newspapers]

Selected historical news sources (online)

  • 17th-18th century Burney Collection newspapers
    British Library’s collection of the newspapers, pamphlets, and books gathered by Reverend Charles Burney (1757-1817).
  • Atlanta Constitution (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1868-1945
  • Boston Globe (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1872-1927
  • Chicago Defender (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1910-1975
  • Chicago Tribune (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1849-1986
  • Christian Science Monitor (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1908-1996
  • Early American Newspapers, Series I, 1690-1876
    Historic newspapers listed in Clarence Brigham's History and bibliography of American newspapers McLennan Reference Z6951 B86.
  • Financial Times Historical Archive
    1888-2006
  • Gale NewsVault
    Extensive collection of historical newspapers. Over 400 years of content to be explored. Search the full text of over 10 million page images.
  • Globe and Mail: Canada's Heritage from 1844
    from 1844
  • Google News Archive Search
    Provides searchable archives of many Papers of Record. Users can search these digitized newspapers, however in 2011 Google stopped adding to the database.
  • Hartford Courant (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1764-1984
  • Historical Jewish Press
  • Los Angeles Times (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1881-1986
  • New York Times (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1851-2007
  • Paper of Record
    Searchable archive of historical newspapers from Europe, Australia, and the Americas. Coverage varies, from the mid-1800s to 2000s.
  • The Times Digital Archive (U.K.)
    1785-1985
  • Toronto Star: Pages of the Past
  • Wall Street Journal (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1889-1993
  • Washington Post (Proquest Historical Newspapers)
    1877-1994
