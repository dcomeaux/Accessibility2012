Citation management software

[Download icon]

EndNote Workshops

Click here for a listing of upcoming Endnote workshops

Citation management software allows you to manage your references and create bibliographies and in-text citations.

McGill Library provides the citation management programs EndNote, Reference Manager, and ProCite. With our university-wide site license for these software packages, McGill students, faculty, and staff can download them free of charge. The library recommends the EndNote program for new users, since the software provider is no longer updating Reference Manager and ProCite.

EndNote and Reference Manager are available on all library computers as well as other locations on campus.

EndNote Web, which is accessed and used online, is also available to the McGill community.

EndNote

[Endnote logo]

You can use EndNote to search online information resources, such as the McGill Library Catalogue or article indexes and databases, and import references into your own personal database. This database, which is fully searchable, can then be used to generate reference lists and bibliographies for publications, assignments and reports.

EndNote training sessions are available from the McGill Library throughout the year.

EndNote Web

[EndNote Web]

EndNote Web can be used alone or in tandem with the desktop version. Users can import references from online databases and catalogues, organize references in folders and store them online, and transfer references directly between the web and desktop versions of EndNote. In addition, the web-based version allows you to share folders, enabling collaboration with colleagues.

To sign up for an account on EndNote Web:

  1. Go to www.myendnoteweb.com.

    NOTE: to create the account, you must sign in from a computer on the McGill campus, sign-in to your McGill account, or activate your off-campus connection (VPN).

  2. Click on Sign Up
  3. Enter the required information into the mandatory fields, marked with an asterisk (*). Take note of the password guidelines!

All subsequent logins require your email address and this new password.

Your EndNote Web account grants roaming access to your references from any computer with an internet connection for one year after your last log-in from a computer on the McGill network.

Download

McGill students and staff may download EndNote free of charge onto their personal computers at home or at the university.

  1. Choose the Windows or the Mac version of the EndNote software.
    • EndNote X5 for Windows
    • EndNote X5 for Mac OS X

      Please note: The Safari browser does not work well with EndNote. We suggest that Mac users download and install the Mozilla Firefox browser.
  2. Login with your McGill Username and McGill Password.
  3. Click on ADD TO CART.
  4. Click on CHECKOUT.
  5. After you've "checked out," click on Start Download. The installation files will be downloaded onto your computer (the file name is SDM_EN.msi).
  6. Click on the SDM_EN.msi file to start the installation.
  7. Windows users: After installing the program, you can download the latest update to version X5. To do this, open the EndNote program. Go to the Help menu and choose EndNote Program Updates. You can also download the update directly from the EndNote website.
