What am I Searching in the Articles Tab?

The Articles tab on the UT Libraries home page searches selected databases in the
General Topics category (databases listed below) to find articles with one click.

  • Academic Search Premier
  • CQ Researcher
  • JSTOR

The drop-down menu in the "Discover Library Resources" Articles tab provides for searching selected databases in a number of other subject areas:

MetaSearch functionality, i.e. the ability to choose your own custom set of databases to search, is also available in the Articles Tab.

  • Click on the "MetaSearch" link at the top of the page, select the databases you want to search in the Find Databases window, click the "Selection Completed" link, and search in your Selected Databases using the search box.
