The Studio, located in 245 Hodges Library, is a media production lab. Open to UT students, staff and faculty, The Studio provides computers, software and staff to assist users in the creation of multimedia-enhanced educational projects.

What exactly is The Studio?
Read our FAQ and Blog to get the answers to your questions, then check out how much cool gear we have to offer to students.

Equipment Certifications
You can complete certifications for cameras and video cameras or digital audio recorders online. Contact us to make an appointment to be certified for other equipment including DSLR cameras, lighting kits, and green screens.

View Reservations Online
You can view reservations for workstations and equipment online. You can check your existing reservations or look for availability before requesting a reservation.
