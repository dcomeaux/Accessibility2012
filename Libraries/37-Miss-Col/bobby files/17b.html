POPULAR COMBOS
Basic English 1000 Combo Approx. 50 minutes read more Introduction, Research Strategy, MERLIN Library Catalog Basics, Summon@MU, Searching Databases Basics, Popular vs. Scholarly Sources.
Extended English 1000 Combo Approx. 75 minutes read more Introduction, Research Strategy, MERLIN Library Catalog Basics, Summon@MU; Searching Databases Basics and 1st additional database, Popular vs. Scholarly Sources, Evaluating Websites, Google Scholar and/or Google Books, Citations.
None
REQUIRED SESSIONS
Introduction & Conclusion Approx. 5 mins.  read more Introductions and explanation of goals/purpose of the session. Conclusion: Questions? Getting help; Sourcepack; Evaluation.
Research Strategy Approx. 10 mins.  read more Sources of Information; Choosing a topic & refining a topic; Translating a topic into good database searching: Keywords, Boolean operators, truncation, field searching.
MERLIN LIBRARY CATALOG (Must choose one)
MERLIN Library Catalog Basics Approx. 10 mins.  read more An introduction to MERLIN Library Catalog; demonstrate finding a known item (author, title search); looking for a topic by keyword; deciphering a catalog record to check if the item is available and getting a book from MOBIUS if unavailable at MU
MERLIN Catalog Advanced Approx. 15 minutes   read more MERLIN Library Catalog Basics plus Subject Search, Advanced Keyword search, limits and My Library Account.
DATABASES
Searching Databases Basics Approx. 15 minutes   read more Includes what a database is and how best to find it. Brief introduction to Academic Search Premier including simple keyword searches, post search limits, finding full text, and evaluation of results
1st additional database Approx. 5 minutes Which database?
2nd additional database Approx. 5 minutes Which database?
GOOGLE SCHOLAR & GOOGLE BOOKS
Searching Google Scholar Approx. 5 minutes   read more How to use Google's cross-disciplinary article search and link results to the MU Libraries' full text articles
Searching Google Books Approx. 5 minutes   read more How to search the text of millions of books scanned by Google and locate copies of those books available in local libraries
SUMMON@MU
Summon@MU for Articles and Books Approx. 5 minutes   read more How to use the search box embedded in the MU Libraries homepage to search for articles and books owned by the MU Libraries. Searches for articles across databases, and narrow your search to peer-reviewed, by date, and other key limiters. Use SummonMU as a quick book finder and another entry point into the MERLIN catalog as well as our e-book collections.
CRITICAL EVALUATION OF SOURCES
Popular vs. Scholarly Sources Approx. 5 minutes   read more A brief discussion of what it means for an article to be scholarly or popular. Some examples will be given, as well as tips on how to recognize the difference between the two.
Evaluating Websites Approx. 5 minutes   read more A discussion about information on the web and reliability of Wikipedia. Includes information about the publishing and peer-review process for articles and books and compares that to the publishing on the web. Includes tips for identifying a web page's author, their credibility, and the timeliness of a webpage.
Using the Web for Research Approx. 25 minutes   read more Students will evaluate information available on the web in terms of currency, relevance, authority , accuracy and purpose. Librarians can use preset in-class exercises or work with instructor to develop exercises that relate to course research assignments(s).
CITATIONS
Citation Styles Approx. 5 minutes   read more Show the "Writing & Citing" web page and focus on APA, MLA, Turabian, Chicago section. Show some examples on Duke University webpage for citing sources for a bibliography and citing sources within a paper. Another cool tool is the Citation Builder from North Carolina State University.
Avoiding Plagiarism Approx. 25 minutes   read more Students will do in-class exercises to help them recognize and avoid several forms of plagiarism, develop alternative strategies for getting work done quickly, and become aware of institutional consequences of plagiarism.
Zotero Approx. 25 minutes   read more Download and learn to use this free, simple citation management software to capture, organize your citations and format in-text citations and bibliographies.
