Visiting Scholars  

Library Privileges for all Visiting Scholars

  • Standard check-out privileges of books owned by the MU Libraries. Excluded are interlibrary loans.
  • Access to all print journals while in the library, but no check-out privileges for these materials.
  • Access to all online journals and ebooks while in the library, but no off-campus access.
  • Access to most, but not all, databases while in the library but no off-campus access.
  • Computer use in Ellis Library is limited to 2 hours per day and requires a guest login user name and password which can be obtained at the Reference Desk located in the Information Commons, first floor, South, Ellis Library.
  • Wireless Access: contact the sponsoring department liaison for information.

How to Obtain MU Libraries Borrowing Privileges

International Student and Scholar Services has written a form letter approved by the MU Libraries.
Please use this form letter when creating the department letter for the scholar.

  • All visiting scholars must have a valid MU ID card to check out materials from the MU Libraries.
  • Obtain the letter referenced above from one of the following:
    • International Office or Asian Affairs Center (international guests)
    • Your Department Chair’s Office (non-international guests)
  • The Visiting scholar will go to the ID office located in the University Bookstore and obtain an MU ID card.
  • The visiting scholar will then take the letter and the MU ID card to the Ellis Library Circulation Desk to obtain library borrowing privileges.

Visiting Scholars—International

Most international visiting scholars start the process by one of the following:

  • Go to the International Office website
  • Visit the International office in N52 Memorial Union, University of Missouri, Columbia, MO.

Those coming through the Missouri International Training Institute (MITI) will receive permission through:

Asian Affairs Center
N49 Memorial Union
University of Missouri

Visiting Scholars—Non-International

The library privileges outlined for International Visiting Scholars also apply to all other visiting scholars. It is suggested that departments use the same form letter and follow the same process for all visiting scholars.

Everyone will need to have a valid MU ID card in order to borrow materials from the MU Libraries.

Questions:  June DeWeese, Head of Access Services, MU Ellis Library deweesej@missouri.edu (573-882-7315)
