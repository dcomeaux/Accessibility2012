Giving to the MU Libraries

The University of Missouri Libraries occupy a central point both on the campus and in the minds of students and faculty. Join the Libraries to help our talented and dedicated staff bring students and scholars together with the resources they need in a new kind of learning environment — the heart of this great flagship university. There are many ways to support the MU Libraries.

Giving Opportunities

  •  
    • Send checks to MU Libraries Development, 104 Ellis Library, Columbia, MO 65201-5149.
    • Online gifts can be made by using the MU Online Giving Form.
    • Adopt-a-Book
      • Contribute to the preservation of a rare or fragile book.
    • Honor with Books
      • Celebrate loved ones by  purchasing a book in their honor for the MU Libraries collection.

    Memberships

    • Friends of the MU Libraries
      • Join the Friends in supporting the Libraries.
        • Library Society
          • Contribute $1,000 and become a member of the Library Society.
        • Endowments
          • Establish a named library endowment by contributing $25,000 or more

    Methods for Giving

    For information about additional giving options, contact the MU Libraries Development Office. To donate books, journals or other items, please contact Amy Lana  at lanaa@missouri.edu or 573-882-4757.
