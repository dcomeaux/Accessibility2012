Browsing Collection

// The Browsing Collection is located across from the Information Desk on Level 1 of the Library. This collection is designed for recreational reading and includes many popular books – mysteries, romance novels, sci-fi, etc. Faculty, staff, students, and the community are invited to donate used books to this collection. Books are added daily and can be dropped off at the Circulation Desk.

This collection can be accessed whenever the library is open.
