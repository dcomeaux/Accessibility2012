Request Library Services

// Request Library Services:
Current TWU students, staff, and faculty are automatically registered for library services. TWU students are registered automatically when all university registration processes are complete and staff and faculty are registered upon hire.

TWU students may submit address updates to the Registrar's Office: http://www.twu.edu/registrar/forms.asp

TWU staff and faculty may update address information with the Office of Human Resources: http://www.twu.edu/humanresources/payroll/Payroll_Forms.htm

TWU Libraries will receive automatic address updates from both the Registrar's Office and the Office of Human Resources.

Apply for a TexShare Card
TWU students, staff, and faculty wishing to use other Texas participating Libraries may apply for a TexShare Card by completing the following information:

Complete Form

*Required Field

*Full Name
*TWU ID (shown on your Portal Home Page)
*E-mail address
*Phone
*Address
*City
*State
*Zip Code
*Preferred form of Delivery  

If you are unable to submit this information online, please email the TWU Libraries Circulation Department in Denton at circ@twu.edu or call (940) 898-3719. The TexShare card will be held in your name for pickup at the Denton Circulation Desk.

Register for a Courtesy Card
Denton and Dallas County Residents, UNT Faculty and Students, TexShare cardholders from other institutions, and TWU Alumni members may come to any of the circulation desks to receive a Courtesy card.
