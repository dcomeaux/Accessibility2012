    How do I... Details

    Find library materials >>

    Search the library catalog for books, print and electronic journals, microfilms, and other materials owned by the Libraries.

    Search Database Finder for individual research databases, article indexes and full text materials available on the Web and CD-ROM.

    Borrow library materials >>

    		 Circulation Loan Policy

    Renew library books >>

    Connect to the Minerva online catalog and log into "My Minerva Account."

    For step-by-step instructions, see How to Renew Your Own Books Using the Minerva Online Catalog

    Recall library books >>

    		 Connect to the Minerva online catalog and search for a book.

    Click the Holdings link to retrieve the holdings record. In the Options column, click on "Recall this item."

    Request delivery of books and articles from any campus library >>

    		 UA Delivery Services

    Look up materials available in other libraries >>

    Check for the locations of books in other libraries. When you've found what you want, request it by using ILLiad below.

    Connect to the catalog of The Center for Research Libraries

    Connect to WorldCat, a catalog of library holdings around the world

    Order books, journal articles and other materials from other libraries >>

    Connect to ILLiad, our interlibrary loan system. Select "First Time User" to set up an account.

    Check your library account >>

    		 Connect to Minerva, the online catalog, to find out what books you've taken out, or any overdue books, fees or holds on your account. Select "My Minerva Account" at the top of the screen to access your account information.

    Put money on your SUNYCard to make printouts and copies in the Libraries >>

    		 Your SUNYCard is part of your Podium Account. To add money to your account go to: http://www.albany.edu/services/sunycard/.

    		 NetID's in the University Libraries
