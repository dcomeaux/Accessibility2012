    The University Library is the largest of the university’s libraries. Located in this building are:

    • Collections for social sciences, humanities and business
    • Reference services, see Ask-a-Librarian
    • Information literacy classes
    • Information Commons
    • Large areas of study space — group study, individual study, study carrels
    • Interactive Media Center
