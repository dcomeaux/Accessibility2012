Photocopying & Printing

Network Printing and Copying services are being managed by the Office of Converging Technologies. Please see OCT's FAQ about using printing services on campus, and contact the OCT help desk (x74444) with any issues.

For further questions and assistance related to these services contact the OCT help desk at (718) 997-4444.

During the Library's renovations, the printers and copiers have moved from Level 2 to Level 1.

Assistance

During the semester, a worker from OCT is available Monday through Friday between 9AM and 6PM to help students who have questions about printing or copying. Check near the copiers.

Level 1

Printing and Copying

You can add money to your ID card or buy a guest print card from the machine located across from the stairwell on Level 1. Please note that guest cards will only work for the printer near the kiosk computers on Level 3.

  • 4 black and white photocopiers
  • 1 laser print station printing jobs from computers throughout the library
  • 1 scanner

Multimedia Commons

  • 2 microform readers (printing available)
  • 2 scanners

Level 2

Due to renovations, printing and copying services have moved to Level 1.

Music Library

1 black and white photocopier (no large 11" x 17" paper available)

Other Photocopiers and Printers

Other photocopiers and printers are available at “The Corner Pocket,” which is at the back of the Dining Hall close to the Music Building (Lefrak).

Printing is also available at all computer labs on campus.

Flushing, NY 11367-1597
