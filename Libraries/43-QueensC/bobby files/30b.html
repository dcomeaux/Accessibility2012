Ask a Librarian

Frequently Asked Questions

[Question] How can I use Library resources from home?
[Answer] Make sure your ID card has been activated and sign in with your barcode. If you have any problems with the proxy, please contact our Circulation Desk at (718) 997-3702 or via email.
[Question] Do you have the book I need?
[Answer] Search the CUNY+ Catalog.
[Question] Do you have the article I need?
[Answer] Search Electronic Journals, or use the Title begins with search in the CUNY+ Catalog. In either case, search using the journal title, NOT the article title.
[Question] Where can I find items on reserve?
[Answer] Search EReserve by title, course, etc.
[Question] Who may use the Library building and when?
[Answer] Consult Visitors and Hours.
[Question] How do I get an item from a CUNY library or from outside of CUNY?
[Answer] Borrow CUNY books through CUNY CLICS or borrow other material from non-CUNY libraries through Interlibrary Loan.
[Question] When will an item arrive that I requested from another CUNY library?
[Answer] CLICS turnaround time can vary.
[Question] How do I borrow or renew an item, or pay a fee?
[Answer] Consult QC Books & Media. You may contact Circulation at (718) 997-3702 or QC_ask_circ@qc.cuny.edu.

Information & Research Help

[Email] Ask a question, or leave a comment by email, just as you would at the information or reference desk.

[Phone] For help with brief questions, call us when the Library is open at (718) 997-3700.

[In Person] Visit us in our Consultation Room, near the stairs on Level 3 (Room 339). Ideal for all inquiries and available during Library open hours starting at 11 AM weekdays and at 12:00 noon on weekends.

[Subject Specialists] For more subject-specific questions you can ask a Subject Specialist, but this may take more time.
