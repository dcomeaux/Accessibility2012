Library

at Irving K. Barber Learning Centre

http://aarp.library.ubc.ca/   |   http://scieng.library.ubc.ca/

The Library at Irving K. Barber Learning Centre includes:

Circulation and Automated Storage Retrieval System (ASRS) on the 2nd Floor
At Circulation Services, patrons may check out books, pay fines, pick up requested materials and make other inquiries. The ASRS contains materials selected for storage in a secure, climate-controlled setting, and may be retrieved at the Circulation desk upon request.
Art + Architecture + Planning (AArP) on the 3rd Floor
AArP contains materials that support the research needs of faculty and students in the Department of Art History, Visual Art and Theory, the School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture, and the School of Community and Regional Planning.
Science & Engineering (SciEng) on the 4th Floor
SciEng contains materials on the physical and applied sciences such as chemistry, civil engineering, computer science, earth and ocean science, mathematics, mining engineering, physics, statistics and wood science.
